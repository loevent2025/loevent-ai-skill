# LARGE EXHIBITIONS × GENERAL

## Applicable Scenarios
General Web3 expos, mixed-theme conferences, industry networking events, ecosystem showcases, brand activation events, multi-track summits, regional Web3 gatherings, industry anniversary celebrations, cross-sector meetups

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale:** 500–10,000+ attendees
- **Duration:** 1–5 days
- **Format:** Comprehensive exhibition areas, multi-theme forums, social events, brand showcases, side events
- **Core Value:** Industry visibility, cross-sector exchange, brand building, ecosystem connection

## Audience Profiles
- **Primary Audience:** Industry Practitioners — professionals across all tracks, keeping up with industry developments
- **Secondary Audience:** Projects & Brands — seeking visibility and partnership opportunities
- **Tertiary Audience:** Cross-industry Explorers — external parties interested in Web3
- **Extended Audience:** Media & Creators — covering industry developments, producing content
- **Audience Behavior:** Understanding the industry landscape, expanding networks, finding partnerships, gaining inspiration, building brand presence

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Industry Networking:** Practitioners + projects — ideal for industry exchange events
- **Brand Activation:** Projects + media — ideal for brand promotion events
- **Open Exploration:** Full audience coverage — ideal for large-scale comprehensive expos

## Content Elements
- Overall expo positioning and scale overview
- Theme tracks and exhibition zone introductions
- Core forum topics
- Featured projects and brands
- Social event schedule (networking sessions, parties, dinners)
- Side event information
- Speaker lineup
- Community channel access

## Language Guidelines
- Calibrate technical depth to the specific theme
- Balance information density with readability
- Highlight the event's unique value proposition
- Convey both scale and atmosphere
- Avoid excessive jargon stacking
- Maintain industry credibility without being exclusionary

## Structure Strategy
- Opening info block: 15% — event name, positioning, scale, time and venue
- Context and value block: 20% — why this matters, industry backdrop
- Content highlights block: 30% — zones, forums, speakers, activities
- Participation value block: 25% — who it's for, what you'll gain
- Call-to-action block: 10% — registration, community channels
- Emoji density: 10–14 total

## Emotional Tone
Flexible based on event tone — industry summits lean authoritative and professional; social events lean relaxed and energetic; brand events lean engaging and fun. The core is always conveying "this is worth your time."

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Industry Overview
- **Best for:** Comprehensive industry conferences, ecosystem summits
- **Opening format:** 【Industry Event】{Event Name} | {Industry Positioning}
- **Tone:** Comprehensive, authoritative, connective

### Variant B: Theme Focus
- **Best for:** Single-theme large events (DeFi, NFT, AI+Web3, etc.)
- **Opening format:** 🔥 {Theme} @ {Event Name} | {Value Proposition}
- **Tone:** Focused, in-depth, expert-level

### Variant C: Social Vibe
- **Best for:** Networking-forward events, parties, dinners
- **Opening format:** ✨ {Event Name} | Meet a group of people doing interesting things
- **Tone:** Relaxed, fun, anticipation-building

## Notes

### General Copy Principles
1. **Calibrate professional depth to the theme:** Technical events go deeper; social events stay lighter
2. **Describe people by vibe, not title:** Avoid stacking credentials — lead with "interesting people"
3. **Perks must be real:** Never fabricate rewards or benefits
4. **Balance information density with readability:** Large expos have lots of content — help readers quickly find what's relevant to them

### Theme Adaptation Guide
- **DeFi theme:** More technical — reference technical track templates
- **Investment theme:** More institutional — reference investment templates and be mindful of compliance language
- **NFT / community theme:** More vibe-led — reference community track templates
- **Mixed theme:** Balance across all tracks, emphasize "full landscape" and "connection" value
