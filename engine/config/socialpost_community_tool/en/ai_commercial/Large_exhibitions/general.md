# AI Large-Scale Expo / Conference — General Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply this module when the event is a comprehensive AI expo covering multiple thematic areas:
- Global AI conferences, AI industry expos
- AI world summits, AI tech weeks
- Comprehensive AI exhibitions (spanning technology / products / investment / talent)

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Main stage + Multi-track breakout sessions + Product exhibition hall + Workshops + Social events |
| Content Scope | Technology frontiers, Commercial deployment, Investment & funding, Talent development — full spectrum |
| Participants | AI companies, Investment firms, Enterprise buyers, Developers, Media |
| Core Objective | Industry-wide showcase, Ecosystem connections, Brand exposure, Business development |

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Exaggerating AI industry scale or growth figures
- ❌ Using hype language like "AI can do anything" or "disrupting everything"
- ❌ Promising specific lead conversion outcomes
- ❌ Disparaging competitor companies or instigating industry rivalries
- ❌ Fabricating exhibitor or speaker information
