# HACKATHONS × INVESTMENT

## Use Cases
Startup hackathons, accelerator hackathons, VC-sponsored hackathons, fundraising-track events, pitch competitions with building component, incubator selection hackathons

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale:** 50–200 people
- **Duration:** 24–72 hours
- **Format:** Intensive development + business pitching + investor judging
- **Core Value:** Project incubation, investor access, commercial validation, investor visibility

## Audience Targeting
- **Primary Audience:** Technical Founders — teams with project ideas seeking funding opportunities
- **Secondary Audience:** Developer Entrepreneurs — strong technical background, looking to validate commercial viability
- **Tertiary Audience:** Investors — discovering early-stage projects, deal sourcing
- **Extended Audience:** Full-stack Teams — complete teams with technical, product, and business functions
- **Audience Behavior:** Validate commercial ideas, get investor attention, win incubation spots, find co-founders

### Common Audience Configurations
- **Founder Focus:** Founders + investors — best for funding-oriented hackathons
- **Team Building:** Developers + product + business — best for team incubation formats

## Content Elements
- Hackathon positioning (incubation / funding-oriented)
- Judging criteria (with commercial weighting explained)
- Investor and VC judge lineup
- Demo Day / Pitch segment details
- Post-event incubation support
- Compliance disclaimer

## Language Tone
- Emphasize commercial potential and investment value
- Highlight incubation resources and access opportunities
- Investor participation is the core selling point
- Clearly explain the commercial weighting in judging criteria
- Must include NFA disclaimer

## Content Structure
- Opening info block: 15%
- Opportunity overview block: 30% — investor lineup + incubation resources
- Track overview block: 20% — tracks + judging criteria
- Judging process block: 20% — Pitch segment + access mechanism
- Compliance and action block: 15%
- Emoji density: 10–12 total

## Emotional Tone
Opportunity-driven, startup-focused, investor-connected. "Build your startup in 48 hours" energy.

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Fundraising Track
- **Best for:** VC-led events where fundraising is the primary goal
- **Opener format:** 🚀 {Hackathon Name} | Build + Pitch + Meet investors
- **Tone:** Opportunity-driven, investor-backed

### Variant B: Incubator Selection
- **Best for:** Incubator-led events selecting projects for their program
- **Opener format:** ⭐ {Incubator Name} Hackathon | The next cohort starts here
- **Tone:** Resource-oriented, long-term support

## Notes

### Key Principles for Investment Hackathons
1. **Compliance disclaimer is non-negotiable:** Investor introductions ≠ funding commitments
2. **Investor lineup is the selling point:** Real, verifiable, decision-making level
3. **Commercial weighting in judging:** Explicitly state the business dimension's weight
4. **Post-event pathway must be clear:** How does access continue after the hackathon?
