# DIMENSIONAL — Multi-Dimensional Space Style Guide

## Applicable Use Cases

On-chain art projects, generative graphics, futuristic protocol branding, blockchain visualization, metaverse architecture, crypto infrastructure events, smart contract visual identity, spatial design

---

## Color Palette

- **Primary tones:** Deep space blue, electric cyan, neon violet, metallic silver
- **Backgrounds:** Deep cosmic black, dark navy gradients, void-like depth
- **Saturation:** Medium-to-high saturation with strategic neon accents
- **Color expression:** Gradient transitions suggest dimensional depth; edge lighting effects

---

## Visual Elements

- Isometric geometric structures
- Wireframe cubic grids extending into the deep field
- Floating geometric platforms and planes
- 3D extruded shapes with perspective depth
- Overlapping transparent layers creating depth illusions
- Grid lines converging to a vanishing point
- Tessellated surfaces with dimensional folding
- Prismatic geometric tunnels
- Layered rectangular frames suggesting portal effects
- Abstract architectural impossibility (Escher-style)

---

## Treatment & Effects

- Linear perspective with multiple vanishing points
- Atmospheric depth via color desaturation
- Edge glow / rim lighting on geometric shapes
- Drop shadows and ambient occlusion
- Gradient overlays for spatial depth
- Opacity variation to create layered space
- Sharp geometric edges with neon outline glow
- Fog / haze effects to simulate distance
- Specular highlights on reflective surfaces
- Geometric light rays and volumetric lighting

---

## Composition Strategies

- Central perspective with strong depth of field
- Isometric or axonometric projection
- Layered foreground, midground, and background planes
- Converging grid lines drawing the eye into the depth
- Elements floating at varying Z-axis depths
- Portal-style compositional framing devices
- Symmetrical or radial compositions
- Dynamic diagonal arrangements implying spatial movement
- Strategic use of scale variation to enhance depth perception

---

## Typography Guidelines

- **Color selection:** Choose solid colors with high contrast against dark backgrounds
  - For deep cosmic black / dark navy backgrounds: Pure White `#FFFFFF`, Electric Cyan `#00D9FF`
  - Alternative solid colors: Emerald Green `#00FF88`, Bright Magenta `#FF0080`, Ice Blue `#A0E7E5`
- **Fill method:** Solid color fill only — single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No edge glow / rim lighting
  - No neon glow effects
  - No outer glow / inner glow
  - No shadows / drop shadows
  - No gradient fills
  - No 3D extrusion effects
  - No text effects of any kind
- **Overall principle:** Typography must remain clean, sharp, and highly legible — serving as a clear information anchor within the multi-dimensional space. Visual impact is achieved through typeface selection and high-contrast solid color, creating a deliberate contrast between "information clarity" and "spatial depth."
