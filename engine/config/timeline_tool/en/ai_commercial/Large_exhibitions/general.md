# AI Tech Large Exhibitions/Expos Knowledge Base

## Scenario Overview

Large exhibitions/expos are the most influential display platforms in the AI technology industry, bringing together AI products, solution providers, research institutions, and industry users. Activities cover diverse forms such as product displays, technology demonstrations, industry forums, and business negotiations.

## Time Compression Strategies

### Relaxed Mode (30+ days)
- Exhibitor recruitment can be extended to 8-10 days
- Full demo system testing
- Complete experience zone construction
- Multiple rounds of rehearsal drills

### Compact Mode (15-29 days)
- Exhibitor recruitment compressed to 5-6 days
- Parallel processing of booth setup and system deployment
- Simplified experience zone scale
- Priority for key demo projects

### Urgent Mode (8-14 days)
- Limited number of exhibitors
- Use standardized booth solutions
- Streamlined demo content
- Focus on core feature demonstrations

### Extreme Mode (1-7 days)
- Rapid setup plan
- Small-scale boutique display
- Single demo main thread
- Basic display functions

## Risk Warnings

- ⚠️ AI demonstrations have high computing power and network requirements; stress testing must be performed in advance
- ⚠️ Model response latency can affect the experience; a degradation plan needs to be prepared
- ⚠️ Data security needs special attention to avoid sensitive data leakage
- ⚠️ Large-scale concurrent demonstrations require sufficient computing power reserves
