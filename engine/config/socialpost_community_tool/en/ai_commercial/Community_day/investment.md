# AI Cafe — Investment Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Private cafe venue + Founder cowork + Investor drop-ins + Organic conversation |
| Duration | Half-day to full-day |
| Participants | AI founders, investors (select hours), aspiring founders |
| Core Objective | Casual coworking, organic introductions, informal exchange |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides the standard style:**

✅ Relaxed, intimate, and tasteful
✅ Drop the pitch energy, keep the sense of connection
✅ Emphasize "naturally running into people," not "structured networking"

❌ No terms like "roadshow," "fundraising matchmaking," or "pitch deck"
❌ Avoid formal corporate tone
❌ Do not highlight the investor lineup

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| P0 | AI Founders | A change of scenery to work — and maybe bump into an investor |
| P1 | Investors | Casually learn about projects, informal conversation |
| P2 | Aspiring Founders | Soak in the atmosphere, meet people |

---

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Cafe Scene Setting (required)
- A refined, atmospheric cafe description
- Example: "A quiet independent cafe — a good place to think and get work done."

### 2. Organic Connection (required)
- Investors are "stopping by" or "dropping in"
- Example: "A few investors will swing by for coffee in the afternoon."

### 3. Informal Tone (required)
- No pitch sessions
- Example: "No deck needed — just see where the conversation goes."

### 4. Flexible Participation (required)
- Option to focus on work or socialize
- Example: "Feel like chatting? Chat. Feel like working? Work."

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Turning the cafe day into a pitch session
- ❌ Pressuring founders to prepare a pitch
- ❌ Over-promoting the investor lineup
- ❌ Scheduling formal networking segments
