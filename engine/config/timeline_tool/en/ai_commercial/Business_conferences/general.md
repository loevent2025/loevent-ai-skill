# AI Tech Business Meeting Knowledge Base

## Scenario Overview

Business meetings are a professional event format in the AI industry for discussing technology strategies, negotiating business collaborations, and analyzing industry trends. Participants typically include corporate decision-makers, technical leads, investors, and industry experts, focusing on the commercial value and strategic layout of AI technology.

## Tag Classification Extension

| Tag | AI Tech Business Meeting Specific Content |
|-----|----------------------------------------|
| **venue** | Meeting room booking, presentation equipment, private negotiation rooms, simultaneous interpretation, high-speed network |
| **commercial** | Corporate client invitations, technology partners, investment institutions, industry alliances |
| **content** | Technology strategy sharing, industry reports, case studies, round table discussions, product roadmaps |
| **others** | Business etiquette, non-disclosure agreements, meeting minutes, follow-up |

## Time Compression Strategies

### Relaxed Mode (30+ days)
- Multiple rounds of guest invitations
- Comprehensive industry report preparation
- Thorough technical demo testing
- Detailed business matching arrangements

### Compact Mode (15-29 days)
- Focus on core guest invitations
- Simplified report content
- Selected demo projects
- Key business matching

### Emergency Mode (8-14 days)
- Only invite confirmed guests
- Use existing report data
- Simplified demo sessions
- Single venue mode

### Extreme Mode (1-7 days)
- Small-scale high-end closed-door meeting
- Focus on core topics
- Rapid business matching
- Streamlined processes

## Risk Warning

- ⚠️ Technical demonstrations must ensure stability; prepare multiple backup solutions
- ⚠️ For AI-related sensitive topics, pay attention to wording and compliance
- ⚠️ Strengthen confidentiality management for content involving business secrets
- ⚠️ Consider meeting scheduling for participants across time zones
