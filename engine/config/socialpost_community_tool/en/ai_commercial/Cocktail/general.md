# AI Social Mixer — General Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Open socializing + Free-flowing + Party atmosphere |
| Duration | 3–4 hours |
| Participants | AI practitioners, enthusiasts, and the curious |
| Core Objective | Meet people, unwind, have fun |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides standard styles:**

✅ Relaxed, open, urban vibe
✅ Emphasize "interesting people" over "useful people"
✅ The venue and atmosphere are the selling point

❌ Do not use "industry exchange" or "resource alignment"
❌ Avoid any transactional framing
❌ Do not describe attendees by job title or role

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Scene-First Opening (required)
- Urban vibe + Venue character + Atmosphere setting
- Example: "A rooftop, a sunset, and a crowd of people building AI"

### 2. Sensory Immersion (at least 3 of the following)
- Visual: City skyline, ambient lighting, the crowd
- Taste: Champagne, cocktails, small bites
- Sound: DJ, background music, conversation buzz
- Atmosphere: Laid-back, free-flowing, the feel of a city night

### 3. Label-Free People Descriptions (required)
- ❌ "Developers, investors, product managers"
- ✅ "A bunch of interesting people doing things in AI"

### 4. Venue-Unique Experience (required)
- Highlight what only this specific venue can offer
- Example: "Watch the sunset turn into a city skyline from the rooftop"

### 5. Zero-Pressure Participation (required)
- "Come if you feel like it"
- "Just a relaxed evening out"
