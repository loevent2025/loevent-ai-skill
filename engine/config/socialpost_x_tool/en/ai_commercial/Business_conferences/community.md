# B2B Conference × Community Type (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
AI developer community forums, open-source AI project user conferences, AI Builder community meetups, LLM framework user forums, AI tool community events, AI contributor conferences, ecosystem developer forums

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format**: Community sharing, customer case studies, contributor exchange, product updates, social events
- **Core Value**: Community belonging, experience exchange, contribution recognition, Builder connections

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Active community members—developers who use and contribute to community projects
- **Secondary Audience**: Project users—developers who use community projects/tools
- **Tertiary Audience**: Potential contributors—individuals who want to participate in community contributions
- **Extended Audience**: Ecosystem partners—projects and companies collaborating with the community
- **Audience Behavior**: Participate in the community, share experiences, gain a sense of belonging, build connections

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Core Members**: Primarily active contributors; suited for contributor events
- **Full Community**: All users participating; suited for user conferences
- **Ecosystem Collaboration**: Community + partners; suited for ecosystem forums

## Content Elements
- Community identity rallying
- Project/product updates
- Customer case study sharing
- Contributor content
- Social events
- Community entry points

## Language Approach
- Community-oriented conversational tone
- Sense of belonging through "we" and "us"
- Builder culture atmosphere
- Technically enthusiastic yet relaxed
- Recognition and gratitude for contributions

## Structure Strategy
- Opening rallying block: 15%
- Event highlights block: 35%
- Community value block: 25%
- Participation details block: 15%
- Call-to-action block: 10%
- Emoji density: 12–16 total

## Emotional Tone
Community belonging, Builder enthusiasm. A sense of "being among your people." Technically passionate but not overly serious.

## Style Variants

### Variant A: User Conference
- **Use for**: Community user gatherings, product updates
- **Opening Format**: 🎉 {Community Name} User Conference | A Gathering for Builders
- **Tone**: Celebratory, sharing, connecting

### Variant B: Contributor Forum
- **Use for**: Contributor exchange, open-source collaboration
- **Opening Format**: 🛠️ {Community Name} Contributor Forum | Thank You to Every Builder
- **Tone**: Recognition, gratitude, collaboration

### Variant C: Ecosystem Forum
- **Use for**: Ecosystem project showcases, partnership exchange
- **Opening Format**: ⚡ {Ecosystem Name} Ecosystem Forum | See What Everyone Is Building
- **Tone**: Ecosystem-focused, showcase, collaboration

## Key Considerations

### Community-Type B2B Conference Essentials
1. **Belonging is the core**: "We," "together," "our community"
2. **Users are the stars**: Not an official product launch—it's a user exchange
3. **Recognize contributors**: Make contributors feel seen and valued
4. **Authentic exchange**: Share real experiences, including lessons learned
5. **Community connections**: Emphasize ongoing, sustained participation
