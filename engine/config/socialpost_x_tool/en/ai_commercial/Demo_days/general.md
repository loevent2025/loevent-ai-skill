# Demo Day × General (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
AI product Demo Days, LLM application showcases, AI Agent project launches, AI tool product releases, AI innovation project showcases, AI ecosystem project debuts

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format**: Project demo presentations, judge commentary, Q&A interaction, networking
- **Core Value**: Product exposure, receiving feedback, building connections, attracting attention

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Demo project teams—teams looking to showcase their product for feedback and exposure
- **Secondary Audience**: Industry practitioners—people curious about what's new in the space
- **Tertiary Audience**: Potential users—prospective users interested in AI products
- **Extended Audience**: Investors / media—observers tracking new projects in the AI space
- **Audience Behavior**: Showcasing products, receiving feedback, discovering opportunities, building connections

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Product Showcase**: Project teams + practitioners + users, suited for product launch formats
- **Industry Watch**: Project teams + investors + media, suited for ecosystem showcase formats
- **Technical Exchange**: Project teams + developers, suited for technical demo formats

## Content Elements
- Demo Day theme positioning
- Types / directions of showcased projects
- Demo format description
- Judging / commentary panel lineup
- Q&A session
- Networking arrangements
- Post-event connection mechanisms

## Language Guidelines
- Product-oriented expression
- Emphasize "Show, Don't Tell"
- Live Demo is the centerpiece
- Keep technical terms in English
- Avoid over-marketing

## Structure Strategy
- Opening information block: 15%
- Project showcase block: 35%
- Judging value block: 25%
- Participation details block: 15%
- Call-to-action block: 10%
- Emoji density control: 10-14 total

## Emotional Tone
Product showcase, builder culture. The hands-on spirit of "let the product speak for itself."

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Multi-Project Showcase
- **Use For**: Showcasing multiple projects together
- **Opening Format**: 🎯 {Demo Day Name} | See what everyone's building
- **Tone**: Discovery, openness, showcase

### Variant B: Theme-Focused
- **Use For**: Showcasing projects in a specific direction
- **Opening Format**: 🔧 {Theme} Demo Day | {Core Direction} projects take the stage
- **Tone**: Focused, deep, professional

### Variant C: Product Launch
- **Use For**: New product / major update releases
- **Opening Format**: 🚀 {Product/Project} Launch Day | The official debut
- **Tone**: Launch, milestone, anticipation

## Key Considerations

### Demo Day Best Practices
1. **Live Demo is the centerpiece**: This is not a slide presentation
2. **Curate project quality**: Showcase projects must be vetted
3. **Judging panel must be strong**: Judges who can deliver valuable feedback
4. **Post-event connection mechanisms**: How to facilitate follow-up after the demos
5. **Strict time management**: Keep each demo tightly on schedule
