# San Francisco Venue Knowledge Base

## Receptions/Social Venues

### 1. The Treasury (Financial District)
- **Capacity**: 100-400 people
- **Suitable for**: High-end industry dinners, investor receptions, summit after-parties

### 2. The View Lounge (Marriott Marquis)
- **Capacity**: 80-200 people
- **Suitable for**: Industry receptions, VC networking, Networking Night

### 3. Trademark (SoMa)
- **Capacity**: 50-150 people
- **Suitable for**: Tech industry receptions, builder gatherings, casual networking

### 4. Coin-Op (SoMa)
- **Capacity**: 80-200 people
- **Suitable for**: Community mixers, team socials, after-work parties

### 5. SFMOMA Rooftop Garden
- **Capacity**: 100-300 people
- **Suitable for**: High-end industry receptions, art x tech socials, brand launches

### 6. Local Edition (Financial District)
- **Capacity**: 50-150 people
- **Suitable for**: Investor networking, small VIP receptions, founder gatherings
