# DREAMY MOONLIT NIGHT — Visual Style Guide

## Applicable Scenarios
Mid-Autumn Festival event promotions, traditional holiday parties, nighttime music festivals, moonlight-themed events, East Asian aesthetics exhibitions, romantic-themed parties, nightclub special events, brand holiday marketing, cultural fusion events, starry sky-themed exhibitions, meditation/wellness events, weddings and proposals

## Color Palette
- **Primary tones:** Deep night sky palette — Midnight Blue (#1A2744), Starry Indigo (#2C3E6E), Moonlight White (#F5F5F5), Neon Violet-Pink (#D896FF)
- **Background:** A night sky gradient transitioning from deep midnight blue (#0F1729) at the top, to starry blue (#1E3A5F) at the center, to misty blue (#4A6FA5) at the bottom — smooth and natural
- **Moon:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) at the center fading to moonlit off-white (#FFF8E7) at the edges, with a soft luminous glow surrounding it
- **Clouds:** Volumetric gradient from white (#FFFFFF) to pale grey-blue (#C8D4E3), lit from the direction of the moon
- **Accent colors:** Neon violet-pink (#D896FF) and electric purple (#7B4FBF) for text emphasis
- **Saturation:** Low saturation (20–40%) on the background for a serene atmosphere; high saturation (70–90%) on emphasized elements
- **Color behavior:** Overall cool-toned palette; the warm white moon serves as the visual focal point in contrast; neon-colored text adds modern energy

### Recommended Color Combinations
- Serene Moonlit Night: Midnight Blue (#0F1729) → Starry Blue (#1E3A5F) → Misty Blue (#4A6FA5) + Moonlight White (#FFFFFF)
- Neon Mid-Autumn: Deep Violet-Blue (#1A1A3A) → Indigo (#2E4A7D) + Neon Pink (#D896FF) + Electric Purple (#7B4FBF)
- Dreamy Starfield: Deep Space Blue (#0A1628) → Cobalt Blue (#1E4D8C) → Sky Blue (#6B9AC4) + Starlight White (#FFFAFA)

## Visual Elements

### Core Element: Oversized Glowing Moon
- **Full moon as the visual centerpiece** — large circular moon with a diameter spanning 40–60% of the canvas width
- **Moon glow effect** — bright pure white at the center, softly fading at the edges, with a wide ambient halo (50–100px)
- **Moon placement** — typically centered or in the upper-center of the canvas as the absolute focal point
- **Moon texture** — can be a clean glowing disc or carry subtle lunar surface texture

### Night Sky Background
- **Gradient night sky** — blue-toned gradient from dark to light, creating a sense of spatial depth
- **Star field** — scattered stars of varying sizes with cross-shaped star flares, adding detail to the night sky
- **Star flare effects** — select stars with 4- or 6-pointed flares in white or pale blue
- **Optional: faint nebula/Milky Way texture** — enhances the dreamlike quality

### Cloud Layer Elements
- **Volumetric clouds** — positioned in the lower or lower-center area of the canvas with defined light and shadow layering
- **Moonlit clouds** — the side facing the moon is brighter; the opposite side is darker
- **Layered clouds** — multiple overlapping cloud layers at varying depths to create spatial dimension
- **Soft cloud edges** — natural feathering that blends seamlessly with the night sky

### Decorative Details
- Optional East Asian silhouette accents (e.g., distant mountains, architectural outlines)
- Floating light particles or firefly-effect highlights
- Minimalist geometric lines or framing elements

## Post-Processing & Effects
- Moon rendered with multiple overlapping glow layers: inner hard light + mid-range soft glow + outer diffused ambient halo
- Background gradient smooth and natural; a subtle noise overlay (3–5%) adds texture
- Clouds rendered with soft light-and-shadow gradients; edges feathered (20–50px)
- Stars use Screen blend mode; select stars receive cross-shaped flare treatment
- A subtle blue color grade can be applied globally for visual cohesion
- Avoid overly sharp edges throughout — maintain an overall soft, dreamlike quality
- A light lens flare effect may be added around the moon

## Composition Strategy
- **Center-focal composition** — moon placed at the center or along the golden ratio
- **Clear vertical layering** — Sky (top) → Moon (middle) → Clouds (bottom)
- **Symmetrical or near-symmetrical** — creates visual balance and stability
- **Text zone planning** — primary headline placed above or within the moon; subheadlines placed below
- **Bottom information zone** — event details positioned below the cloud layer or at the very bottom of the canvas
- **Breathing room** — keep the area surrounding the moon free of excessive elements
- **Corner placements** — brand logos, dates, and supplementary details can occupy the four corners

## Typography Guidelines
- **Color usage:**
  - Use white (#FFFFFF) or light-colored text against the dark night sky
  - Use dark text for contrast within the moon area: deep purple (#3D2B56) or deep blue (#1A2744)
  - Use neon violet-pink (#D896FF) or electric purple (#9B6DFF) for emphasis words
- **Fill style:** Flat solid color fills only — no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No gradient fills
  - No drop shadows or inner shadows
  - No outer glow or inner glow
  - No stroke/outline
  - No blur effects
  - No text special effects of any kind
- **Typography style:**
  - Primary headline: Bold sans-serif with a 3D perspective effect, deep purple, slightly tilted
  - Accent words: Neon-style script or cursive, violet-pink, for a romantic touch
  - Informational text: Clean, modern sans-serif, white, Light to Regular weight
- **Overall principle:** Clear typographic hierarchy with distinct primary and secondary levels; text must contrast cleanly against the dreamlike background; when mixing Chinese and English, ensure typeface harmony

## Mood & Atmosphere
Dreamy, romantic, serene, mysterious, and intimate. The overall feeling evokes "romance beneath the moonlight" — the oversized full moon creates a breathtaking yet tender visual anchor, while the night sky and cloud layers add spatial depth and a dreamlike quality. Ideal for brands and events that blend romantic aesthetics with Eastern cultural heritage and contemporary style. This visual language awakens a poetic longing for moonlit nights, a yearning for reunion, and an immersion in magical moments.
