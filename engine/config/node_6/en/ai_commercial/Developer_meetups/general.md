# AI Industry — Developer Meetup Marketing Strategy

## Event Definition

A developer meetup is a lightweight community gathering designed for local AI developers, typically featuring technical presentations, project demos, and networking. The format is flexible and casual, making it a key venue for building local AI developer communities and fostering technical exchange.

## Core Objectives

- Build and grow the local AI developer community
- Foster peer-to-peer knowledge sharing among developers
- Share AI development experience and best practices
- Identify local AI technical talent
- Cultivate a strong sense of community belonging

## Distribution Timeline

### T-1 Week: Recruitment Launch
- Publish event announcement
- Reveal session topics
- Open registration
- Distribute via WeChat groups and Jike

### T-3 Days: Warm-Up Phase
- Publish speaker introductions
- Release full agenda
- Send registration reminders
- Share venue and transportation details

### T-Day: Event Day
- Run check-in at the door
- Share live session highlights in real time
- Encourage attendees to post photos and updates
- Facilitate networking activities

### T+1 Day: Post-Event Phase
- Publish event recap
- Organize and share presentation materials
- Collect attendee feedback
- Announce preview of next edition

## Venue Selection Guidelines

### Venue Types
- AI company offices
- Co-working spaces
- Cafés or restaurants
- University facilities

### Selection Criteria
- Convenient transportation access
- Appropriate capacity
- Projection and audio equipment
- Open space for networking
- AI-native environment is a plus

## Session Content Design

### Presentation Formats
- Technical Talk: 15–20 minute thematic presentation
- Project Demo: 10-minute AI project demo
- Lightning Talk: 5-minute rapid-fire share
- Panel Discussion: open discussion on AI topics

### Recommended Agenda Structure
- Opening and Introductions: 5 minutes
- Featured Talks: 1–2 sessions, 40–60 minutes total
- Lightning Talks / Demos: 2–3 sessions, 15–20 minutes total
- Free Networking: 30–60 minutes

### Suggested Topics
- Large language model application in practice
- Lessons learned from AI tools and workflows
- Open-source project showcases
- Lessons learned from real-world challenges
- Industry trend discussions

## Beginner-Friendly Strategy

### Welcome Mechanisms
- New attendee introductions segment
- Encourage returning members to initiate conversations
- Prepare introductory AI learning resources
- Establish a mentor pairing program

### Content Design
- Include introductory-level content in each agenda
- Avoid excessive technical jargon
- Actively encourage questions
- Offer post-event Q&A support

## Jike Operations Focus

### Event Distribution
- Post to popular circles such as AI Explorer Station
- Invite community thought leaders to reshare
- Publish a post-event recap after the meetup

### Community Engagement
- Spark discussion threads around event topics
- Cross-promote with speakers' Jike accounts
- Encourage attendees to share posts on Jike
