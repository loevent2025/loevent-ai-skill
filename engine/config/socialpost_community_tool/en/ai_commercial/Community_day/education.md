# AI Cafe – Education Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Private café venue + coworking study session + peer Q&A + casual hangout |
| Duration | Half-day to full day |
| Participants | AI learners, students, mentors (drop-in during select hours) |
| Core Objective | Study together, help each other out, meet fellow students, relaxed atmosphere |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides the standard style:**

✅ Casual, youthful, study-café vibes
✅ Emphasize "together," "help each other," "classmates"
✅ Drop the classroom feel — bring in the café feel

❌ Do NOT use words like "course," "training," or "assessment"
❌ Avoid the formal tone of educational institutions
❌ Do NOT highlight learning outcomes

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| P0 | AI Learners | Finding a place to study, meeting classmates |
| P1 | Bootcamp Students | Working on assignments together, helping each other |
| P2 | Self-learners | Having someone to ask, not studying alone |

---

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Café Study Atmosphere (required)
- Studious but relaxed vibe
- Example: "A café turned study room — take a break and chat when you need to"

### 2. Peer Support (required)
- You can ask each other questions
- Example: "Stuck on something? Ask the person next to you — they might've just figured it out"

### 3. Mentor Drop-in (optional)
- Mentors stop by for Q&A
- Example: "Mentors will swing by for coffee in the afternoon — feel free to ask questions"

### 4. Zero Pressure (required)
- This isn't a class — it's a study session
- Example: "Go at your own pace — nobody's grading you"

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Turning the study session into a lecture
- ❌ Assigning mandatory study tasks
- ❌ Assessing or grading learning outcomes
- ❌ Using words like "must" or "required"
