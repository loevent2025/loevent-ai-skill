# AI Social Mixer – Education Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Casual party + icebreaker social + celebratory vibe |
| Duration | 3–4 hours |
| Participants | AI learners, students, mentors, AI-curious newcomers |
| Core Objective | Celebrate, unwind, meet fellow students, build connections |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides the standard style:**

✅ Casual, youthful, celebratory
✅ Emphasize "together," "classmates," "friends"
✅ Zero study pressure — pure party

❌ Do NOT use words like "learning," "growth," or "takeaways"
❌ Avoid the formal tone of educational institutions
❌ Do NOT highlight courses or certificates

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| P0 | AI Learners / Students | Celebrating, socializing, meeting classmates |
| P1 | Mentors | Casual interaction with students |
| P2 | AI-Curious Newcomers | Exploring the community, making friends |

---

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Scene-first Opening (required)
- Youthful, energetic, party vibes
- Example: "Music, beers, and a crowd fresh off their AI course"

### 2. Sensory Immersion (at least 3 of the following)
- Visual: Lighting, party décor, young faces
- Taste: Beer, cocktails, pizza
- Sound: Pop music, laughter, clinking glasses
- Atmosphere: Celebratory, relaxed, youthful — no code talk

### 3. Celebratory Tone (required)
- "Class is done — tonight, we don't study"
- "The night classmates become friends"

### 4. No-pressure Participation (required)
- "No prep needed — just show up"
- "Finished the course or not, everyone's welcome"

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Turning the party into a graduation ceremony
- ❌ Emphasizing "learning outcomes" or "skill development"
- ❌ Scheduling formal presentation segments
- ❌ Making it feel overly official
