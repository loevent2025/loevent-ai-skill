# AI Cafe — General Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Café takeover + Open coworking + Free-form socializing |
| Duration | Half-day to full-day (time slots) |
| Participants | AI practitioners, enthusiasts, and the curious |
| Core Objective | Work in a new setting, meet people, soak in the atmosphere |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides standard styles:**

✅ Relaxed, open, everyday café feel
✅ Emphasize the scene and atmosphere
✅ Work, socialize, or just zone out — all welcome

❌ Do not use "industry exchange" or "resource alignment"
❌ Avoid any transactional framing
❌ Do not imply anyone has to do anything specific

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Café Scene Setting (required)
- Concrete atmosphere description
- Example: "Sunlight, coffee, and laptops"

### 2. Open Participation (required)
- All types of people are welcome
- Example: "Building in AI, curious about AI, or just here for the coffee"

### 3. Flexible Format (required)
- Work or socialize — either is fine
- Example: "Work if you want to work, chat if you want to chat"

### 4. Time Slot Design (required)
- Choose a time slot or drop in anytime
- Example: "Pick a slot to cowork, or just swing by whenever"

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Scheduling formal sessions or agendas
- ❌ Forcing introductions
- ❌ Using words like "networking," "resource alignment," or "matchmaking"
- ❌ Turning a café day into a conference
