# Web3 Large-Scale Conference Knowledge Base — Investment Scene

## Scene Overview
Investment-focused Web3 large-scale conferences center on fundraising, project pitching, and capital matchmaking in the crypto space, bringing together Web3 founders, crypto VCs, corporate venture capital, family offices, and financial advisors. These events are core venues for discovering high-quality Web3 projects, understanding investment cycle trends, and building capital relationships. They also serve as important stages for founders to secure funding and raise their project's profile.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Hundreds to thousands of participants
- **Duration**: Typically 1–2 days
- **Format**: Project pitches + investment forums + 1-on-1 meetings + closed-door sessions + awards ceremonies
- **Participants**: Web3 founders, Crypto VC/funds, family offices, FA/OTC firms, exchange investment arms, incubators, and government-backed funds
- **Defining Traits**: High concentration of projects, capital-dense environment, efficient deal-making, cycle-sensitive

## Attendee Tips

### For Investors
- Review the project list, decks, and on-chain data in advance
- Define your priority verticals and target stages for this event
- Prepare a standard DD question checklist (technology / tokens / security / team)
- Pre-schedule 1-on-1 Office Hours with projects of interest
- Prepare a concise fund overview and investment criteria summary

### For Founders
- Prepare a tight, compelling 8-minute pitch + live demo
- Anticipate likely investor DD questions (token design / security / competition)
- Have pitch decks ready at varying levels of detail + a concise one-pager
- Research each attending fund's portfolio and investment focus
- Prepare an on-chain data dashboard and smart contract audit report

### Engagement Tips
- Lead with your core value and traction — make it clear within 30 seconds
- Let on-chain data tell the story — avoid vague narrative without substance
- Be direct and transparent on token model and security questions — don't deflect
- Follow up proactively within 24 hours via Telegram or email and define the next step
- Take thorough notes and organize your key takeaways and feedback promptly

## Compliance & Content Notes
- **Compliance disclaimers required**: NFA / DYOR / not investment advice / introduction ≠ commitment
- **Do not reference token recommendations, price predictions, or return commitments**
- **Investor lineup must include decision-makers**: GP / Managing Director level
- **Clarify the matchmaking process**: 1-on-1 booking flow, Office Hours rules
- **Dual-sided value**: content must be compelling for both investors and founders
- **Substantive data and analysis required**: on-chain metrics, vertical insights — not empty narrative
