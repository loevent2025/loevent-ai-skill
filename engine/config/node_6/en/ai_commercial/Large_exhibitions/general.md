# AI Industry — Large-Scale Exhibition / Expo Marketing Strategy

## Event Overview

Large-scale exhibitions are among the most influential in-person event formats in the AI industry, bringing together global AI companies, research institutions, developers, and enterprise users. These events typically feature keynote speeches, product showcases, technical demos, and business networking — making them a primary venue for brand exposure, product launches, and industry dialogue.

---

## Core Objectives

- Brand exposure and industry positioning
- Product and technology launch announcements
- Building strategic industry partnerships
- Attracting potential clients and collaborators
- Generating media coverage and industry influence

## KOL Partnership Strategy

### KOL Profile Types

| Type | Profile Description | Collaboration Format |
|------|---------------------|----------------------|
| Technical Thought Leaders | Prominent AI experts, technical leads from major tech companies | Keynote speaking, content endorsement |
| Product KOLs | AI product managers, startup founders | Product talks, panel participation |
| Science Communicators | AI education content creators on Bilibili / Xiaohongshu | Content creation, on-site reporting |
| Media KOLs | Tech media editors-in-chief, industry columnists | In-depth reporting, exclusive interviews |

### Collaboration Formats

- **Pre-event:** Event preview reposts, exclusive interviews
- **During event:** On-site check-ins, live streaming collaborations
- **Post-event:** In-depth recaps, product reviews

## Distribution Timeline

### T–4 Weeks: Launch Phase

- Publish event preview announcement
- Establish event community groups
- Initiate media partnership discussions
- Release first round of key visual assets

### T–3 Weeks: Warm-up Phase

- Announce keynote speakers in batches
- Publish agenda overview
- Activate KOL partnerships
- Publish in-depth event preview on Official Account

### T–2 Weeks: Build-up Phase

- Release product and technology teasers
- Announce booth details and partners
- Publish in-depth media features
- Roll out pre-event video content

### T–1 Week: Final Push Phase

- Daily countdown posts
- Last-call registration reminders
- Tease on-site highlights
- Publish attendee guide

### Event Days

- Real-time on-site updates
- Live product launch coverage
- Stream key sessions and moments
- Encourage attendees to share content

### T+1 Week: Post-event Phase

- Publish event highlight recap
- Publish in-depth product coverage
- Release performance data roundup
- Publish industry insights and analysis
