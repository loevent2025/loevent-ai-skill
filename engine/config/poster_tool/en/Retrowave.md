# RETROWAVE / VAPORWAVE Style

## Use Cases
Conference key visuals, hackathons, NFT airdrop events, crypto art festivals, blockchain community meetups, cultural showcases

## Signature Color Palette
- **Primary Colors:** Deep purple (#1A0F3D), neon pink (#FF1B8D), neon cyan (#00D4FF), vivid magenta (#8B4A9E)
- **Gradients:** Multi-stop gradients transitioning from purple → pink → cyan
- **Background:** Deep-space purple with scattered star particles
- **Neon Colors:** High-saturation pink, cyan, and purple with glow effects
- **Shadows:** Deep purple-black (#0A0520)
- **Characteristics:** Retro neon, blue-pink gradients, nostalgic color grading, soft light bloom, 80s–90s aesthetics

## Visual Elements

### Illustration Style
- **Line work:** Hand-drawn comic/cartoon style with bold black outlines (3–5px)
- **Objects:** Clean, defined elements (cryptocurrency coins, NFT frames, wallet icons, token badges, DAO badges)
- **Perspective:** Diagonal top-down view with dynamic tilted composition
- **Style:** Flat illustration with dimensional shadows
- **Details:** Handwritten typography, tech labels, blockchain lettering
- **Texture:** Subtle grain or noise for analog feel (optional, 5–10%)

### Elements
- **Crypto symbols:** Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoin logos with neon treatment
- **NFT artifacts:** Digital art frames, collectible cards, pixel art displays
- **Simple icons:** Digital wallets, DAO badges, token badges
- **Geometric shapes:** Abstract pills, capsules, geometric ornaments
- **Metaverse objects:** 3D avatars, virtual blocks, digital wearables
- **Token representations:** Coin stacks, token badges, simplified logos

### Backgrounds & Decorations
- **Space backdrop:** Deep-purple starfield with scattered star particles
- **Grid lines:** Optional retro perspective grid (vaporwave style)
- **Floating elements:** Abstract geometric shapes, light particles, sparkles, simple pills/capsules
- **Atmospheric effects:** Purple haze, soft mist, neon fog

## Visual Treatment

### Neon Lighting Effects
- **Outer glow:** Strong neon glow on all bright elements (20–40px)
- **Edge lighting:** Bright pink/cyan rim lighting on objects
- **Soft bloom:** Gaussian blur halo effect (30–50px radius)
- **Light rays:** Diagonal gradient beams cutting through the composition
- **Reflections:** Subtle neon reflections on object surfaces

### Color Gradients
- **Multi-stop gradients:** 4–6 color stops transitioning from purple → pink → cyan
- **Gradient overlay:** Color wash applied across the entire composition
- **Chromatic aberration:** Slight RGB color separation at edges (optional, 2–3px)
- **Analog color grading:** Cinematic color shift with nostalgic warmth
- **Saturation:** High saturation for neon tones; desaturated in shadow areas

### Texture Treatment
- **Line quality:** Slightly imperfect hand-drawn line work with organic character
- **Shadows:** Cel-shading or gradient shadows on objects
- **Highlights:** Bright specular highlights on reflective surfaces
- **Grain:** Optional film grain or VHS noise (5–8% opacity)
- **Surfaces:** Mix of matte and glossy finishes

## Composition Strategy

### Perspective & Layout
- **Viewpoint:** 30–45° diagonal top-down perspective
- **Composition:** Objects arranged diagonally or radially with clear spacing
- **Focal point:** Central hero object (largest and brightest)
- **Balance:** Well-separated elements in asymmetric dynamic balance
- **Depth:** Overlapping objects create spatial hierarchy while maintaining legibility

### Element Arrangement
- **Hero object:** Central focal point (30–40% of the frame), highest contrast — primary crypto coin or NFT artwork
- **Supporting objects:** 3–5 secondary clean elements (15–20% each) — tokens, wallet icons, NFT frames, geometric shapes
- **Small details:** Simple scattered particles, stars, minor geometric accents (5–10%)
- **Negative space:** 25–30% breathing room, primarily in the background
- **Dynamic flow:** Diagonal lines guide the viewer's eye through the composition
- **Clear separation:** Visible spacing between every element; no cluttered overlapping

### Visual Hierarchy
- **Foreground:** Brightest neon objects with the strongest glow
- **Midground:** Supporting elements at moderate brightness
- **Background:** Dark space with stars and atmospheric effects
- **Glow layer:** Dedicated lighting effects layer behind objects

## Typography Settings
- **Color:** Choose solid colors that create high contrast against the deep purple background
  - Primary headlines: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
  - Emphasis text: Neon pink (#FF1B8D) or neon cyan (#00D4FF)
  - Alternative: Vivid magenta (#8B4A9E)
- **Fill Style:** Solid fill, single flat color, no gradients
- **Prohibited Effects:**
  - No outer glow or inner glow
  - No neon lighting effects
  - No outlines or strokes (text only — distinct from the bold black outlines on illustration elements)
  - No drop shadows or cast shadows
  - No gradient fills
  - No chromatic aberration
  - No text effects of any kind
- **Overall Principle:** All text remains clean, minimal, and highly legible — forming a deliberate contrast against the neon glow effects and illustration elements in the background. Retro character is established through typeface selection and typographic layout, not through visual effects

## Art References

## Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 1080×1920 for vertical social media; 4K for presentations; 300 DPI for print
- **Color Space:** RGB, wide gamut for vivid neon reproduction
- **Software:** Procreate for illustration; Photoshop for effects; After Effects for animation
- **Line Weight:** 3–5px for primary outlines; 1–2px for detail work
- **Glow Effects:** Layer blend modes (Screen, Add, Linear Dodge)
- **Formats:** PNG with transparency; JPG for final output; GIF for animated versions
