# AI Hackathon – Education Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply the rules in this module when the event theme involves the following keywords:
- AI beginner hackathons, introductory coding marathons, learning-focused AI hackathons
- University AI hackathons, student developer challenges, AI bootcamp capstone competitions
- Prompt engineering hands-on competitions, AI skill certification challenges

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Pre-event training + mentor-led teams + hands-on development + project showcase |
| Duration | 24–48 hours, typically paired with a training course |
| Participants | AI learners, students, mentors, training institutions |
| Core Objective | Hands-on practice, validating learning outcomes, building a project portfolio |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| P0 | AI Learners | Hands-on opportunities, skill validation, portfolio building |
| P1 | University Students | Résumé boost, internship opportunities, industry exposure |
| P2 | Career Changers | Validating their technical transition, gaining project experience, job search preparation |
| P3 | Bootcamp Students | Applying coursework, capstone assessment, skill certification |

---

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| University AI Hackathon | Energetic & Engaging (B) | Learning atmosphere, growth experience |
| Beginner Hackathon | Energetic & Engaging (B) | Low barrier to entry, mentor support |
| Bootcamp Capstone Competition | Benefits-oriented (D) | Certification, career opportunities |
| Skill Challenge | Suspense-driven (C) | Skill validation, proving capability |

## ❓ FAQ Templates

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Can I join with zero experience? | Absolutely! We offer pre-event training and mentor support |
| Do I need to form my own team? | You can team up on your own or be assigned to a group on-site |
| What if I don't finish my project? | Completion isn't the only metric — the learning process matters most |
| What is the certificate good for? | It serves as proof of learning; recognized by select employers |

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Over-emphasizing competition and rankings
- ❌ Putting excessive pressure on beginners
- ❌ Overstating the career value of certificates
- ❌ Promising guaranteed internship or job placements
