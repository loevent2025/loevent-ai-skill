# AI Tech Workshop Knowledge Base – General

## Scene Overview
General AI tech workshops are hands-on learning events open to a broad range of participants. Under the guidance of experienced instructors, participants learn AI technologies and complete real working projects. These events prioritize practical, immediately applicable skills — making them one of the most efficient formats for mastering AI and solving real-world problems. Suitable for participants with varied backgrounds and experience levels.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Typically 20–100 participants, balanced for quality interaction
- **Duration**: Half-day to 2 days
- **Format**: Instruction and demonstration + hands-on practice + Q&A + project showcase
- **Participants**: Developers, product managers, enterprise employees, AI enthusiasts
- **Key Traits**: Highly practical, immediately applicable, clear deliverables

## Curriculum Design Recommendations

### Content Planning
- Define clear learning objectives and expected deliverables upfront
- Keep conceptual instruction to 30% or less of total time
- Let hands-on practice drive the majority of the session
- Anticipate and prepare solutions to common participant issues
- Design exercises that progressively build in complexity

### Preparation
- Provide complete, well-structured code templates
- Set up a ready-to-use development environment
- Create clear, step-by-step operation guides
- Allocate generous time for Q&A
- Have backup plans ready for unexpected technical issues

### On-Site Execution
- Manage pacing to keep all participants on track
- Monitor participant progress throughout the session
- Address questions promptly and thoroughly
- Encourage peer support and collaboration
- Manage time effectively across all segments

### Post-Workshop Support
- Distribute the full set of course materials and code
- Establish a community channel for ongoing discussion
- Collect participant feedback and iterate on the program
- Provide a curated guide for continued self-directed learning
- Offer follow-up office hours and optional re-audit access
