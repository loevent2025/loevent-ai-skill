# Web3 Cafe Cursor — Community Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Café setting + community gathering + open coworking + casual socializing |
| Duration | 3–5 hours, flexible time slots, come and go as you please |
| Participants | Community members, Builders, ecosystem developers, community newcomers |
| Core Objective | Community bonding, belonging, like-minded gathering, casual coworking |

---

## 🎨 Tone & Style Override

When a Cafe Cursor community event is detected:

✅ Relaxed, community-driven, sense of belonging
✅ "Grabbing coffee with community frens" vibe
✅ Coding or just chatting — both welcome
✅ Community culture flows naturally (WAGMI, GM, etc.)
✅ Flexible timing, come and go freely

❌ Do NOT write it like a formal community meeting
❌ Don't overdo the slogans
❌ Do NOT create a sense of distance between OGs and newcomers
❌ Don't force participation in any segment

**Core Principle**:
🎯 This is a coffee hangout for community frens. Bring your laptop or don't. Talk projects or just socialize. A sense of belonging is the best takeaway.

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Community Vibe Opening (required)

Lead with a "community hangout" vibe:

**Formula**:
```
[Café environment] + [Community gathering] + [Frens together]
```

**Key Points**:
- ❌ Don't write: "Community meeting"
- ✅ Do write: "Hanging out with community frens at a café"
- Make it feel like a warm community space

---

### 2. Five-Sense Immersion (include at least 3)

**Sight**:
- Space: "Sunlight pouring into the café," "long tables with some people coding and others chatting"
- Community elements: "Someone wearing the community tee," "community merch on the table"

**Taste**:
- Be specific: "Pour-over coffee," "lattes," "free refills on americanos"
- ❌ Avoid: "Beverages will be provided"
- ✅ Do write: "Unlimited coffee — essential fuel for a frens hangout"

**Sound**:
- Music: "lo-fi beats," "chill background music"
- Ambient sounds: "Chatter," "laughter," "someone discussing the latest community updates"

**Touch**:
- Temperature: "The café AC is just right"
- Connection: "Finally meeting that fren you've been chatting with online forever"

**Atmosphere**:
- Energy level: "Relaxed," "community warmth," "vibes are just right"
- Social dynamics: "OGs and newcomers mingling together," "some people chatting about projects, others just hanging out"

---

### 3. Flexible Timing Framework (required)

**Format**:
```
⏰ Pick a time that works for you:
- [Slot A] Afternoon session
- [Slot B] Evening session
- Drop by anytime for a bit
```

**Key Points**:
- Emphasize "come and go freely"
- No mandatory duration
- You can always just pop in and say hi

---

### 4. Cowork + Social Coexistence (recommended)

Both modes of participation are welcome:

**Key Points**:
- ✅ "Want to code? Code. Want to chat? Chat"
- ✅ "Bring your laptop to cowork, or just come to hang out"
- ✅ "Some people are heads-down coding, others are swapping community gossip"

---

### 5. Organic Community Spirit (recommended)

Community culture is the backdrop, not the headline:

**Key Points**:
- ✅ "GM frens," "WAGMI" — use naturally
- ✅ "Finally meeting IRL the person you've been chatting with online forever"
- ❌ Don't slogan every sentence

---

### 6. Newcomer-Friendly (required)

Make newcomers feel welcomed:

**Key Points**:
- ✅ "Whether you're an OG or just joined — if you're here, you're a fren"
- ✅ "Don't know anyone? No worries — by the end of your first coffee, you will"
- ✅ "OGs and newcomers hanging out together — nobody cares when you joined"
- ❌ Do NOT create a sense of distance between OGs and newcomers

---

### 7. Fostering Belonging (recommended)

Community warmth is the core:

**Key Points**:
- ✅ "The community exists because of you"
- ✅ "If you're here, you're a fren"
- ✅ "That feeling of finding your tribe"
