# Web3 Cafe Cursor - Investment Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Café setting + casual conversation + informal exchange + organic connections |
| Duration | 3-5 hours, flexible time slots, come and go freely |
| Participants | Founders, investors, FAs, project teams |
| Core Objective | Casual introductions, informal exchange, building initial connections |

---

## 🎨 Tone & Style Override

When a Cafe Cursor investment event is detected:

✅ Relaxed, café atmosphere, non-transactional
✅ "Grab a coffee and chat" vibe — not a formal roadshow
✅ Investment topics come up naturally, never forced
✅ Investors are here for coffee too, not to sit through pitches
✅ Flexible timing, come and go as you please

❌ Do not write it as a formal roadshow or investor matching session
❌ Avoid transactional vocabulary like "fundraising opportunity" or "investor matching"
❌ Do not create a sense of hierarchy between investors and founders
❌ Do not pressure attendees into feeling they must pitch

**Core Principle**:
🎯 This is a "grab a coffee and chat" kind of space — investors and founders are both here to socialize. Having a great conversation matters most.

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Café Atmosphere Opening (mandatory)

Lead with the "coffee + casual chat" vibe:

**Formula**:
```
[Café setting] + [Relaxed socializing] + [Grab a coffee and chat]
```

**Key Points**:
- ❌ Don't write: "Investor matching session"
- ✅ Do write: "Come to the café — grab a coffee and chat with investors, founders, and builders"
- Make it feel like a relaxed social space

---

### 2. Five-Sense Immersion (include at least 3)

**Visual**:
- Space: "Sunlight streaming into the café," "floor-to-ceiling windows with a city view," "cozy sofa area"
- Social elements: "Small groups chatting," "someone sketching a business model on the whiteboard"

**Taste**:
- Be specific: "Pour-over coffee," "latte," "endless americano refills"
- ❌ Avoid: "Beverages provided"
- ✅ Write: "Coffee's on us — chat as long as you like"

**Sound**:
- Music: "Relaxed background music," "jazz"
- Ambient: "Low murmurs of conversation," "occasional laughter"

**Touch**:
- Temperature: "The café's AC is just right"
- Connection: "Finally meeting in person after only chatting online"

**Atmosphere**:
- Energy level: "Relaxed," "easy-going," "conversation flows naturally"
- Social dynamics: "Someone's having a great time at the counter," "you can't tell who's an investor and who's a founder"

---

### 3. Flexible Timing Framework (mandatory)

**Format**:
```
⏰ Pick a time that works for you:
- [Slot A] — Morning coffee
- [Slot B] — Afternoon tea
- Or just drop by whenever
```

**Key Points**:
- Emphasize "come and go freely"
- No mandatory time commitment
- It's fine to just pop in for a quick coffee and a hello

---

### 4. Non-Transactional Framing (mandatory)

Investment connections happen organically — don't force the spotlight:

**Key Points**:
- ❌ "Precision investor matching"
- ✅ "Mid-conversation, you realize there's more to talk about"

- ❌ "Fundraising opportunity"
- ✅ "Meet interesting people"

- Use details and scene-setting to convey this, rather than stating it outright

---

### 5. Zero Pitch Pressure (mandatory)

Make attendees feel at ease:

**Key Points**:
- ✅ "No pitch deck needed, no presentation required"
- ✅ "The investors are here for coffee and to unwind too"
- ✅ "Feel like talking projects? Go for it. Feel like just socializing? That works too"

- ❌ "Get your elevator pitch ready"
- ❌ "This is a rare investor matching opportunity"

---

### 6. Label-Free Descriptions (recommended)

Investors and founders are all just people here for coffee:

**Key Points**:
- ✅ "Some are investors, some are building, some are in advisory, some are just here for the coffee"
- ✅ "You won't know what someone does until you actually chat with them"
- ❌ "Star-studded investor lineup"
- ❌ "Face time with investors"

---

### 7. Compliance Disclaimer (mandatory)

```
"⚠️ Social event — conversations do not constitute investment advice or commitments (NFA)"
```

## ✅ Cafe Cursor Investment Event Quality Checklist

- [ ] **Café atmosphere**: Opening conveys a relaxed, social vibe
- [ ] **Flexible timing**: Emphasizes come-and-go freedom and optional time slots
- [ ] **Non-transactional**: No emphasis on "fundraising opportunities" or "investor matching"
- [ ] **Zero pitch pressure**: Clearly communicates that no pitch deck is needed
- [ ] **Label-free**: No heavy emphasis on the "investor vs. founder" divide
- [ ] **Relaxed tone**: Maintained throughout
- [ ] **Compliance disclaimer**: NFA notice included
- [ ] **Five-sense details**: At least 3 sensory details included

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Framing Cafe Cursor as a formal roadshow or investor matching session
- ❌ Using terms like "fundraising opportunity," "investor matching," or "resource acquisition"
- ❌ Creating a sense of hierarchy between investors and founders
- ❌ Pressuring attendees into feeling they must pitch
- ❌ Exaggerating investor lineups or credentials
- ❌ Implying or promising fundraising outcomes
