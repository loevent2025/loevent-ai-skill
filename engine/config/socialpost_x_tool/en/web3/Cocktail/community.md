# Cocktail / Social Events × Community

## Applicable Scenarios
Community mixers, DAO member social nights, holder meetups, community birthday parties, contributor appreciation receptions, NFT community IRL gatherings

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format**: IRL socializing for community members, community celebrations, pure fun
- **Core Value**: Community belonging, the magic of meeting in person, community culture experience

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Community members—people who interact online, meeting tonight IRL
- **Secondary Audience**: Community-curious—people who want to learn about this community
- **Extended Audience**: Friends of friends—brought along by community members
- **Audience Behavior**: Meeting IRL, community belonging, having fun, snapping photos to post in the community

## Content Elements
- Venue atmosphere
- Community identity call-out (casual version)
- "Finally meeting IRL" anticipation
- Community culture elements woven in
- No-pressure participation

## Language Treatment
- ✅ Community-native lingo, inside jokes
- ✅ "Finally putting faces to names" excitement
- ✅ Emphasize "our" community
- ❌ Don't be too official or too formal
- ❌ Avoid words like "exchange," "share," or "discuss"

## Structural Strategy
- Scene-setting opener block: 20%
- Community call-out block: 25%—use community language
- Atmosphere & experience block: 25%
- Community elements block: 15%
- Call-to-action block: 15%
- Emoji density control: 10–12 total, community-specific emoji welcome

## Emotional Atmosphere
Community belonging, finally meeting, hanging out together. The anticipation of "we've been chatting online forever—time to meet the real humans behind the screens."

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Community Mixer
- **Use Case**: Regular community IRL meetups
- **Opening Format**: 💙 [Community Name] IRL Night | Time to Step Out from Behind the Screen
- **Tone**: Anticipatory, community-driven, casual

### Variant B: Community Celebration
- **Use Case**: Community anniversaries, milestone celebrations
- **Opening Format**: 🎂 [Community Name] 1st Anniversary | Let's Celebrate Together
- **Tone**: Celebratory, grateful, warm

### Variant C: Holder Meetup
- **Use Case**: NFT/Token holder-exclusive events
- **Opening Format**: 🎫 Holders Only | IYKYK
- **Tone**: Exclusive, insider, unspoken understanding

## Atmosphere Keyword Library
IRL meetup, finally meeting, ours, community, together, real humans, the group chat, Discord, profile pics come to life, insiders, IYKYK

## Prohibited Vocabulary
❌ Exchange, share, learn, official, formal, event, project team

---

## Key Considerations

### Community Social Event Essentials
1. **Community language**: Use the community's own memes and style
2. **Don't be too official**: This is a community gathering, not a brand event
3. **Belonging is the core**: "Our" community
4. **Exclusive feel**: The special feeling of being holder/member-only
5. **Snap and post**: Sharing photos in the group chat is a key community ritual
