# AI DEMO DAY × INVESTMENT

## Applicable Scenarios
AI fundraising pitch days, investor-only demo days, VC portfolio showcases, angel investor matching sessions, corporate venture special events

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format:** Fundraising pitches + product demos + investor Q&A + 1-on-1 matching
- **Core Value:** Investor connections, feedback from capital allocators, deal flow, project screening

## Audience Profile
- **Primary Audience:** Investors — discovering AI projects, evaluating investment opportunities, screening projects efficiently
- **Secondary Audience:** AI founders — showcasing projects, gathering feedback, seeking investment
- **Tertiary Audience:** Corporate venture teams — identifying investment, acquisition, or partnership targets
- **Extended Audience:** Financial advisors — sourcing project information and connecting resources
- **Audience Behavior:** Screening projects, evaluating live demos, meeting founders, making investment decisions

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Institutional Focus:** VCs + founders — ideal for formal fundraising pitch days
- **Angel Focus:** Angel investors + early-stage founders — ideal for seed-stage matching

## Content Elements
- Investment positioning for the demo day (funding stage)
- Project information and traction data
- Investor and judge lineup
- Pitch + demo format details
- 1-on-1 matching mechanism
- Compliance disclaimer (required)

## Language Guidelines
- Investor-first perspective; lead with project quality and data
- Investor lineup is the primary draw
- Demo completeness and commercialization progress are the key signals
- Matching mechanism must be clearly explained
- NFA disclaimer is mandatory

## Content Structure
- Opening info block: 15%
- Project overview block: 25% — project quality + traction highlights
- Investor lineup block: 25% — investor backgrounds
- Matching mechanism block: 20% — how connections are made
- Compliance and action block: 15%
- Emoji density: 8–12 total, professional and restrained

## Tone & Atmosphere
Professional, efficient, and deal-oriented. The anticipation of: *"Finding the next AI unicorn."*

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Fundraising Pitch Day
- **Best for:** Explicitly fundraising-oriented demo days
- **Opening format:** 🚀 {Demo Day Name} | AI Founders, Meet Your Investors
- **Tone:** Fundraising-driven, efficient matchmaking

### Variant B: Investor-Only Session
- **Best for:** Closed sessions exclusively for investors
- **Opening format:** 💼 {Demo Day Name} | Investor-Only AI Project Showcase
- **Tone:** Curation-focused, quality-assured

### Variant C: Corporate Venture Session
- **Best for:** Sessions targeting corporate VC and strategic investment teams
- **Opening format:** 🏢 {Demo Day Name} | Corporate Venture AI Project Matching
- **Tone:** Strategic partnership, industry collaboration

## Attendee Tips

### For Founders
- Prepare a pitch version tailored specifically for investors
- Test your demo repeatedly in advance — it must be stable
- Have your data ready; investors will ask for specifics
- Know your fundraising ask and valuation rationale before walking in

### For Investors
- Review project materials ahead of time; come with questions
- Focus on the authenticity and completeness of the live demo
- Observe how the team handles unexpected questions in real time
- Follow up promptly with teams you're interested in

## Notes

### Key Principles for Investment Demo Days
1. **Compliance Disclaimer Is Mandatory:** NFA; matchmaking ≠ investment commitment
2. **Project Quality Must Be Vouched For:** Traction data, funding stage, demo completeness
3. **Investor Lineup Is Critical:** Attendees must have actual decision-making authority
4. **Matching Mechanism Must Be Clear:** How to schedule meetings, how to follow up
5. **Demo Is the Deciding Factor:** AI projects must demonstrate live functionality on-site
