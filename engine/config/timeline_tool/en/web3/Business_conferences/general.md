# Web3 Business Conference Knowledge Base

## Scenario Overview

Business conferences are professional event formats in the Web3 industry for strategic discussions, industry trend analysis, and business cooperation. Participants are typically industry decision-makers, investors, project founders, and core management, focusing on in-depth communication and business value.

## Tag Classification Extension

| Tag | Web3 Business Conference Specific Content |
|-----|-------------------------------------------|
| **venue** | Meeting room booking, business reception area, private discussion rooms, simultaneous interpretation equipment |
| **commercial** | Business invitations, investor matchmaking, strategic partnership negotiations, media interviews |
| **content** | Industry report release, closed-door roundtables, strategic speeches, case sharing |
| **others** | Business etiquette, reception procedures, non-disclosure agreements, meeting minutes |

## Web3 Business Conference Specific Dependency Chains

### Chain E - Guest Management
Key guest invitation (5d) → Itinerary confirmation (2d) → Reception plan (2d) → Exclusive services (1d)

### Chain F - Agenda Planning
Topic planning (3d) → Agenda design (2d) → Presentation material collection (3d) → Agenda confirmation (1d)

### Chain G - Business Matchmaking
Attendee list screening (3d) → One-on-one meeting arrangement (3d) → Discussion room allocation (1d)

### Chain H - Confidentiality and Compliance
NDA preparation (2d) → Information security audit (2d) → Compliance check (1d)

## Event Task Expansion

### Mandatory Tasks (Core)
- Attendee invitation (5d)
- Agenda planning and design (3d)
- Meeting room setup (2d)
- Business reception process (2d)
- Meeting material preparation (3d)
- Check-in system deployment (1d)

### Optional Tasks (Based on preparation time)
- Industry report drafting (4d)
- Closed-door roundtable organization (3d)
- One-on-one business matching (3d)
- Simultaneous interpretation service (2d)
- On-chain identity verification (2d)
- NFT attendance certificates (2d)
- Business dinner arrangement (2d)
- Media interview arrangement (2d)
- Conference live streaming system (2d)

### Post-Conference Tasks
- Meeting minutes compilation (2d)
- Business matchmaking follow-up (3d)
- Attendee feedback collection (1d)

## Time Compression Strategies

### Relaxed Mode (30+ days)
- Multiple rounds of guest invitations possible
- Prepare detailed industry reports
- Arrange multiple closed-door roundtables

### Compact Mode (15-29 days)
- Focus on core guest invitations
- Simplify industry report content
- Reduce the number of closed-door roundtables

### Urgent Mode (8-14 days)
- Only invite confirmed guests
- Use existing industry data
- Single main venue model

### Extreme Mode (1-7 days)
- Small-scale high-end closed-door meeting
- Simplify the agenda to core segments
- Cancel parallel sub-forums

## Risk Alerts

- ⚠️ High risk of changes in VIP guest itineraries, backup plans needed
- ⚠️ Involves commercially sensitive information, information security management needs to be strengthened
- ⚠️ International attendees need to consider visa and time difference issues
