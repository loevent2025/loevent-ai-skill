# Demo Day × Community Type (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
Open-source AI project Demo Days, community contributor showcases, LLM framework ecosystem demos, AI community project launches, hackathon showcase events, community co-built project reveals

## Scene Characteristics
- **Format**: Community project demos, contributor sharing, community voting, celebratory exchange
- **Core Value**: Contribution recognition, community showcase, Builder connections, ecosystem growth

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Community contributors—members who have built projects and want to showcase them
- **Secondary Audience**: Community members—people who want to see what the community is building
- **Tertiary Audience**: Potential contributors—newcomers who want to learn what the community is working on
- **Extended Audience**: Ecosystem partners—collaborators following the community ecosystem
- **Audience Behavior**: Showcase contributions, discover projects, build community connections, explore the ecosystem

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Contributor-Focused**: Contributors + core team
- **Full Community**: All community members
- **Ecosystem Collaboration**: Community + ecosystem partners

## Content Elements
- Community identity rallying
- Project origin context (hackathon / contribution / self-initiated)
- Community voting mechanism
- Contributor recognition
- Community feedback segments
- Pathways for future contributions

## Language Approach
- Community-oriented conversational tone
- Sense of belonging through "we" and "together"
- Contribution recognition
- Celebratory atmosphere
- Technically enthusiastic yet relaxed

## Structure Strategy
- Opening rallying block: 15%
- Project showcase block: 35%
- Community interaction block: 25%
- Recognition and celebration block: 15%
- Call-to-action block: 10%
- Emoji density: 12–16 total

## Emotional Tone
Community belonging, celebrating contributions. The warm feeling of "build together, celebrate together."

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Hackathon Showcase
- **Use for**: Post-hackathon / contribution marathon showcases
- **Opening Format**: 🎉 {Event Name} Showcase | See What Everyone Built
- **Tone**: Celebratory, showcase, results-driven

### Variant B: Community Co-Build
- **Use for**: Regular community project showcases
- **Opening Format**: 💙 {Community Name} Project Showcase Day | Creations by Community Builders
- **Tone**: Community-driven, belonging, co-building

### Variant C: Ecosystem Demo
- **Use for**: Ecosystem project showcases
- **Opening Format**: ⚡ {Ecosystem Name} Ecosystem Demo Day | What the Ecosystem Is Building
- **Tone**: Ecosystem-focused, rich, evolving

## Key Considerations

### Community Demo Day Essentials
1. **Belonging is the core**: Emphasize "the community's" and "ours"
2. **Community voting drives engagement**: Let community members decide
3. **Recognize every contribution**: Not just the award winners
4. **Celebratory atmosphere**: This is the community's festival
5. **Follow-up connections**: How projects continue to develop after the event
