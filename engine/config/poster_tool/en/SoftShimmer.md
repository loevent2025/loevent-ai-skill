# SOFT FUTURISM Style Guide

## Core Style Definition
This aesthetic envisions a future where **"technology should be gentle and invisible."** It rejects the cold rigidity of traditional cyberpunk in favor of a warmer, more approachable sensibility — one where technology and nature coexist harmoniously. Visuals are highly ordered, pure, and possess remarkable tactile depth. Every form is pushed toward extreme abstraction and softness, as if shaped by flowing water over time. The overall spirit communicates an optimistic vision: technology makes life better and lighter.

**Style positioning:** Modernist 3D abstract art + Neo-minimalist aesthetics + Healing futurism + Organic tactile design

- **Artistic characteristics:** Extreme abstraction, organic fluid forms, rounded soft edges, zero ornamentation, soft-focus dreaminess
- **Material textures:** Translucent resin, matte flexible surfaces, internal luminescence, pearlescent sheen, soft blurred edges
- **Light & shadow:** Global diffuse reflection, soft-key lighting, internal luminescence, zero harsh shadows, edge fade-out
- **Visual atmosphere:** Purity, breathability, weightlessness, soft tactility, dreamlike tenderness, organic poetry

## Color Palette

**Primary tones:** Fresh bright pastels, high-brightness low-saturation color system
- **Backgrounds:** Pure delicate gradients or infinitely extending void space
- **Saturation:** Low to medium, colors soft and translucent
- **Color relationships:** Gentle transitions, harmonious color blending, no strong contrasts

### Recommended Color Combinations
- **Fresh & Healing:** Mint green (#B5EAD7) + Sea salt blue (#C7CEEA) + Butter yellow (#FFEAA7) + Cream white (#FDFEFE)
- **Dreamy Pastels:** Soft lavender (#E0BBE4) + Powder blue (#D4F1F4) + Blush pink (#FEC8D8) + Ivory white (#FFFEF2)
- **Future Soft:** Light aqua (#A8E6CF) + Pale orange (#FFD3B6) + Lavender sage (#DCEDC1) + Pearl white (#FFF9F0)

- **Sky & Ocean:** Sky blue (#AED9E0) + Light green (#B8F2E6) + Pale yellow (#FFF3B0) + Soft pink (#FAF0E6)

## Visual Elements

### Core Forms
Extremely abstracted organic fluid shapes — soft spheres, flowing droplet forms, pebble shapes, free-form surfaces, irregular organic silhouettes.

### Theme-Responsive Element Design Principle
Primary visual elements should reference the event theme and organizer identity — abstracting the thematic concept into organic fluid forms that suggest rather than depict. For music events: wave-like fluid forms evoking sound waves. For cocktail receptions: soft container forms enclosing colored fluid evoking beverages. For tech events: suspended spheres suggesting innovation. For community events: multiple interacting fluid forms suggesting connection. **Key rule: avoid realistic representation — all elements must undergo abstraction, softening, and organic transformation.**

### Shape Characteristics
- No sharp edges or corners — all edges fully rounded and soft, as if shaped by long exposure to flowing water
- Edges blur and fade out, forms transitioning gently into backgrounds or adjacent elements
- Dominant shapes: soft spheres, droplet forms, pebble shapes, fluid curved surfaces, free organic forms
- Forms may flow as S-curves, arcs, or irregular curves to convey a sense of motion
- Shapes maintain breathing room and dynamic balance between one another
- **Avoid:** Regular geometric solids, hard edges, realistic shapes, complex surface detail

### Material Rendering
- **Core material:** Translucent resin — soft, warm, light-permeable, blurred edges
- Matte flexible surfaces (flat finish, soft to the touch)
- Internally visible blurred gradient colors or enclosed colored fluid
- Pearlescent sheen (subtle glow, gentle reflection, angle-dependent iridescence)
- Internal luminescence (soft radiance emanating from within the material)
- **Avoid:** Hard glass, metal, ceramic, or any realistic material textures

### "Container + Contents" Composite Form (for themes requiring enclosed elements)
- **Outer layer:** Semi-transparent light-toned resin container (milky white, pale beige, soft gray), edges soft and blurred
- **Inner layer:** Colored fluid or color field (chosen per theme), semi-transparent, edges blending into the container
- Overall form is abstract and organic — retaining thematic suggestion without realistic representation

### Space & Negative Space
- Infinite negative space — backgrounds are pure gradients or open void
- Objects suspended in space with no ground or support, emphasizing weightlessness and lightness
- Generous negative space to create breathing room and elegance

## Visual Effects

- **Soft-focus Lighting:** Global diffuse illumination — light appears to emanate from within the material, no directional emphasis
- **Zero Harsh Shadows:** Near-absence of deep shadows; where present, shadows are extremely light with soft, pastel-toned color (matching the element hue), edges fully blurred and dissolving into the background
- **Internal Luminescence:** Materials emit a gentle inner glow, creating an ethereal, dreamlike quality — as if self-illuminated
- **Soft Edge Fade:** All form edges blur and dissolve gently — no crisp boundaries between elements and background
- **Translucent Layers:** Multiple semi-transparent material layers stacked to create depth and rich visual dimensionality
- **Pearlescent Sheen:** Surfaces carry subtle iridescent reflections that shift with viewing angle, enhancing the dreamlike quality
- **High-key Lighting:** Overall composition is bright and luminous — no dark zones — creating a healing, uplifting atmosphere
- **Smooth Gradients:** Color transitions are extremely smooth and refined, with no visible color banding
- **Shallow Depth of Field:** Foreground sharp and crisp, background softly defocused for spatial depth

### Prohibited Effects
- No sharp edges or hard boundaries
- No realistic surface detail or complex textures
- No high-contrast shadows
- No dark tones or oppressive color schemes
- No hard materials (realistic rendering of metal, glass, or ceramic)
- No regular geometric shapes or technical-looking graphics

---

## Composition Strategy

- **Minimalist Layout:** Strip away all non-essential elements — retain only the core abstract forms
- **Floating Composition:** Objects suspended in void with no ground plane or support, emphasizing weightlessness
- **Central or Dynamic Balance:** Primary elements centered or symmetrically arranged, or achieving dynamic balance through scale and placement variation
- **Ample Negative Space:** Generous whitespace — objects occupy 20–40% of the frame — for breathing room
- **Flowing Arrangement:** Multiple elements arranged in flowing, orbital, or constellation-like configurations, maintaining visual tension
- **Clear Layering:** Foreground–midground–background distinguished through sharpness, opacity, and blur
- **Golden Ratio:** Primary elements placed at visual golden points
- **Breathing Rhythm:** Comfortable spacing between elements — not crowded — creating a relaxed visual cadence

---

## Typography Settings

- **Color selection:** Soft neutral tones or light colors that harmonize with the background
  - On light backgrounds: Neutral gray (#6B7280), soft blue-gray (#8B9DC3), muted lavender-gray (#9B8FB3), soft warm gray (#8B7D6B)
  - On dark backgrounds (use sparingly): Cream white (#FDFEFE), pale beige (#FFF9F0)
- **Font style:**
  - Modern geometric sans-serif
  - Tall elegant letterforms
  - Rounded humanist sans-serif
- **Font weight:** Light to Medium — avoid heavy weights; maintain an elegant, airy feel
- **Fill method:** Solid color fills only — single color per element, no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No gradient fills
  - No drop shadows
  - No outer glow / inner glow
  - No strokes or outlines
  - No blur effects
  - No text effects of any kind
- **Placement:**
  - Clean typographic layout, typically positioned at the top or bottom of the frame
  - Maintain visual balance and adequate distance from organic elements
  - Never overlap or obscure primary 3D elements
- **General principle:** Typography should be "invisible yet elegant" — never competing with the primary visual elements, always consistent with the composition's minimalist and soft aesthetic.

## Mood & Atmosphere

**Core emotions:** Tender futurism, healing technology, pure optimism, soft tactility, dreamlike poetry, organic warmth

This style communicates an idealized vision of the future — technology no longer cold or imposing, but rather like soft light, flowing water, warm touch, and clouds from a dream. It evokes a sense of safety, calm, and optimistic anticipation. The pure negative space symbolizes infinite possibility. The deeply abstracted organic forms suggest a harmonious coexistence of nature and technology. Blurred, fading edges create a dreamlike sense of ease. This is a vision of **"effortless modernity"** — highly advanced yet never aggressive, forward-looking yet accessible, refined yet never cold, abstract yet never opaque.

**Applicable contexts:** Technology brand launches, Web3 community events, contemporary art exhibitions, health and wellness products, sustainability initiatives, EdTech events, design and innovation conferences, premium social events, wine tastings, cocktail parties — any occasion requiring communication of "humanized technology," "gentle innovation," "healing futures," or "elegant modernity."

## Sample Prompts

### Prompt 1 — Social Cocktail Reception: The Flow of Connection

**Style definition:** Soft Futurism, modernist 3D abstract art, neo-minimalist aesthetics, healing futurism.

**Main scene:** A pure void space with an exceptionally refined background gradient — pale aqua (#A8E6CF) at the top flowing smoothly into warm beige (#F5E6D3) at the midpoint, dissolving at the base into pale orange (#FFD3B6) and cream white (#FFFEF2). The overall tone is bright, luminous, and warm. No texture of any kind. The background fills the entire frame, creating an infinitely extending elegant spatial quality.

**Primary visual element (cocktail reception theme — abstracted champagne vessel):** Suspended at the center of the frame, a large organic fluid form occupying 40–45% of the frame height. This is a radically abstracted and softened interpretation of a champagne flute — the overall elongated upward silhouette is suggested, but all edges are completely rounded and soft, the form shaped as if by flowing water into an organic body. The vessel itself is rendered in translucent resin (not realistic glass), with soft blurred edges and a gentle streamlined profile.

The vessel comprises two fluid layers:

- **Outer container (abstracted vessel body):** Semi-transparent matte resin, near-ivory or pale beige (#FFF9F0), 70–80% opacity. Edges fully rounded with no angles, form extending upward as a soft elongated oval or gentle teardrop. Surface carries a subtle pearlescent sheen; edges blur and fade into the background for a dreamlike effect.
- **Inner liquid (champagne):** Positioned inside the container, a gentle gradient from pale orange (#FFD3B6) to butter yellow (#FFEAA7). The liquid itself takes an organic fluid form — not a flat horizontal surface — like a suspended, flowing color field. Semi-transparent resin texture with minimal visible bubble detail inside (tiny semi-transparent circles or light points drifting within). Edges soft and blurred, blending naturally with the container.

The entire form is gently tilted or follows a soft S-curve for an elegant sense of motion — avoiding rigidity. All edges fade softly, consistent with the Soft Futurism "water-shaped" aesthetic.

**Supporting elements (abstracted beverage vessels):** Three to four smaller organic fluid forms suspended around the primary vessel, each approximately one-third to one-half the scale of the primary element, representing different beverages. Each is an abstracted soft "container + fluid" composition:

- **Mint drink form:** Rounded oval or pebble-shaped; outer layer in semi-transparent pale white resin (80% opacity); interior enclosing a mint green (#B5EAD7) to light aqua (#A8E6CF) fluid color field; edges soft and blurred; interior carries a minimal abstracted mint leaf suggestion (a small semi-transparent deep green form); the whole piece reads as a colored droplet encased in resin.
- **Rosé drink form:** Elongated soft teardrop or gentle cylinder; outer layer semi-transparent pale beige resin; interior in a blush pink (#FEC8D8) to soft lavender (#E0BBE4) fluid gradient; edges rounded and blurred; surface carries pearlescent sheen; the overall form is tender and graceful.
- **Blue drink form:** Round, low-profile pebble shape or soft sphere; outer layer semi-transparent resin; interior in sea salt blue (#C7CEEA) to sky blue (#AED9E0) fluid; a simplified lemon slice abstraction visible within (a small pale yellow semi-transparent disc floating inside); edges fully softened.
- **Amber drink form (optional):** Irregular organic shape; outer layer semi-transparent resin; interior in pale orange (#FFDAC1) to lemon yellow (#FFF3B0) fluid; minimal decorative suggestion at the top (a small colored sphere or abstracted cherry form).

All forms are semi-transparent resin enclosing colored fluid, with rounded blurred edges, pearlescent surfaces, and internal luminescence. Forms maintain breathing-room spacing, arranged in dynamic balance around the primary vessel; some gently tilted or in flowing postures to suggest toasting and social interaction. Avoid any realistic glass shapes — maintain the abstracted organic soft aesthetic throughout.

**Fluid ribbon elements (dialogue and flow suggestion):** Optionally, one to two minimal fluid ribbons or soft arcing lines between the vessel forms, in semi-transparent white or pale champagne (#FFDAC1). Lines are naturally soft with blurred edges, suggesting the flow of conversation, dissolving gently into the background.

**Light & shadow:** Global soft diffuse illumination, warm and even, creating a welcoming atmosphere. All forms cast extremely faint, soft, color-tinted shadows beneath them (using a lighter version of the interior fluid color, at only 30% opacity), edges fully blurred and absorbed into the background. Resin container surfaces carry subtle ambient light reflections that pick up the warm tones of the background. Interior fluid color fields emit a slight internal glow. Bubble or accent elements emit soft light points. Overall: high-key lighting, no dark zones, bright and healing.

**Technical details:** All forms are extremely rounded and soft — no hard edges, no realistic detail. Core material emphasis: soft translucent resin quality, gradient color fields within, blurred fading edges, pearlescent micro-reflections. Forms are abstract yet suggest their beverage themes through color and general silhouette. Color transitions are extremely smooth and refined. Shallow depth of field — primary form sharp, supporting forms gently defocused by distance. Generous negative space (60%+ whitespace) for breathing room and elegance. The overall composition maintains the core Soft Futurism aesthetic — dreamlike, soft, organic — with no hard or realistic qualities.

**Typography:** Event title in a modern elegant sans-serif (Avenir or Futura style), Light to Regular weight, in soft warm gray (#8B7D6B) or neutral warm gray at 90% opacity, letter-spacing widened, positioned at the top or bottom of the frame. Supporting information in a lighter weight, slightly softer in color. Optionally, pale gold (#E8D7B5) for the title to add an elegant accent.

**Overall atmosphere:** The graceful flow of social connection, the abstract poetry of fine beverages, soft warmth, inclusive friendliness, dreamlike healing, effortless delight. A perfect fit for upscale cocktail receptions, wine tastings, social dinners, and cocktail parties — embodying the core Soft Futurism aesthetic of softness, organic forms, dreamlike quality, and gentle tactility.

### Prompt 2 — Tech Brand Launch: Web3 Innovation Product

**Style definition:** Soft Futurism, 3D abstract tactile art, neo-minimalist aesthetics, human-centered tech design.

**Main scene:** An infinitely extending pure void. Background uses a premium gray-toned gradient — mist gray (#E8E8E8) at the top, transitioning to pale blue-gray (#B8C5D6) at the midpoint, dissolving at the base into warm beige (#F5E6D3) and pure white (#FFFFFF). The overall tone is refined and restrained, luminous and clear. No texture whatsoever — balancing professional tech presence with futuristic sensibility.

**Primary visual element (technology/innovation theme, abstracted):** Suspended slightly above center in the frame: a large organic fluid sphere or free-form surface occupying 35–40% of the frame width. The form is completely rounded and smooth, rendered in a refined gradient from sky blue (#AED9E0) to light green (#B8F2E6). Material is semi-transparent matte resin — within, a deeper layer of pale blue gradient is subtly visible, suggesting interior color depth like fluid layers inside. Edges soft and blurred, dissolving naturally into the background. Surface carries a pearlescent sheen with subtle angle-dependent iridescence, suggesting technology's multi-dimensional innovation. The shape reads as soft as a water droplet or cloud formation, symbolizing technology's approachability.

**Supporting geometric elements (suggesting connection/network/innovation):** Five to seven smaller 3D organic forms suspended around the primary element:
- 2–3 small semi-transparent soft spheres (diameter one-fifth to one-quarter of the primary element), in mint green (#B5EAD7) and pale yellow (#FFF3B0) semi-transparent resin, edges blurred
- 2 rounded pebble or fluid droplet shapes (diameter one-sixth of primary element), in soft pink (#FAF0E6) and pale orange (#FFD3B6) semi-transparent resin, forms soft and flowing
- 1–2 torus or ring forms in sea salt blue (#C7CEEA) semi-transparent resin, but with rounded, soft edges throughout — organically treated to avoid a regular geometric feel

These supporting elements are arranged in a loose orbital or constellation pattern around the primary form, suggesting decentralized networks and interconnection while remaining abstract and organic. All edges blurred and soft, with generous spacing between elements for dynamic balance and breathing room.

**Connecting elements (optional):** Between select smaller elements, extremely fine semi-transparent flexible curves (1px line weight), in pale white or light blue at 50–60% opacity, edges softly blurred, suggesting data flow and network connectivity — used sparingly so as not to disrupt the overall minimalist aesthetic.

**Light & shadow:** Soft diffuse illumination, light gentle and even with no clear direction. All objects cast extremely faint, soft shadows (in pale blue-gray at 30–40% opacity), edges fully blurred and defocused. The primary sphere's surface carries subtle ambient reflections picking up the pale blue and beige of the background. All semi-transparent materials emit a slight internal glow, as if gently filled with light. High-key overall — no dark zones — bright and professional.

**Brand element integration (optional):** If a specific logo or graphic is required, reduce it to its most simplified, abstracted organic equivalent (soft circle, fluid form, etc.), treated as one of the supporting small elements. Use soft colors harmonious with the overall palette in semi-transparent resin, edges blurred, maintaining the abstract treatment.

**Technical details:** All shapes are extremely rounded and soft, edges blurred and fading — no sharp edges. Material emphasis: soft translucent resin quality, diffuse refraction, pearlescent micro-reflections, ethereal internal luminescence. Color transitions extremely smooth and refined. Shallow depth of field — primary element sharp, peripheral elements gently defocused. Generous whitespace (60%+), elements floating in void to emphasize weightlessness and futurism. 3D depth is natural but not exaggerated — maintaining design sensibility and abstraction, avoiding any realistic detail or hard material feel.

**Typography:** Brand or product name in a modern tall sans-serif, Light weight, neutral gray (#6B7280) at 85–90% opacity, letter-spacing widened, placed in the lower third or at the base of the frame. Launch event information in a lighter weight with slightly softer color. Clean typographic layout maintaining visual balance with the organic elements.

**Overall atmosphere:** Humanized technology, gentle innovation, professional yet never cold, forward-looking yet approachable, premium restraint, dreamlike softness, optimistic futurism. A perfect fit for technology brand launches, Web3 product showcases, and innovation technology summits.
