# AI Social Mixer – Community Events Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Community party + Open socializing + Relaxed atmosphere |
| Duration | 3–5 hours |
| Participants | Community Builders, AI enthusiasts, community newcomers |
| Core Objective | Community bonding, reuniting with old frens, welcoming newcomers, pure fun |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides the standard style:**

✅ Relaxed, community-driven, friends-hanging-out vibe
✅ Emphasize "frens," "together," "us"
✅ Less "event" energy, more "party" energy

❌ No "networking," "resources," or "connections"
❌ Avoid sounding like an official organizer
❌ Do not emphasize the utilitarian value of the community

---

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Focus |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| P0 | Long-time community members | Seeing old friends, having a good time |
| P1 | Community newcomers | Fitting in, meeting people, finding belonging |
| P2 | AI enthusiasts | Getting to know the community, making friends |

---

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Scene-First Opening (required)
- Community feel + friends-gathering atmosphere
- Example: "Same spot, same frens, new faces welcome"

### 2. Sensory Immersion (at least 3 of the following)
- Sight: Familiar faces, community merch and decor
- Taste: Beers, community-special cocktails
- Sound: Music, laughter, "long time no see"
- Vibe: Feels like a friends' get-together, not an event

### 3. Community Belonging (required)
- "The whole crew's showing up"
- "Catch up with old frens, meet new ones"

### 4. No-Pressure Participation (required)
- "Just show up, no hoops to jump through"
- "If you're part of the community, you're in"
