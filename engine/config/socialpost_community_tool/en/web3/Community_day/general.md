# Web3 Cafe Cursor — General Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Café setting + Open cowork + Diverse socializing + Come-and-go flexibility |
| Duration | 4–6 hours, multiple time slots, flexible participation |
| Participants | Developers, founders, investors, learners, community members, industry practitioners |
| Core Objectives | Broad connections, Diverse conversations, Cowork companionship, Industry socializing |

---

## 🎨 Tone & Style Override

When a general-purpose Cafe Cursor event is detected:

✅ Relaxed, inclusive, open
✅ "Come hang at the café — code or social, your call"
✅ People from all backgrounds — diversity is the feature
✅ No single theme emphasized
✅ Flexible timing, come and go as you please

❌ Never write it like a formal conference or themed event
❌ Don't over-emphasize any single group
❌ No participation barriers whatsoever
❌ Don't create distance between any groups

**Core Principle**:
🎯 This is an open "come hang at the café" space. Want to code? Code. Want to social? Social. Everyone's welcome. Any topic is fair game.

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Open & Inclusive Opening (required)

Lead with a "come hang out" vibe:

**Formula**:
```
[Café environment] + [Open & inclusive] + [Come hang out]
```

**Guidelines**:
- ❌ Don't write with a specific theme restriction
- ✅ Do write: "Come hang at the café — code or social, your call"
- Make it feel like a completely open space

---

### 2. Five-Sense Immersion (include at least 3)

**Visual**:
- Space: "sunlight streaming into the café," "people coding and chatting at the long table," "city views through the floor-to-ceiling windows"
- Diversity: "all kinds of people mingling," "some writing code, some just socializing"

**Taste**:
- Be specific: "pour-over coffee," "lattes," "bottomless americanos," "afternoon tea snacks"
- ❌ Avoid: "Beverages provided"
- ✅ Do write: "Coffee's on us — grab whatever you like"

**Sound**:
- Music: "chill beats," "easy background music"
- Ambient: "clinking cups," "laughter," "conversations blending together"

**Touch**:
- Temperature: "the café AC is just right"
- Connection: "finally meeting someone you've only interacted with on Twitter"

**Atmosphere**:
- Energy level: "laid-back," "flow," "vibe just right"
- Social dynamics: "small clusters forming," "strangers hitting it off instantly," "conversations happening naturally"

---

### 3. Flexible Timing Framework (required)

**Format**:
```
⏰ Open all day — pick a time that works for you:
- [Time slot A]
- [Time slot B]
- [Time slot C]
- Or just drop by whenever
```

**Guidelines**:
- Emphasize "come and go as you please"
- All-day or extended hours
- Drop in anytime

---

### 4. Diversity as a Natural Feature (required)

Diverse backgrounds are the feature — no need to categorize:

**Guidelines**:
- ❌ "Bringing together investors, founders, and developers"
- ✅ "People from all kinds of backgrounds show up — you won't know what someone does until you start talking"

- ❌ "Cross-sector networking"
- ✅ "The Web3 circle isn't that big — everyone can find someone to chat with"

---

### 5. Flexible Participation Modes (required)

Both cowork and social are welcome:

**Guidelines**:
- ✅ "Bring your laptop and cowork"
- ✅ "Come empty-handed and just social"
- ✅ "Stay as long as you like"

---

### 6. Newcomer-Friendly (required)

Make everyone feel welcome:

**Guidelines**:
- ✅ "Whether you're an OG or brand new to the scene — welcome"
- ✅ "Don't know anyone? No worries — one coffee in and you will"
- ❌ Don't create distance between any groups

---

### 7. No Formal Agenda (recommended)

Open space, no structured programming:

**Guidelines**:
- ✅ "No agenda, no theme — just come hang out"
- ✅ "Someone might spontaneously share something, but nothing's mandatory"
- ❌ "Agenda," "Presentation session"

- [ ] **Diverse backgrounds**: No categorizing people — diversity is the feature

- [ ] **Newcomer-friendly**: Anyone would feel welcome reading this

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Writing a Cafe Cursor event as a formal conference or themed event
- ❌ Over-emphasizing any single group
- ❌ Setting any participation barriers
- ❌ Creating distance between any groups
- ❌ Using transactional language
- ❌ Including a formal agenda

---

## 🚫 Banned Vocabulary

**Transactional language**:
❌ "Resource matchmaking," "Network expansion," "Industry opportunity"
❌ "Deep exchange," "High-quality networking"

**Exclusionary language**:
❌ "Heavyweights gather," "Top-tier lineup," "Industry elite"
❌ "High-end," "Exclusive"

**Overly formal language**:
❌ "Agenda," "Presentation session," "Keynote"

---

## ✅ Recommended Vocabulary

**Open feel**:
✅ "Come hang out," "Code or social — your call"
✅ "People from all kinds of backgrounds," "You won't know what they do until you chat"

**Café feel**:
✅ "Coffee's on us," "Grab whatever you like"
✅ "Bring your laptop to cowork, or come empty-handed — both work"

**Low pressure**:
✅ "Come and go as you please," "Stay as long as you like"
✅ "No agenda — just come hang out"
