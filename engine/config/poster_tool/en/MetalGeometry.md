# CRYSTAL BURST — Flat Geometric Fragment Style Guide

---

## Intended Use Cases

Developer conferences, AI technology summits, tech product launches, cloud computing forums, artificial intelligence exhibitions, technology community annual conventions, programming language releases, open-source project conferences, data science summits, machine learning symposiums, blockchain technology conferences, enterprise technology days, innovation and technology expos.

---

## Color Palette

**Base Tones:** Deep blue-black, pure black, dark blue-gray

**Geometric Fragment Colors:** Electric blue, violet, magenta, cyan, fluorescent green, amber orange — small fragments in multi-color mix; solid fill only

**Text Colors:** Pure white, light gray

**Accent Colors:** Gradient blue-purple (logo icon use only) · Fluorescent green (data emphasis icons only)

**Saturation:** Fragments at high saturation; background at low saturation in deep tones

**Color Treatment:** Fragments use solid fill — no gradients. Text uses solid fill — no special effects.

## Visual Element Library (Select 2–3 per composition)

### Flat Geometric Fragments — Core Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Small square fragments** | Large quantities of tiny squares/rectangles; approx. 3–8px; solid fill |
| **Diamond shapes** | Small diamond/rhombus flat fragments; solid fill; no dimensional effect |
| **Parallelograms** | Diagonal parallelogram shapes implying motion direction |
| **Triangle fragments** | Small triangles; solid color fill |
| **Pixel particles** | Extremely small squares forming a particle cloud effect |
| **Thin lines** | Slender diagonal rectangles mixed among fragments |

### Fragment Distribution Patterns

| Pattern | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Diagonal flow** | Fragments arranged diagonally to create a dynamic directional stream |
| **Burst / explosion** | Fragments radiating outward from a single point |
| **Convergence** | Fragments gathering toward a focal point |
| **Random scatter** | Fragments distributed without a dominant direction |
| **Band distribution** | Fragments concentrated along a diagonal band |

### Decorative Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Gradient logo** | Brand graphic — gradient fill permitted on logo only |
| **Geometric icons** | Small solid-fill diamonds or triangles used as list markers |
| **Code symbols** | Decorative symbols: `>`, `<`, `/>` |

---

## Element Selection Principles

- **Keep fragments small:** Individual fragment size must remain tiny (approx. 3–12px) — visual impact is achieved through quantity, not scale.
- **Solid fill only:** Each fragment uses a single solid color; no gradients; no dimensional effects.
- **Color variety:** A single composition should include 4–6 distinct fragment colors.
- **Directional implication:** Fragment shapes and arrangement should suggest motion direction to enhance dynamism.
- **Density gradient:** Fragment density decreases outward from one focal area to create a natural visual anchor.
- **Do not obscure text:** Fragments should concentrate on one side or in a corner — never block core text content.

---

## Effects & Treatment

### Flat Geometric Fragment Treatment

- **Micro scale:** Each fragment strictly 3–12px
- **Solid fill:** Single color per fragment; no gradients
- **Fully flat:** No light/shadow faces; no 3D effect whatsoever
- **No sheen:** No metallic gloss or specular highlights
- **No shadows:** No drop shadows or cast shadows on any fragment
- **Sharp edges:** Crisp, precise geometric edges throughout
- **Multi-color mix:** Blue, purple, pink, cyan, green, and orange fragments distributed together
- **Density contrast:** Dense zones and sparse zones create visual rhythm

### Thin Line Treatment

- **Solid fill:** Lines use solid color from the same palette as fragments
- **Diagonal alignment:** Lines run in the same direction as fragments
- **Consistent weight:** Line width approx. 1–3px; keep slender

### Background Treatment

- **Deep, clean base:** Background is a solid fill of deep blue-black or pure black
- **No gradients:** Background receives no gradient treatment
- **No texture:** No noise or texture added to background
- **Fully flat:** Background functions as a pure-color canvas

### Text Treatment

- **Primary white:** Main text in pure white (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Solid fill:** All text in a single solid color; no gradients
- **No special effects:** No glow, shadow, stroke, or any text treatment
- **High legibility:** Text zones maintain a clean background to ensure readability

---

## Composition Strategy

- **Fragment offset:** Flat geometric fragments concentrated on the right side or a diagonal corner
- **Text left, visuals right:** Text information anchored to the left; fragment visuals to the right
- **Diagonal dynamism:** Fragments and lines arranged diagonally to energize the composition
- **Density as focal point:** The densest fragment cluster serves as the visual anchor
- **Negative space balance:** Generous negative space in the text zone contrasts with the fragment zone
- **Frontal flat perspective:** Strictly front-facing; fully flat; no perspective distortion; no dimensional effects

---

## Typography Rules

**Fill Method:** Solid color fill — single color, no gradients.

**Strictly Prohibited Effects:**
- Outer glow or inner glow
- Halo effects
- Stroke or outline
- Drop shadow or text shadow
- Gradient fill
- Contour lighting
- Any other text special effects

| Text Role | Style |
|-----------|-------|
| **Primary headline** | Large sans-serif; pure white; bilingual (Chinese/English) where applicable |
| **Subheading** | Medium sans-serif; pure white or light gray |
| **Data emphasis** | Numbers paired with small solid-fill diamond icons |
| **Supporting text** | Small sans-serif; white or light gray |

**Guiding Principle:** Typography must remain clean, legible, and professional. Hierarchy is built through font weight and scale — consistent with the flat, geometric aesthetic of the Crystal Burst style.

## Mood & Tone

Technology vanguard · Innovation burst · Data intelligence · Professional and premium · Future vision.

The overall atmosphere speaks to developers and the technical elite — full of innovative energy, professional yet dynamic. The scattered distribution of flat geometric fragments communicates the energy of technological breakthroughs and innovation, while maintaining the restraint and clarity of flat design.

**[Background]**
Entire background: deep blue-black (`#0D1B2A`) solid fill. Fully flat — no gradients, no textures.

**[Flat Geometric Fragment Visual — Core Element]**
Approximately 40% of the right side of the canvas is covered in large quantities of tiny flat geometric fragments. Fragment shapes include: small squares, diamonds, parallelograms, triangles, and slender diagonal lines. All fragments arranged diagonally, extending from the upper-right toward the lower-left, implying flow and movement.

Fragment size strictly controlled to micro scale (approx. 3–10px) — visual impact is achieved through quantity, not size.

All fragments are completely flat: solid fill, no gradients, no dimensional effects, no light/shadow faces, no metallic sheen, no reflective highlights, no shadows. Edges are sharp and precise. Fragment density is highest in the upper-right, gradually decreasing toward the lower-left for a natural diffusion effect. Diagonal thin lines are interspersed among the fragments in matching colors to reinforce directionality.

**[Main Visual Logo]**

Below the logo: main headline "魔搭开发者大会" (ModelScope Developer Conference) in large white Chinese text with slightly expanded letter-spacing. Below: English subtitle "ModelScope DevCon" in medium white text. All text: solid fill — no glow, no shadow, no stroke, no effects of any kind.

**[Event Information]**
Below the headline: year "2025" and date "06.30" in large light-weight white numerals. Below: venue "北京海淀 · 香格里拉饭店" in small white text.

**[Bottom Data Highlights]**

**[Overall Visual Requirements]**
The poster follows a text-left, visuals-right layout: clean text information zone on the left; vibrant flat geometric fragment zone on the right. Fully flat throughout — front-facing perspective; no perspective distortion; no dimensional effects. Fragments are tiny and precise (3–10px); solid fill, no gradients, no light effects. Deep background makes colorful fragments and white text stand out. All text: solid fill, clean and legible, no effects. Suitable for developer conferences, technology summits, and AI product launches.
