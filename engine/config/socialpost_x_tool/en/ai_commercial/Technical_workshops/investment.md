# Technical Workshop × Investment (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
AI technical due diligence workshops, AI technology primers for investors, AI project technical assessment training, AI comprehension workshops for non-technical audiences, AI investment analysis framework workshops

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format**: Concept explanation + case analysis, assessment framework workshops, interactive discussion
- **Core Value**: Technical understanding, assessment capability, investment judgment, due diligence skills

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: AI investors — VCs who need to understand technology to make investment decisions
- **Secondary Audience**: Investment analysts — analysts responsible for technical analysis of AI projects
- **Tertiary Audience**: Corporate strategic investors — enterprise investors evaluating AI projects
- **Extended Audience**: Non-technical founders — founders who want to understand technology for better communication
- **Audience Behavior**: Understanding technical concepts, building assessment frameworks, improving judgment capability

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Investor Core**: VCs + analysts, suited for technical due diligence workshops
- **Strategic Investment Background**: Corporate strategic investors + technical consultants, suited for assessment framework workshops
- **Mixed Background**: Investors + non-technical founders, suited for concept comprehension workshops

## Content Elements
- Workshop positioning (investment perspective)
- Technical concept explanation (simplified but accurate)
- Assessment framework introduction
- Case analysis workshops
- Question checklists and evaluation criteria
- Compliance disclaimers

## Language Approach
- Explain technology for non-technical audiences
- Frame from an investment perspective
- Simplified but accurate
- Framework- and methodology-oriented
- Must include NFA (Not Financial Advice) disclaimer

## Structure Strategy
- Opening information block: 15%
- Concept comprehension block: 30%
- Assessment workshop block: 35%
- Application scenario block: 10%
- Compliance and action block: 10%
- Emoji density control: 8–10 total

## Emotional Tone
Investment perspective, technical understanding. A practical feel of "you don't need to write code, but you need to ask the right questions."

## Key Considerations

### Investment Workshop Essentials
1. **Investment perspective is the core**: This is not about teaching technology — it's about teaching investment judgment
2. **Simplified but accurate**: Don't mislead for the sake of simplification
3. **NFA disclaimer is mandatory**: Does not constitute investment advice
4. **Hands-on content must be sufficient**: Case analysis, simulated due diligence
5. **Deliverables must be practical**: Question checklists, assessment frameworks
