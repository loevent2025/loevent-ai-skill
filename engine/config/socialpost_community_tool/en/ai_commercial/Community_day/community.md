# AI Cafe – Community Events Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Private cafe space + Community member cowork + Open socializing |
| Duration | Half day to full day |
| Participants | Community Builders, AI enthusiasts, community newcomers |
| Core Objective | Community bonding, working side by side, meeting frens |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides the standard style:**

✅ Community feel, friends feel, everyday feel
✅ Emphasize "our cafe"
✅ Like the community's living room

❌ No official organizer tone
❌ Avoid making it feel too much like an event
❌ Do not emphasize community value

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Focus |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| P0 | Community members | Seeing frens, working together |
| P1 | Community newcomers | Fitting in, meeting people |
| P2 | AI enthusiasts | Getting to know the community, making friends |

---

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Community Belonging (required)
- "Anyone in the community is welcome"
- "Our cafe day"

### 2. Cowork Atmosphere (required)
- The feeling of working alongside each other
- Example: "Everyone's doing their own thing, but doing it together"

### 3. Organic Socializing (required)
- Nothing forced, it just happens naturally
- Example: "Chat if you want, zone in if you don't"

### 4. Low-Key Participation (required)
- Come and go as you please
- Example: "Stay all day or just swing by for a coffee"
