## Execution Guidelines

### Priority Sequence
1. **Bilateral co-hosts** — Confirm simultaneously, 2–3 months before event
2. **Cross-industry sector selection** — During initial event planning
3. **Bilateral speaker invitations** — 1–2 months before event
4. **Bilateral communications launch** — 2–3 weeks before event

### Special Considerations for Cross-Industry Collaboration
- **Bilateral balance**: Benefits, content, and communications must be equitable between AI and industry sides
- **Language translation**: Help both sides understand each other's terminology and objectives
- **Expectation management**: Clarify that the goal is "exploratory collaboration" rather than "immediate deals"
- **Neutral facilitation**: Organizers must remain neutral between both sides
- **Post-event follow-up**: Establish clear mechanisms for tracking bilateral partnership intent
- **Case study documentation**: Cross-industry outcomes should be co-branded and promoted by both sides

### Common Issues & Contingency Plans

| Issue | Contingency Plan |
|-------|-----------------|
| Imbalanced registration between sides | Phased recruitment; targeted invitations to supplement the weaker side |
| Significant knowledge gap | Strengthen icebreaker activities; assign cross-industry facilitators |
| One side dominating | Moderator intervention to balance; enforce bilateral speaking opportunities |
| Partnership intent falls through | Lower expectations; emphasize long-term relationship building |
