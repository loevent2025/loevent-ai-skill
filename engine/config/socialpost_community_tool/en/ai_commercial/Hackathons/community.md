# AI Hackathon – Community Events Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply this module when the event theme involves the following keywords:
- AI Community Hackathon, Builder Jam, AI Maker Weekend
- Open-Source AI Contribution Challenge, Community Co-Build Hackathon
- AI Creative Marathon, Prompt Creation Contest

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Online/offline development + Community voting + Showcase & discussion + Celebration party |
| Duration | 24–72 hours, relaxed atmosphere |
| Participants | AI Builders, creators, open-source contributors, AI enthusiasts |
| Core Objective | Community engagement, ecosystem building, Builder connections, creative expression |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Focus |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| P0 | AI Builders | Hands-on project work, community recognition, collaboration opportunities |
| P1 | Open-source contributors | Ecosystem contributions, technical incentives, visibility |
| P2 | AI creative enthusiasts | Creative expression, community interaction, prizes and perks |
| P3 | Community newcomers | Getting involved, community integration, learning opportunities |

---

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| AI Builder Co-Build Challenge | Energetic & Engaging (B) | Community atmosphere, co-building spirit |
| Open-Source Contribution Hackathon | Energetic & Engaging (B) | Open-source spirit, community participation |
| AI Creative / Prompt Contest | Value-Driven (D) | Fun participation, prizes and incentives |
| AI Tool Innovation Challenge | Curiosity-Driven (C) | Technical contributions, ecosystem value |

---

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Community Atmosphere (at least 2 instances)
- "Build alongside [number]+ AI Builders"
- "[Community Name]'s biggest co-build event of the year"

### 2. Participation Incentives (at least 1 instance)
- "Community votes decide the most popular project"
- "All participants receive an exclusive commemorative badge"

### 3. Ecosystem Value (at least 1 instance)
- "Top projects earn a spot on the official recommendation list"
- "Receive official support from model providers"

### 4. Community Spirit (recommended)
- "Let's Build AI together!" / "AI Builder, Unite!"

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Over-commercializing the community event
- ❌ Compromising the fairness of community voting
- ❌ Overemphasizing prizes at the expense of the co-building spirit
- ❌ Exaggerating AI product capabilities
