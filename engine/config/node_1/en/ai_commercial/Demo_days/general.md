# AI Commercial · Demo Day Knowledge Base

## Section 1: Scenario Definition and Characteristics

### Scenario Overview
Demo Days are the core format for project showcasing and investor matchmaking in the AI commercial sector. They give AI startups a platform to present their work, provide investors with a structured deal-sourcing channel, and serve as a key event format for accelerators, incubators, and corporate innovation teams.

### Core Characteristics
| Characteristic | Description |
|---|---|
| Scale | 50–500 attendees; primarily investors, enterprises, and presenting teams |
| Duration | Half-day to 1 day; compact and high-efficiency format |
| Format | Project pitches + judge feedback + one-on-one matchmaking + networking reception |
| Positioning | Fundraising matchmaking, project visibility, investment discovery, innovation showcase |

### Typical Event Formats
- AI accelerator graduation Demo Days (Y Combinator AI Day, a16z AI Demo Day)
- Corporate innovation showcases
- Vertical-track AI Demo Days (AI Agent Demo Day, AI Coding Startup Pitch)
- VC portfolio AI days
- Hackathon final-round pitches

## Section 5: Key Success Factors

### Project Curation
- **Technical differentiation**: Core technology, model capabilities, data advantages
- **Team background**: Technical team quality, industry experience, execution track record
- **Market potential**: Market size, growth trajectory, competitive landscape
- **Commercial validation**: Revenue / user metrics, client case studies, product-market fit signals

### Pitch Preparation
- **Content structure**: Problem → solution → market → team → vision
- **Data presentation**: Key metrics, growth curves, benchmark comparisons
- **Product demo**: Live product demonstration, technical capability showcase
- **Time management**: 3–8 minute pitch; prepared Q&A

### Investor Recruitment
- **Precision matching**: Match investor profiles to project stage and track
- **Decision-maker level**: Prioritize partners and investment directors
- **Active funds**: Funds with an active AI investment mandate
- **Strategic investors**: Relevant corporate CVCs and strategic investment arms

### Matchmaking Mechanics
- **Booking system**: Investor-to-team one-on-one scheduling
- **Information access**: Deck shared in advance; data room prepared
- **Meeting space**: Private meeting rooms; quiet conversation zones
- **Post-event support**: Contact exchange facilitation, follow-up coordination

## Section 6: Key Considerations

### Project Protection
- Execute NDAs and protect competitive information
- Control distribution of materials
- Respect each team's fundraising timeline and information disclosure boundaries

### Investor Experience
- Maintain consistent project quality; avoid significant variance across pitches
- Provide adequate time for one-on-one meetings
- Efficient scheduling and disciplined time management

### Information Integrity
- Verify accuracy of all project metrics before the event
- Avoid over-packaging or exaggerated claims
- Establish a feedback and credibility tracking mechanism

### Conflicts of Interest
- Transparent project selection criteria
- Disclose affiliated party relationships
- Implement judge recusal procedures where applicable
