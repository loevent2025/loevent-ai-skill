# Web3 B2B Conference – Education Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply this module when the event theme involves the following keywords:
- Corporate training workshops, internal training program meetings, talent strategy sessions
- University partnership discussions, industry-academia collaboration meetings, curriculum development sessions
- Certification program workshops, training institution partnerships, education resource networking

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Invitation-only workshop + Program review + Partnership discussion + Resource networking |
| Content Depth | Training system design, curriculum development, certification standards, talent strategy |
| Participants | Corporate HR/Training leaders, University representatives, Education institutions, Project team HR |
| Core Objective | Establish training partnerships, deploy curriculum frameworks, build talent pipelines |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Concerns |
|----------|---------------|--------------|
| P0 | Corporate Training Leaders | Team upskilling, internal training programs, cost-effectiveness |
| P1 | Universities/Research Institutions | Curriculum partnerships, internship programs, industry-academia projects |
| P2 | Education & Training Institutions | Course resources, certification partnerships, enrollment channels |
| P3 | Project Team HR | Talent acquisition, employer branding, campus recruitment partnerships |

---

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| Corporate Training Workshop | Formal & Professional (A) | Program evaluation, outcome validation |
| University Partnership Meeting | Formal & Professional (A) | Academic collaboration, resource exchange |
| Talent Strategy Session | Suspense-Driven (C) | Recruitment pain points, solution-oriented |
| Training Resource Networking | Benefits-Oriented (D) | Partnership opportunities, resource access |

## ❓ FAQ Templates

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| What are the participation requirements? | Corporate training/HR leaders or equivalent decision-making roles |
| Can training institutions attend? | This session is exclusively for corporate attendees; a separate session is available for institutions |
| Will materials be shared afterward? | Yes — meeting notes and program templates will be distributed |
| Can I bring a team member? | In principle, each company is limited to one representative |

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Allowing the session to become a sales pitch for training institutions
- ❌ Exaggerating training outcomes or making employment guarantees
- ❌ Disclosing any company's internal training budget information
- ❌ Publicly sharing the list of participating companies
