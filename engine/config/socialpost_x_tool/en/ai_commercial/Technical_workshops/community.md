# Technical Workshop × Community (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
Open-source contributor workshops, LLM framework training, community co-learning workshops, AI tool usage tutorials, ecosystem project hands-on training, community onboarding workshops

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format**: Community teaching, co-learning & mutual support, contributor training, hands-on practice
- **Core Value**: Community skill-sharing, contributor development, ecosystem familiarization, community connection

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Community members—developers who want to learn community projects
- **Secondary Audience**: Potential contributors—people who want to start contributing to projects
- **Tertiary Audience**: New members—people who have recently joined and want to get familiar
- **Extended Audience**: Ecosystem developers—teams building within the ecosystem
- **Audience Behavior**: Learning community projects, preparing to contribute, understanding the ecosystem, building connections

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Contributor Training**: Primarily potential contributors; suitable for contribution onboarding workshops
- **User Learning**: Primarily community users; suitable for usage tips & techniques workshops
- **Co-Learning & Mutual Support**: Members at all levels; suitable for co-learning group formats

## Content Elements
- Community identity call-out
- Workshop theme (community/ecosystem-oriented)
- Learning content (project-related)
- Community support (mentors/teaching assistants from the community)
- Contribution guidance (how to participate)
- Community entry point

## Language Treatment
- Community-style conversational tone
- "We together" sense of belonging
- Contribution-oriented phrasing
- Technically passionate yet friendly
- Welcoming to newcomers

## Structural Strategy
- Opening call-out block: 15%
- Learning content block: 40%
- Community support block: 25%
- Contribution guidance block: 10%
- Call-to-action block: 10%
- Emoji density control: 12–16 total

## Emotional Atmosphere
Community belonging, co-learning & mutual support. A warm feeling of "learning together, growing together."

## Key Considerations

### Community Workshop Essentials
1. **Community belonging is the core**: Emphasize "we" and "our community"
2. **Contribution guidance**: Not just learning—include a clear contribution path
3. **Community members as mentors**: Let the community teach the community
4. **Long-term connection**: Provide community entry points after the workshop
5. **Co-learning atmosphere**: Mutual support, not one-way teaching
