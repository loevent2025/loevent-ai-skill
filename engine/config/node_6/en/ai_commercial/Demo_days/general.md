# AI Industry Demo Day – Marketing Strategy

## Event Overview

A Demo Day is a pitch and showcase event for AI projects, where early-stage AI startup teams present their products and technology to investors, industry users, and the broader community. Typically held as an accelerator graduation event, hackathon finale, or standalone project showcase, Demo Day is a key opportunity for AI projects to gain visibility and access resources.

## Core Objectives

- Create exposure opportunities for AI project teams
- Facilitate investor-project matchmaking
- Showcase AI technology implementation outcomes
- Identify high-potential AI startup teams
- Build connections across the AI venture ecosystem

## Distribution Strategy

- Primary tags: #[EventName]DemoDay #AIPitchShowcase
- Project-specific tags: Individual hashtags per project
- Engagement tags: #MostPromisingAIProject #AIStartupToWatch

### Content Strategy

| Phase | Content Type | Posting Frequency |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| Warm-Up | Event announcements, project previews, judge reveals | 1 post/day |
| Build-Up | Individual project spotlights, evaluation criteria, technical highlights | 1–2 posts/day |
| Event Day | Live pitch updates, product demos, judge commentary | Real-time updates |
| Post-Event | Project roundup, investment updates, in-depth coverage | 1 post/day |

### Project Showcase Strategy
- Standardized asset templates for all participating teams
- 60-second product demo video per project

- Technical architecture overview

## KOL Partnership Strategy

### KOL Types

| Type | Profile | Engagement Format |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| AI Investors | VC partners focused on the AI sector | Judge / Commentary panelist |
| AI Founders | Founders of successful AI companies | Judge / Experience sharing |
| Technical Experts | Recognized authorities in AI technology | Technical reviewer |
| Industry Observers | Tech journalists, analysts | Commentary / Media coverage |

### Engagement Formats
- **Judge Invitation:** Provide professional evaluation during pitches
- **Commentary Participation:** On-site or remote commentary segments
- **Investor Matchmaking:** Post-event one-on-one project introductions
- **Media Coverage:** In-depth project reporting and features
