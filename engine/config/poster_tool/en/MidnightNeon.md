# Futuristic Neon Tech / Ethereum Event Style

## Applicable Scenarios
Tech conferences, academic symposiums, research-oriented content, blockchain tech summits, developer meetups, innovation showcases, enterprise tech presentations, forward-looking educational events

## Signature Color Palette
- **Primary:** Electric Cyan (#00E5FF), Deep Violet-Blue (#5B4FFF), Neon Pink (#FF6B9D)
- **Secondary:** Sunset Orange (#FF9E80), Mint Green (#00FFA3)
- **Background:** Deep Navy Black (#0A1628)
- **Accents:** Pure White (#FFFFFF), Glowing Cyan-White (#E0FFFF)
- **Characteristics:** Clean lines, geometric iconography, minimalist futurism, high-contrast neon palette

## Color Scheme
- **Rendering approach:** Smooth gradients transitioning from warm tones (pink/orange) to cool tones (cyan/violet), high saturation

## Visual Elements
- **Line art:** Clean vector dual-line technique (2–3px main line + 1px glowing edge), fluid curves
- **Architecture:** Flowing organic curves + geometric precision, glass curtain walls, suspended circular structures, arched doorways, open-plan layouts
- **Decor:** Tropical palm trees, central glowing logo (Ethereum), connecting cables, geometric exhibition platforms, tech screens
- **Figures:** 15–20 simplified silhouettes, 6–8% of frame height, flat color fill, casual-professional postures

## Effects & Treatment
- **Neon glow:** 25px outer glow at 80% opacity
- **Bloom:** 50px Gaussian blur bloom applied to bright elements
- **Lighting:** Vertical light beams (25% opacity), ground reflections (20% opacity), edge lighting (1–2px)
- **Depth of field:** Progressive desaturation (100% → 60%), atmospheric perspective, three distinct depth layers

## Composition Strategy
- **Perspective:** One-point perspective, wide-angle (28mm feel), horizon at 42% height, 16:9 ratio
- **Focal point:** Central subject element at golden ratio position, visual lines converging to center
- **Balance:** Architectural symmetry, asymmetric decoration, 30% negative space

## Typography Settings
- **Color selection:** Choose solid colors with high contrast against dark backgrounds
  - Recommended on deep navy black: Pure White (#FFFFFF), Electric Cyan (#00E5FF)
  - Alternative solid colors: Neon Pink (#FF6B9D), Mint Green (#00FFA3)
- **Fill method:** Solid color fill, single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No neon glow
  - No outer/inner glow
  - No bloom effect
  - No edge lighting on text
  - No drop shadow / projection shadow
  - No gradient fill
  - No any text special effects
- **Overall principle:** Keep text clean, sharp, and highly legible — rely on font choice and high-contrast solid colors for visual impact, forming a clear contrast against the neon-lit background elements

## Mood & Atmosphere
Refined futurism, optimistic innovation, premium tech experience, community warmth, tropical-tech fusion, inspirational yet approachable, combining academic rigor with visual appeal

## Art References
Moebius line style, Syd Mead futurism, refined 80s cyberpunk, vaporwave gradients, Makoto Shinkai lighting

## Technical Specifications
8K resolution (7680×4320), 300 DPI, vector graphics, RGB color mode, flat color with gradient overlays, no texture/grain, glow effects using overlay blend mode
