# KEYBOARD INFO-CARD — 3D Keycap Information Card Style

---

## Use Cases

Brand event key visuals, design summit posters, product launch KVs, tech event promotions, developer conference posters, co-branded event visuals, creative workshop promotions, hackathon key visuals, tech forum banners, offline meetup/salon event posters

---

## Color Scheme

**Primary Palette:** Multi-colored keycaps — each keycap features its own bold, distinctive color, creating a "color array" effect

**Keycap Color Strategy:** Each keycap uses a different color family with strong contrast between them, while maintaining overall harmony. A typical combination is 3–4 keycaps corresponding to brand color, information color, and ambient color respectively

**Background:** Clean, light background (white, light gray-white, very pale warm gray) to maximize the visual impact of the 3D keycap cluster

**Glow Accent:** Very pale cyan-blue, cool white, light ice-blue, etc. — used for subtle ambient rendering behind the keycaps. High-saturation pinks, magentas, or other intense glow colors are strictly prohibited

**Saturation:** High saturation on keycap bodies, very low saturation on background — creating strong subject-background separation

---

## Common Color Combinations (Design Reference Only — Not for Direct Use in Prompts)

- **Brand Launch:** Brand red + silver gray + pure white + deep black | Very pale cool white glow
- **Tech Summit:** Electric blue + charcoal black + ivory white + amber orange | Cool white glow
- **Designer Event:** Coral red + mint green + cream white + graphite black | Very pale cyan-blue glow
- **Developer Competition:** Bright orange + indigo blue + light gray-white + dark gray | Cyan-blue glow
- **Co-branded Event:** Each keycap uses one partner's brand color; glow uses a transitional blend between both brand colors

## Core Visual Elements

### 3D Information Keycap Cluster (Primary Element)

The defining feature of this style is **3–4 three-dimensional mechanical keycaps** arranged in a tight cluster, with each keycap carrying different key event information:

- **Date Keycap** — Keycap surface engraved with event date (e.g., "JUNE 19", "7.26", "MAR 15")
- **Time Keycap** — Keycap surface engraved with event time slot (e.g., "18:00 - 20:30", "14:00 - 17:00")
- **Brand/Theme Keycap** — Keycap surface engraved with organizer logo, event logo, or core theme icon
- **Keyword Keycap** — Keycap surface engraved with a core keyword (e.g., "AI", "WEB3", "DESIGN")

**Information Assignment Rules:** Each keycap carries only one type of information (date OR time OR brand OR keyword). Information must be distilled to its most essential form to ensure readability on the keycap surface. Placing two types of information on a single keycap is strictly prohibited. Multiple keycaps displaying identical content is strictly prohibited. If event information does not fill all keycaps, remaining keycaps may feature minimalist, theme-related icons or keywords, but the content must differ from other keycaps.

### Keycap Cluster Arrangement

- **Grid/Diamond Array:** 3–4 keycaps arranged tightly in a 2×2 or diamond layout, slightly rotated at various angles, with partial occlusion to create depth
- **Each keycap tilted at a slightly different angle:** Not rigidly aligned — each one has a subtle angular variation to add dynamism
- **Keycaps closely spaced but not overlapping:** Maintaining the spacing feel of a mechanical keyboard while forming a compact visual unit as a whole

---

## Effect Treatments

- **3D Rendering:** Keycaps display realistic 3D volume with clearly defined top surfaces and side structures

- **Material Texture:** Keycap surfaces render with smooth plastic/metallic finishes, with subtle reflections. Different colored keycaps can suggest different materials (silver gray = metallic, white = matte plastic, dark colors = matte rubber)

- **Lighting and Depth:** Keycaps feature soft shadows and highlights. Light source enters from an upper-rear angle, with subtle drop shadows beneath the keycaps

- **Subtle Backlight/Ambient Glow:** A very faint cool white or pale cyan-blue halo emanates from behind the keycap cluster, typically diffusing softly outward from the center or the uppermost keycap. Glow colors are restricted to very pale cyan-blue, cool white, and light ice-blue — low-saturation cool tones only. Intensity should be "barely perceptible" to avoid competing with the keycaps themselves for visual focus

- **Perspective Angle:** Slightly tilted 3/4 overhead view (approximately 30–45 degrees), allowing visibility of both the keycap top surfaces (information) and side structures (3D depth)

- **Rounded 3D Form:** Keycaps maintain the standard rounded-square shape of mechanical keycaps, with clearly defined thickness and 3D chamfered edges

- **Text/Icons on Keycaps:** Engraved into the keycap surface — may appear slightly recessed or embossed — contrasting with the keycap color

---

## Composition Strategy

- **Upper-Center Subject:** The 3D keycap cluster occupies the upper-center area of the canvas, taking up approximately 40–50% of the overall composition

- **Lower Title Area:** The bottom third of the canvas serves as a large-text title zone, using bold sans-serif type (English or Chinese), at a large scale for strong visual impact

- **Brand/Co-branding Identifiers:** Organizer logos or co-branding identifiers placed directly below the title (e.g., "Brand A × Brand B")

- **Upper-Left Subtitle:** A category tag or subtitle may be placed in the upper-left corner (e.g., "AI Generation", "Tech Summit") in small type, functioning as a contextual anchor

- **Generous Whitespace:** Background remains clean. Ample breathing room between the keycap cluster and title area, as well as around all edges

- **Visual Flow:** Upper-left subtitle → center keycap cluster (core info: date, time, brand) → lower title → bottom brand identifiers. Clear information hierarchy — all key information scannable at a glance

## Typography

**Main Title:**
- Extra-large bold sans-serif typeface
- Dark color (near-black dark gray), creating strong contrast against the light background
- May be arranged across multiple lines, one keyword/phrase per line, left-aligned or centered
- Solid color fill, no effects of any kind

**Subtitle/Category Tag:**
- Small type, light gray or medium gray
- Sans-serif typeface, commonly in all-caps English
- Positioned in the upper-left corner or above the title

**Text on Keycaps:**
- High contrast with keycap color (light text on dark keycaps, dark text on light keycaps)
- Sans-serif bold or monospace typeface
- Concise information: dates in abbreviated form (JUN 19), times in numerals (18:00-20:30)
- Text size proportional to keycap surface area, maintaining readability
- Each keycap displays only one piece of information — no mixing

**Brand Identifiers:**
- Positioned at the bottom of the canvas, in small type
- Co-branded events use "Brand A × Brand B" format
- Dark gray or brand color

**Prohibited Effects (for bottom text area):**
- No outer glow / inner glow
- No text stroke
- No text shadow / drop shadow
- No gradient fill
- No outline glow
- No text effects of any kind

## Mood and Atmosphere

Brand-forward, futuristic tech, creative energy, professional quality, diverse collision. The overall mood targets the intersection of creativity and technology — using the multi-colored 3D keycap arrangement to convey collaboration, brand synergy, and diverse perspectives. Each keycap functions as an "information card," condensing core event details (when, where, who's hosting, what topic) into a visual, symbolic format across a few keycaps. The subtle cool-toned backlight adds restrained technological sophistication. The overall look is clean and sharp, with the quality feel of a premium brand launch.

### Mandatory Rules

1. **The first sentence of every prompt must be fixed as:** "Render the graphic as a flat image, surface graphic design, pattern design. Full bleed, no external borders, no canvas/paper texture/mockups." This cannot be modified or omitted.

2. **Hex color values are strictly prohibited in prompts** (e.g., #FF0000, #212121, #F5F5F5). Gemini will render these as visible text on the keycap surface. All colors must be described using plain Chinese or English descriptors (e.g., "vibrant royal blue", "deep charcoal black", "very pale gray-white").

3. **Each keycap must be described in its own separate paragraph — merging is prohibited.** Each keycap gets a dedicated paragraph containing only that keycap's position, color, material, and its single engraved content. Using words like "respectively" or "in sequence" to combine multiple keycaps into one paragraph is strictly forbidden.

4. **Each keycap carries only one piece of information, and no two keycaps may repeat information.** If there are four items — date, time, location, keyword — they must be distributed across four separate keycaps, with each keycap displaying only one.

5. **Use "keycap surface engraved with text" instead of vague phrasing like "printed with"** to help the model understand that text is part of the keycap rather than an independent element.

6. **No numbers or codes inside parentheses.** Avoid any parenthesis-wrapped numbers/codes in prompts — Gemini may render parenthesized content as visible text.

7. **Glow must use only very pale cool tones.** The backlight halo behind keycaps is restricted to very pale cyan-blue, cool white, and light ice-blue — low-saturation cool colors only. Pink, magenta, neon, and other high-saturation warm-toned glow effects are prohibited. Glow intensity should be described as "very faint" or "barely perceptible" — avoid terms like "soft" or "noticeable," which may result in overly strong rendering.

### Mandatory Keycap Description Format

Each keycap must strictly follow this template as an independent paragraph — no merging, no omissions, no structural reordering:

```
The Nth keycap is positioned [location], [color description] [material description]. The keycap surface is engraved only with the text "[unique content]", in [font color].
```

Examples:
```
The first keycap is positioned upper-left, in deep royal blue with a matte finish. The keycap surface is engraved only with the text "2026.05.09", in white monospace type.

The second keycap is positioned upper-right, in bright amber orange. The keycap surface is engraved only with the text "14:00", in white bold type.

The third keycap is positioned lower-left, in pure white with a matte finish. The keycap surface is engraved only with the text "北京", in dark gray sans-serif type.

The fourth keycap is positioned lower-right, in deep charcoal black with a matte finish. The keycap surface is engraved only with a minimalist AI-themed line icon, rendered in light gray.
```

### Prohibited Patterns (Negative Examples)

The following patterns will cause Gemini to render information redundantly across all keycaps and must be strictly avoided:

**Error Example 1 — Merged descriptions:**
> ❌ "The four keycaps are engraved respectively with '2026.05.09', '14:00', and '北京'"

**Error Example 2 — Information not assigned to specific keycaps:**
> ❌ "The keycaps are engraved with event date, time, and location information"

**Error Example 3 — Multiple pieces of information on a single keycap:**
> ❌ "The upper-left keycap is engraved with '2026.05.09 14:00 北京'"

**Error Example 4 — Hex color values used:**
> ❌ "Dark gray (#2D2D2D) matte finish"

## Sample Prompt (Co-Branded Event: 4-Keycap Cluster)

Render the graphic as a flat image, surface graphic design, pattern design. Full bleed, no external borders, no canvas/paper texture/mockups. Co-branded event key visual poster. A clean, light gray-white background fills the entire canvas. The background may include a very faint crosshair grid texture for added depth.

The primary element occupies the upper-center area: 4 three-dimensional mechanical keycaps arranged tightly in a diamond array. Each keycap is tilted at a slightly different angle. The keycaps sit closely together to form a compact visual unit. The entire cluster is rendered at approximately a 35-degree overhead perspective, showing both the keycap top surfaces and their side structures.

The first keycap is positioned upper-left, in silver gray with a metallic finish. The keycap surface is engraved only with the text "JUNE 19", in dark gray sans-serif bold type. No other text is displayed.

The second keycap is positioned upper-center, in vivid true red. The keycap surface is engraved only with the organizer's circular logo icon, rendered in white. This keycap serves as the visual focal point of the cluster and may be slightly larger than the others. No other text is displayed.

The third keycap is positioned on the right, in deep black with a matte finish. The keycap surface is engraved only with the text "18:00 - 20:30", in bright orange monospace type. No other text is displayed.

The fourth keycap is positioned at the bottom, in pure white with a matte finish. The keycap surface is engraved only with the partner's brand logo icon, rendered in indigo blue. No other text is displayed.

Important: The engraved content on these four keycaps is distinct — each keycap displays only its assigned piece of information. Repetition is strictly prohibited.

All keycaps feature a rounded-square shape with clearly defined 3D thickness and side structures. Surfaces are smooth with soft highlights and shadows. A very faint cool white halo with a slight cyan-blue tint emanates from behind the keycap cluster, diffusing softly outward from the center. The glow intensity is very low — barely perceptible — fading gradually until it blends into the background.

In the upper-left corner, small subtitle text reads "AI Generation", in medium gray sans-serif type.

The bottom third of the canvas is the title area: the main title is arranged in two lines — "Design" and "Reimagined" — in near-black extra-large bold sans-serif type, left-aligned or centered, occupying significant visual weight. Below the title, after appropriate spacing, the co-branding identifiers appear, each featuring the respective brand logos and names, in medium type, dark gray.

The overall composition is clearly divided top and bottom: the upper half features the 3D keycap cluster; the lower half features the large title and brand identifiers. Ample breathing room is maintained between the two sections. Title area text uses solid color fill, kept clean and crisp, with no text effects of any kind.

No text, numbers, or symbols appear anywhere on the canvas other than the text engraved on keycap surfaces and the bottom title area.
