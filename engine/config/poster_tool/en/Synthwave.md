# SYNTHWAVE Style Guide (Retro-Futurism)

## Applicable Scenarios
Web3 conference key visuals, hackathons, NFT drops, crypto art festivals, metaverse launches, retro gaming events, electronic music festivals

---

## Color Scheme

- **Primary tones:** Deep Purple (#7B2CBF), Blazing Magenta (#FF006E), Electric Blue (#00B4D8)
- **Background:** Gradient from deep purple to black (#1A0B2E to #000000)
- **Accent colors:** Neon Pink (#FF10F0), Cyan (#00FFFF), Sunset Orange (#FF6D00)
- **Saturation:** High saturation with glowing neon effects
- **Color behavior:** Gradient blending, neon halation, ambient color bleeding

### Common Color Combinations

| Style | Colors | Context |
|---|---|---|
| **Classic Synthwave** | Purple (#7B2CBF) + Magenta (#FF006E) + Cyan (#00FFFF) | On dark gradient background |
| **Sunset Drive** | Orange (#FF6D00) + Pink (#FF10F0) + Purple | With horizon glow |
| **Neon Night** | Electric Blue (#00B4D8) + Blazing Pink | On deep purple base |

---

## Visual Elements

- Grid horizon with perspective vanishing point
- Retro geometric sun/moon (typically semicircular)
- Palm tree silhouettes
- Wireframe mountains or pyramids
- Neon city skyline silhouette
- Chrome/metallic lettering with beveled edges
- Laser beams and light trails
- Star field or dotted sky patterns
- Retro computer graphics aesthetic
- Scanline overlay

---

## Effects & Treatment

- Neon outer glow (10–15px radius with color)
- Gradient mesh with smooth color transitions
- Chromatic aberration on key elements
- VHS tracking lines and noise
- Radial blur on sun/light source
- Ambient haze with purple/pink tones
- Metallic chrome reflections with rainbow gradient
- Bloom and lens flare
- Horizontal scanlines (1–2px spacing, 20% opacity)

---

## Composition Strategy

- Strong perspective with center vanishing point
- Grid floor extending to the horizon (typically at the lower third)
- Sun/moon centered on the horizon line
- Symmetrical composition with center focal point
- Layered depth: foreground grid, midground elements, background sky
- Silhouette elements against glowing background
- Text placement in the upper third or centered
- Vertical light beams or columns as compositional framing
- Retro aspect ratio (4:3 or 16:9 letterbox format)

---

## Typography Settings

- **Color selection:** Choose solid colors with high contrast against the dark background
  - Recommended: Cyan (#00FFFF), Pure White (#FFFFFF), Neon Pink (#FF10F0), Blazing Magenta (#FF006E)
- **Fill method:** Solid color fill, single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No outer/inner glow
  - No stroke/outline
  - No drop shadow or projection shadow
  - No metallic/chrome texture
  - No rainbow gradient reflection
  - No bevel/emboss
  - No text special effects of any kind
- **Overall principle:** Keep text clean, simple, and highly legible — forming clear contrast against the complex visual effects in the background

---

## Mood & Atmosphere

Nostalgic yet futuristic — the dreamlike optimism of 1980s sci-fi vision. The overall energy is vibrant, retro-cool, and euphoric, capturing the spirit of synthwave music: sunset drives, neon nights, and endless digital horizons that evoke both nostalgia and utopian technological dreams.
