# Dubai Venue Knowledge Base

## Cocktail/Social Venues

### 1. Zuma Dubai (DIFC)
- **Capacity**: 80-200 people
- **Suitable for**: High-end investor dinners, VC networking, founder cocktail parties, business networking

### 2. WHITE Dubai (Meydan)
- **Capacity**: 150-500 people
- **Suitable for**: Large industry after-parties, tech summit celebrations, brand launch parties

### 3. CÉ LA VI Dubai (Address Sky View)
- **Capacity**: 100-300 people
- **Suitable for**: Rooftop cocktail parties, high-end networking nights, industry socials, VIP receptions

### 4. Billionaire Mansion
- **Capacity**: 100-400 people

### 5. Iris Dubai (Oberoi)
- **Capacity**: 80-200 people
