# DEMO DAYS × INVESTMENT / Event Playbook

## Applicable Scenarios
Investor demo days, fundraising pitch events, VC showcase sessions, accelerator investor days, deal sourcing events, LP/GP project presentations

## Scenario Profile
- **Format:** Fundraising pitches, investor feedback panels, 1-on-1 meetings, networking
- **Core Value:** Investor-founder matching, investor feedback, deal flow generation, project visibility

## Audience Definition
- **Primary Audience:** Investors — discovering projects, evaluating opportunities, sourcing deal flow
- **Secondary Audience:** Founders — presenting their ventures, gathering feedback, seeking investment
- **Tertiary Audience:** Industry analysts — tracking early-stage projects
- **Extended Audience:** Potential partners — identifying collaboration opportunities
- **Audience Behavior:** Screening projects, connecting with founders, evaluating investment opportunities

### Common Audience Configurations
- **VC Focus:** Institutional investor-majority — suited for funding-driven Demo Days
- **Angel Focus:** Angel investors + founders — suited for early-stage projects

## Content Elements
- Demo Day positioning (funding stage)
- Pitching project details
- Investor / judge lineup
- Pitch format and structure
- 1-on-1 matching mechanism
- Compliance disclaimer (required)

## Language Guidelines
- Write from the investor's perspective; emphasize commercial potential
- Investor lineup is the primary value proposition
- Matching mechanism must be clearly explained
- NFA disclaimer is required

## Content Structure
- Opening info block: 15%
- Project overview block: 25%
- Investor lineup block: 25%
- Matching mechanism block: 20%
- Compliance + CTA block: 15%
- Emoji density: 8–10 total

## Tone & Atmosphere
Opportunity-focused, investor-connected. Deal-making energy.

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Fundraising Pitch
- **Use case:** Demo Days with a clear fundraising objective
- **Opening format:** 🚀💼 {Demo Day Name} | Pitch Directly to Investors
- **Tone:** Opportunity-driven, clear on matching outcomes

### Variant B: Deal Sourcing
- **Use case:** Investor-led project discovery sessions
- **Opening format:** 📊 {Demo Day Name} | Curated Projects, One Showcase
- **Tone:** Curation-focused, quality-assured

## Notes & Best Practices

### Key Principles for Investment Demo Days
1. **Compliance disclaimer is non-negotiable:** NFA, introductions ≠ commitments
2. **Investor lineup is the selling point:** Decision-makers, active, verifiable
3. **Matching mechanism must be clear:** How to book meetings, how to follow up
4. **Dual-sided value:** The program must be compelling for both investors and founders
