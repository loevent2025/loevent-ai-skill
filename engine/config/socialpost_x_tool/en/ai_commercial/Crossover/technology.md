**Narrative Formula**:
"When [Side A representing Tech Stack X] meets [Side B representing Tech Stack Y], how do you design the architecture?"

## Key Considerations

### Essentials for Technical Cross-Discipline Collaboration
1. **Technical collision is the core**: Emphasize the differences and fusion between two technical mindsets
2. **Dual-perspective sharing**: Speakers from both technical domains are a must
3. **Collision session design**: Panel dialogues, technical debates, live co-creation
4. **Concrete fusion solutions**: Don't stop at concepts — provide actionable directions for implementation
5. **Perspective breakthroughs must be clear**: Attendees should walk away thinking "so that's how it can work"
6. **Avoid one-sided framing**: This isn't one side teaching the other — it's mutual learning
