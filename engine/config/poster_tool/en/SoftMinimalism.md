# SOFT LIGHT MINIMALISM — Visual Style Guide
## (High-Saturation Light Gradient Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
Luxury beauty brands, premium goods marketing, meditation / wellness apps, minimalist brands, photography galleries, boutique hotels, wedding and event invitations, literary publishing, art book covers, environmental and nature brands

## Color Palette
- **Primary tones:** High-saturation bright pastel palette — Cherry Blossom Pink (#FFB3D9), Lavender Purple (#C9A0FF), Sky Blue (#A0D4FF), Peach Orange (#FFCBA0)
- **Background:** High-saturation light multi-color gradient — flowing transition from vivid pink (#FFB8D0) to bright violet (#D4A0FF) to clear blue (#A0E0FF)
- **Glow highlights:** Warm pink highlight (#FFE0F0), cool violet highlight (#F0E0FF) — high transparency with soft, diffused edges
- **Image treatment:** Nature photography (mountains, ocean, sky) processed with a color-shift filter blended into the high-saturation color palette
- **Saturation:** Medium-high (50–75%) — aiming for a vivid, bright "candy-toned" quality that remains refined rather than harsh
- **Color behavior:** Colors are clearly distinguishable with well-defined tonal layers; gradient transitions are smooth and natural

### Recommended Color Combinations
- Candy Dawn: Cherry Blossom Pink (#FFB3D9) → Lavender Purple (#C9A0FF) → Sky Blue (#A0D4FF)
- Peach Dusk: Peach Orange (#FFCBA0) → Rose Pink (#FF9EC4) → Lilac (#D9A0FF)
- Dream Aurora: Mint Cyan (#A0FFE0) → Sky Blue (#A0D4FF) → Lavender (#C4A0FF)

## Visual Elements
- Nature landscape photography (mountains, ocean, desert, sky) processed with a high-saturation color-toning filter
- Minimalist geometric shapes — typically circles, arcs, or simple letterforms
- Soft blending of photography into the gradient background with no defined boundary
- Colorful lens flare effects in pink, violet, and blue tones
- Double exposure or overlay effects that intensify the color palette
- Generous negative space (gradient-toned open areas)
- Subtly visible secondary elements (semi-transparent geometric shapes or type)
- Images may be cropped or shown as partial compositions

## Post-Processing & Effects
- Overall image maintains moderate contrast; saturation is enhanced and toned toward pink-violet-blue hues
- Image edges use wide feathering to blend seamlessly into the gradient background (80–150px)
- A high-saturation color overlay layer unified with the background palette is applied to imagery
- Circular highlights simulate colorful lens flare — carrying subtle pink, violet, and orange color tones
- Optional light Gaussian blur (3–8px) for added dreamlike softness
- Screen or Soft Light blend modes used for overlay effects
- Overall color temperature is clearly defined — warm (pink-orange), cool (blue-violet), or full-spectrum gradient
- Optional subtle grain / noise (opacity 3–8%)

## Composition Strategy
- Extreme restraint — the number of elements is kept to an absolute minimum
- Gradient negative space occupies 60–75% of the frame and is the compositional foundation
- Imagery is typically positioned to one side or corner of the frame rather than centered
- Text and imagery maintain a comfortable breathing distance between them
- Natural lines within the image are used to guide the viewer's eye
- A single large-scale element (such as an oversized letter) may serve as a compositional frame
- Symmetrical or deliberately asymmetrical — middle-ground compositions are avoided
- All elements serve the overarching tone of "elegant vibrancy"

## Typography Guidelines
- **Color usage:**
  - Use dark text against the light gradient background for contrast
  - Recommended: Deep Purple (#4A2060), Deep Rose (#6A2050), Deep Blue-Violet (#3A3060)
  - Pure white (#FFFFFF) may be used in specific cases over imagery or deeper gradient areas
- **Fill style:** Flat solid color fills only — no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No gradient fills
  - No drop shadows or inner shadows
  - No outer glow or inner glow
  - No stroke / outline
  - No blur effects
  - No text special effects of any kind
- **Type style:** Elegant serif or ultra-thin sans-serif typefaces, weight Ultra Light to Light — emphasizing refinement and delicacy
- **Overall principle:** Typography remains clean, sharp, and highly legible; visual hierarchy is established through typeface selection and high-contrast solid colors against the vivid gradient background

## Mood & Atmosphere
Fresh, vibrant, sweet, dreamy, and refined. The overall quality is "bright yet elegant" — vivid colors bring a sense of delight while the minimalist composition maintains a premium sensibility. Ideal for brands pursuing a tone of youthful energy, sweet romance, and contemporary artistic spirit. Evokes feelings of lightness, joy, and a longing for the beautiful life.
