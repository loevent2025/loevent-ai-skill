# AI Tech Workshop Knowledge Base – Education

## Scene Overview
Education-focused AI tech workshops are designed for working professionals, career changers, and students seeking a structured approach to AI learning. These programs emphasize systematic knowledge building and transferable skills. Through carefully designed curricula and extensive hands-on practice, participants build foundational AI competencies from the ground up — making this format a key entry point for AI education and career transition.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Typically 20–60 participants, optimized for learning outcomes
- **Duration**: 1–5 days, structured for comprehensive learning
- **Format**: Systematic instruction + step-by-step practice + assignments + assessments
- **Participants**: Early-career professionals, career changers, students, AI beginners
- **Key Traits**: Low barrier to entry, structured curriculum, progressive difficulty, certification-backed

## Curriculum Design Recommendations

### Curriculum Architecture
- Define clear, outcome-oriented learning objectives
- Use a modular structure for ease of comprehension
- Ensure coherent knowledge progression across modules
- Scale difficulty gradually and intentionally
- Design tangible deliverables for each learning phase

### Learning Support
- Staff sufficient TAs relative to participant count
- Establish a dedicated learner community or group
- Provide a comprehensive learning materials package
- Design integrated practice exercises and assignments
- Schedule dedicated office hours for Q&A

### Assessment & Evaluation
- Implement milestone-based assessments
- Provide substantive feedback on all assignments
- Use a capstone project as the final deliverable
- Issue certificates upon successful completion
- Offer re-audit opportunities for continued learning

### Ongoing Community Management
- Maintain an active and engaging alumni community
- Deliver updated content as the field evolves
- Host alumni events and networking opportunities
- Collect feedback continuously to improve the program
- Build and showcase a library of learner success stories
