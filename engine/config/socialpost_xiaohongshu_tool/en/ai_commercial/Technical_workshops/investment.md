# AI Tech Workshop Knowledge Base – Investment

## Scene Overview
Investment-focused AI tech workshops are designed for investors, entrepreneurs, and enterprise decision-makers who need to understand AI's technical capabilities and evaluate the feasibility of AI projects. These events do not require programming experience. Instead, they build technical literacy through hands-on product exposure, structured presentations, and case-based discussion — equipping participants with a technology-informed lens for investment decisions and business judgment.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Typically 20–50 participants, curated small-group format
- **Duration**: Half-day to 1 day
- **Format**: Technical briefing + product demonstration + case analysis + panel discussion
- **Participants**: Investors, founders, C-suite executives, strategic decision-makers
- **Key Traits**: Understanding-focused rather than hands-on, business perspective, decision-oriented

## Curriculum Design Recommendations

### Content Planning
- Explain technical concepts using business language and analogies
- Rely on case studies and comparisons — minimize technical jargon
- Focus on the technical knowledge most relevant to decision-making
- Deliver practical, usable evaluation frameworks
- Prepare a rich, diverse library of real-world case studies

### Audience Analysis
- Understand participants' backgrounds and objectives upfront
- Collect participant questions in advance
- Adjust content depth to match the audience's technical fluency
- Prepare cases relevant to the sectors they operate in
- Identify and address common knowledge gaps and blind spots

### Instructional Approach
- Use clear, accessible language — avoid insider jargon
- Favor interactive discussion over one-way delivery
- Ground case analysis in realistic, relevant scenarios
- Build in ample time for Q&A and peer exchange
- Provide decision-making tools and frameworks participants can keep

### Value Delivery
- Leave participants with a practical evaluation toolkit
- Establish a channel for ongoing advisory access
- Package and distribute key takeaways from the session
- Build a community channel for ongoing peer exchange
- Provide continued updates on industry trends and developments
