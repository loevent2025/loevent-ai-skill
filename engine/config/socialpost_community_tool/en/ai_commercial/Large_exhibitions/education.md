# AI Conference & Expo – Education Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply the rules in this module when the event theme involves the following keywords:
- AI education conference, AI talent development summit
- Prompt Engineering training week, LLM developer bootcamp
- AI product manager training, enterprise AI upskilling
- University AI partnerships, AI career development conference

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Main stage + track-based courses + hands-on workshops + certification assessment |
| Content Depth | Beginner to advanced — building a structured AI skill framework |
| Participants | Education institutions, AI company training teams, universities, enterprise HR |
| Core Objective | AI skill acquisition, career transition, talent development, certification |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| P0 | Traditional Developers Transitioning to AI | Learning path, transferable skills, getting hands-on |
| P1 | AI Product Managers / Operations | Product thinking, AI feature design, ROI measurement |
| P2 | Business-Side Explorers | AI-driven productivity, tool adoption, low barrier to entry |
| P3 | Enterprise Training Leaders | Team upskilling, internal training programs, certification frameworks |

---

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| Comprehensive AI Education Conference | Energetic & Engaging (B) | Learning atmosphere, growth path |
| Developer Skill Training | Suspense-driven (C) | Technical pain points, solutions |
| Career Development / Job Fair | Benefits-oriented (D) | Job opportunities, career pathways |
| University / Academic Partnership | Formal & Professional (A) | Academic value, industry-academia collaboration |

## ❓ FAQ Templates

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Can I attend with zero experience? | Absolutely — courses start from the basics and are designed for complete beginners |
| Do I need to know how to code? | Technical tracks require basic programming; product/operations tracks do not |

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Promising 100% employment rates or fabricating placement statistics
- ❌ Overstating course effectiveness or learning outcomes
- ❌ Using misleading language such as "guaranteed employment" or "high-salary assurance"
- ❌ Fabricating mentor credentials or false backgrounds
