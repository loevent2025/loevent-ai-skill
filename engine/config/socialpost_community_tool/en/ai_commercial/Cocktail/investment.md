# AI Cocktail Reception — Investment Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Relaxed social gathering + Open mingling + Intimate atmosphere |
| Duration | 3–4 hours |
| Participants | AI founders, investors, and the startup-curious |
| Core Objective | Low-key connections, casual startup conversations, organic introductions |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides the standard style:**

✅ Relaxed yet refined — intimate and tasteful
✅ Drop the corporate tone, keep the exclusivity
✅ Emphasize "the right people," not "useful people"

❌ No terms like "fundraising matchmaking" or "project roadshow"
❌ Do not turn the reception into a pitch session
❌ Do not highlight titles or firm names

---

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| P0 | AI Founders | Unwind, meet peers, serendipitous investor encounters |
| P1 | Investors | Casually learn about projects, meet founders |
| P2 | Aspiring Founders | Get a feel for the ecosystem, make friends |

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Scene-First Opening (required)
- Intimacy + Refinement + Relaxed atmosphere
- Example: "A private venue, floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the city, and easy conversation inside."

### 2. Sensory Immersion (minimum 3 of the following)
- Visual: City skyline at night, warm lighting, intimate space
- Taste: Whiskey, red wine, curated bites
- Sound: Jazz, low murmurs of conversation
- Atmosphere: Exclusive, intimate, relaxed, no pitching

### 3. People Description — Title-Free (required)
- ❌ "Sequoia partner, unicorn founder"
- ✅ "People doing interesting things in AI"

### 4. No-Pressure Participation (required)
- "This isn't a pitch night — it's just drinks."
- "Click with someone? Keep talking. Don't? Move on."

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Turning the reception into a pitch competition
- ❌ Over-emphasizing the investor lineup
- ❌ Using phrases like "once-in-a-lifetime" or "top-tier resources"
- ❌ Forcing structured networking or introductions
