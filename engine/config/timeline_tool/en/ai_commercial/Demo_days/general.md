# AI Tech Demo Day Knowledge Base

## Scenario Overview

A Demo Day is a professional roadshow event where AI projects showcase their products and technical capabilities to investors, corporate clients, and industry audiences. Typically hosted by accelerators, investment firms, or technology platforms, it is a crucial occasion for AI startups to secure funding and clients.

## Time Compression Strategies

### Relaxed Mode (30+ days)
- Ample project screening and training period
- Multiple rounds of roadshow rehearsals
- Detailed investor one-on-one arrangements
- Complete product demonstration preparation

### Compact Mode (15-29 days)
- Accelerate project screening pace
- Condense roadshow training into intensive sessions
- Streamline one-on-one matching process
- Process investor invitations in parallel

### Urgent Mode (8-14 days)
- Only screen highly mature projects
- Rapid roadshow training
- Focus on inviting core investors
- Simplify demonstration segments

### Extreme Mode (1-7 days)
- Small-scale private roadshow
- Targeted invitations to known investors
- Use existing demo materials
- Rapid matching mode

## Risk Warnings

- ⚠️ AI product demonstrations have high network and computing power requirements; multiple backup plans are needed
- ⚠️ Model response latency can affect roadshow performance; optimization is needed in advance
- ⚠️ Investor time is precious; strict control of roadshow duration is necessary
- ⚠️ Project information involves commercial sensitivity; confidentiality measures must be in place
