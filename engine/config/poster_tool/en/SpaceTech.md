## SPACE TECH Style

### Applicable Scenarios
Aerospace technology conferences, lunar exploration-themed events, tech company offline events, AI moonshot initiatives, space-themed exhibitions, hard-tech product launches, aerospace recruitment events, exploration-themed summits

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Tones:** Deep space black, space blue, metallic gray
- **Backgrounds:** Pure black or deep blue-black to evoke a sense of outer space
- **Accent Colors:** Gold / amber (lunar lander), bright blue (brand color), white
- **Saturation:** Overall low saturation; accent colors at medium saturation
- **Color Behavior:** Metallic textures and blue tech accents against a deep space backdrop

#### Common Color Combinations
- **Lunar Classic:** Pure black space + gold lunar lander + blue brand color
- **Deep Space Exploration:** Deep blue-black background + silver-white spacecraft + cyan data elements
- **Interstellar Mission:** Black background + metallic gray + bright blue highlights

---

### Visual Elements
- Lunar lander / spacecraft in realistic or semi-realistic rendering
- Binary code rain (vertical columns of 0s and 1s)
- Astronaut silhouettes or figures
- Moon / planetary elements
- Pixelated or digitized space scenes
- Blue wireframe selection boxes / UI elements
- Gold or amber circles (representing the moon or sun)
- Data readouts and coordinate display elements
- Scanlines or grid overlays

---

### Treatment Techniques
- Vertical flow effect for binary code streams
- Metallic texture rendering on spacecraft
- Pixelation treatment to enhance digital quality
- Glowing effect on blue wireframe elements
- Subtle star field across deep space background
- Pixel disintegration / digitization effect at the bottom
- Soft halo on gold elements
- Overall HUD (heads-up display) interface aesthetic

---

### Composition Strategy
- Spacecraft as the primary visual subject, positioned left-of-center or centered
- Binary code columns as background texture
- Blue wireframes to create a UI interface feel
- Gold circle as a compositional counterbalance
- Pixel disintegration effect at the bottom transitioning into the text zone
- Clean, minimal brand area at the top
- Multi-level information layout (main headline, date, venue)

---

### Text Styling

- **Color Selection:** Colors that contrast against the dark space background
  - Recommended primary: Pure white (`#FFFFFF`), bright blue (`#00A8FF`)
  - Secondary: Gold (`#FFD700`), light gray (`#CCCCCC`)
- **Fill Style:** Solid color fill only — single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited Effects:**
  - No gradient fill
  - No drop shadow / shadow
  - No outer glow / inner glow
  - No stroke / outline
  - No blur effects
  - No text effects of any kind
- **Typeface Style:** Modern sans-serif or monospace fonts, Regular or Medium weight
- **Overall Principle:** Text remains clean, sharp, and highly legible — establishing clear visual hierarchy against the space background through typeface selection and high-contrast solid color

---

### Mood & Atmosphere
Exploring the unknown, technological frontier, human achievement. The overall energy is solemn yet exhilarating — conveying awe and aspiration for space exploration, evoking humanity's ambition to conquer the cosmos. Carries the professional precision of a NASA mission control room combined with the romance of science fiction cinema.
