# AI Cafe — Technical Events Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Cafe takeover + Open coworking + Casual tech chat + Drop in anytime |
| Duration | Half-day to full-day (split into time slots) |
| Participants | AI developers, tech enthusiasts, official team members (if applicable) |
| Core Objective | Relaxed coworking, casual tech talk, developer connections, code and socialize |

---

## 🎨 Style Override Rules

**When this module is activated, it overrides the standard style:**

✅ Relaxed, natural, cafe atmosphere
✅ Emphasize "coding together" — not "learning together"
✅ Coworking energy, not event energy
✅ Come whenever, leave whenever

❌ No "takeaways," "talks," or "learnings"
❌ Avoid the structured feel of a formal event
❌ Don't emphasize agendas or scheduled segments

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Cafe Scene-Setting (mandatory)
- Specific cafe atmosphere description
- Example: "Sunlight, coffee, the sound of keyboards"

### 2. Coworking Feel (mandatory)
- Emphasize "coding together" — not "meeting together"
- Example: "Bring your laptop, grab a seat, start coding"

### 3. Flexible Participation (mandatory)
- Split time slots, drop in and out freely
- Example: "Stay the whole day or just swing by for a coffee"

### 4. Light-Touch Socializing (mandatory)
- Conversations that happen naturally
- Example: "When you need a break, just chat with the person next to you"

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Scheduling formal talks or presentations
- ❌ Forcing self-introductions
- ❌ Using words like "learnings," "growth," or "takeaways"
- ❌ Turning the coworking session into a meeting
