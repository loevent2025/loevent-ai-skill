# Developer Meetup × General (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
AI developer meetups, LLM application developer nights, AI Builder meetups, local AI developer exchanges, AI technical salons, weekend AI developer gatherings

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format**: Technical sharing + open networking, casual socializing, roundtable discussions, lightning talks
- **Core Value**: Developer socializing, technical exchange, industry intel, community belonging

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: AI developers—engineers actively building with AI
- **Secondary Audience**: AI product builders—product managers and founders building AI products
- **Tertiary Audience**: AI enthusiasts—people interested in AI development
- **Extended Audience**: Tech-curious professionals—technical people wanting to learn about AI development
- **Audience Behavior**: Meeting peers, exchanging experience, gathering intel, finding belonging

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Builder Core**: Developers + product builders, suited for technical exchange meetups
- **Open Exchange**: All types of AI practitioners, suited for general social meetups
- **Topic-Focused**: Developers in a specific area, suited for themed salons

## Content Elements
- Meetup theme / positioning
- Sharing sessions (lightweight)
- Open networking time (the main event)
- Venue and atmosphere description
- Participant profile
- Refreshments / drinks description

## Language Guidelines
- Casual, conversational tone
- Emphasize "chatting" over "lecturing"
- Builder culture atmosphere
- Keep technical terms in English
- Welcoming and belonging-oriented

## Structure Strategy
- Opening information block: 20%
- Event content block: 30%
- Atmosphere description block: 25%
- Participation details block: 15%
- Call-to-action block: 10%
- Emoji density control: 10-14 total, friendly and relaxed

## Emotional Tone
Casual socializing, builder belonging. The feeling of "finding your people."

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Technical Exchange
- **Use For**: Meetups centered on technical sharing
- **Opening Format**: 🔧 {City} AI Developer Meetup | {Topic}
- **Tone**: Technical, conversational, relaxed

### Variant B: Social-First
- **Use For**: Meetups centered on socializing
- **Opening Format**: 🍻 {City} AI Builder Night | Come meet your peers
- **Tone**: Social, relaxed, friendly

### Variant C: Themed Salon
- **Use For**: Deep-dive discussions on a specific topic
- **Opening Format**: 💬 {Topic} Salon | Let's talk about {Subject}
- **Tone**: Topical, discussion-driven, in-depth

## Key Considerations

### Developer Meetup Best Practices
1. **Casual atmosphere is everything**: This is not a formal conference
2. **Allocate enough social time**: Don't pack the agenda wall-to-wall
3. **Maximize flexibility**: Let participants choose what to talk about
4. **Venue must be comfortable**: An environment that's conducive to conversation
5. **Build a sense of belonging**: The feeling of "finding your people"
