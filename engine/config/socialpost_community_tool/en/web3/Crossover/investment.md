## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Contrast & Tension Building (mandatory)

**Formula**:
```
"What happens when [capital/team representing World X] meets [projects/perspectives representing World Y]?"
```

**Key Points**:
- Clearly highlight each project's crossover background (traditional experience + Web3 capabilities)
- Emphasize the diversity of the investor lineup (traditional VCs + crypto funds)
- Underscore the scarcity and investment value of this combination

---

### 2. Chemical Reaction Descriptions (mandatory)

**Dynamic Verbs**:
- ✅ Use: "collide," "converge," "catalyze," "spark," "reshape"
- ❌ Avoid: "cooperate," "combine," "partner"

**Convergence Formula**:
```
[Traditional Strength] × [Web3 Capability] = [Unique Investment Value]
```

**Key Points**:
- Describe what crossover teams can create that neither purely traditional nor purely Web3-native teams can
- Explain why this specific combination is worth investing in

---

### 3. Participant Collision Descriptions (mandatory)

**Investor Perspective Collision**:
```
"Here, traditional VCs are searching for their Web3 entry point, while crypto funds are looking for teams with real-world implementation capability"
```

**Project Team Background Collision**:
```
"Here, Web2 veterans bring industry resources into Web3, while crypto-natives bring on-chain expertise seeking to break beyond the ecosystem"
```

---

### 4. Scene & Atmosphere Building (choose at least 2)

**a) Concrete Scenes**
- Don't write: "Investors and project teams will have networking sessions"
- Do write: Describe the specific moment when investors from different backgrounds evaluate the same project from contrasting perspectives
- Convey the tension of two investment philosophies colliding in the same room

**b) Temporal Flow**
- Describe the moments of cognitive collision during the pitch sessions
- Show the investor mindset shift from unfamiliarity to understanding

**c) Dynamic Interaction**
- Don't write: "There will be a networking session after the pitches"
- Do write: Describe investors from different backgrounds competing to connect with the same project

**d) Emotional Energy**
- Demo Day: discovery, surprise, validation, competition
- Networking sessions: anticipation, excitement, breakthrough, connection
- Social dinner: convergence, flow, cross-ecosystem, continuity

---

### 5. Unique Investment Value Proposition (mandatory)

**Describe the investment value that only crossover projects can generate**:
- List the business scenarios that only this specific background combination can unlock
- Explain why both traditional capital and crypto capital should pay attention
- Emphasize the irreplaceability of crossover teams

---

### 6. Transformation Arc (recommended)

**Investor Cognitive Journey**:
- **Start**: Arrive with [curiosity about / skepticism toward the other ecosystem]
- **Process**: See [which projects], hear [which perspectives], recalibrate [which assumptions]
- **End**: Leave with [a new investment lens / fresh deal flow / cross-ecosystem connections]

---

### 7. Compliance Disclaimer (mandatory)

```
"⚠️ Project presentations do not constitute investment advice — all investment decisions require independent judgment"
```

## ✅ Crossover Investment Copy Quality Checklist

- [ ] **Contrast & tension**: Clearly articulates the traditional vs. Web3 background contrast (across both project teams and investors)
- [ ] **Chemical reaction**: Uses dynamic verbs like "collide," "converge," and "catalyze" to describe investment value
- [ ] **Participant collision**: Describes the encounter between investors and project teams from different backgrounds
- [ ] **Scene & atmosphere**: At least 2 atmosphere-building techniques applied
- [ ] **Unique value**: Lists investment value that only crossover combinations can generate
- [ ] **Investor journey**: Includes a narrative of cognitive collision and perspective recalibration
- [ ] **Emotional drive**: Contains a sense of discovery, surprise, and excitement from cross-ecosystem connection
- [ ] **Compliance disclaimer**: Investment risk notice included
- [ ] **Lingering resonance**: Hints at continued matching mechanisms beyond the event itself

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Dismissing either investment perspective ("Traditional VCs don't get Web3" / "Crypto funds only chase narratives")
- ❌ Framing the Demo Day as a guaranteed fundraising opportunity
- ❌ Promising project fundraising success rates or crossover premiums
- ❌ Disclosing undisclosed project fundraising details
- ❌ Inflating project valuations or overstating crossover background value
- ❌ Ignoring the crossover convergence value by writing generic pitch event copy
- ❌ Using passive terms like "cooperate" or "combine" instead of conveying collision energy
- ❌ Over-hyping the crossover concept without substantive project value to back it up
