# 80s Japanese City Pop Illustration Style Guide

## Core Style Definition
This is the aesthetic of **"an endless summer and an urban dream that never fades."** It fuses American pop art sensibilities with Japanese precision — carrying both the luminous clarity of California sunshine and the hazy allure of Tokyo nightlife. The imagery evokes a sense of abundance, carefree living, romance, and a touch of elegant solitude — the fantasies and aspirations of the Japanese bubble economy era, when the idea of the good life felt both vivid and just out of reach.

**Style positioning:** Nagai Hiroshi / Suzuki Eiji illustration aesthetics + cel-shading animation flat fills + pop art + retro-futurism

- **Artistic characteristics:** Minimalist geometric composition, high-contrast sharp shadows, clean vector linework, strong horizontal perspective
- **Texture & surface:** Halftone printing texture, lithograph print quality, vintage record cover printing effect, subtle film grain, chromatic aberration at edges
- **Color treatment:** Cel-shading flat fills with clearly defined color blocks and no complex gradients; bold contrast between high-saturation and pastel tones
- **Visual atmosphere:** Nostalgic romance, urban sophistication, seaside resort, neon nightscape, timeless summer

---

## Color Palette

**Primary tones:** High-saturation cool palette with bold pastel contrasts; cobalt blue meets neon

- **Background:** Gradient sky (sunset / dawn / night) or large-area flat color layering
- **Saturation:** Predominantly high-saturation; vivid, high-contrast color relationships
- **Color behavior:** Clearly defined color blocks with sharp edges; soft transitions are almost never used

### Classic Color Combinations

- **Classic City Pop:** Cobalt Blue (#0047AB) + Neon Pink (#FF10F0) + Sunset Orange (#FF6B35) + Turquoise (#40E0D0)
- **Sunset Boulevard:** Deep Violet (#8B00FF) + Magenta (#FF1493) + Coral Orange (#FF7F50) + Sky Blue (#87CEEB)
- **Neon Night:** Electric Blue (#7DF9FF) + Hot Pink (#FF69B4) + Lemon Yellow (#FFF44F) + Deep Indigo (#4B0082)
- **Miami Vibes:** Orchid Purple (#DA70D6) + Cyan Teal (#00CED1) + Peach (#FFDAB9) + Deep Sea Blue (#003366)
- **Warm Wood Tones:** Golden Brown (#D2691E) + Ivory Yellow (#FFF8DC) + Dark Brown (#4B3621) + Teal Green (#40E0D0)

## Visual Elements

- **Signature scenes:** Beachfront villas, poolside settings, modernist building interiors, urban skylines, coastal highways, rooftop observation decks

- **Theme-specific design principle:** Primary visual elements should be designed in reference to the event theme and organizer identity — using environment, architecture, and objects to suggest the theme rather than literal iconography. For example: music events can feature vintage speakers or vinyl turntables; tech events can use futurist architecture, glass curtain walls, or minimalist geometric patterns; community events can feature urban skylines or multi-windowed building facades. All elements must maintain the cel-shading flat fill aesthetic.

- **Signature objects:** Palm tree silhouettes, shimmering pool surfaces, American convertible sports cars, wooden lounge chairs, bamboo curtains / venetian blinds, oversized glass windows, modernist architecture, neon signage, reflective water surfaces

- **Compositional characteristics:**
  - Minimalist geometric composition emphasizing horizontal lines (horizon / building edges / floor lines)
  - Single-point perspective or strong sense of depth
  - Frame-within-frame composition (distant views seen through windows or porticos)
  - Clear layering of foreground, midground, and background

- **Light and shadow:**
  - Extremely high-contrast sharp shadows (simulating midday sunlight)
  - Pure black silhouettes for dramatic effect
  - Neon glow effects (night scenes) with defined edges
  - Parallel cast shadows with razor-sharp edges

- **Linework and texture:**
  - Clean vector linework with precise edges
  - Halftone dot pattern texture simulating vintage print
  - Repeated parallel lines for detailed surfaces (e.g., bamboo curtains, venetian blinds)

---

## Post-Processing & Effects

- **Cel-Shading:** Animation-style flat color blocks with no soft transitions; all color boundaries are crisp and defined
- **Halftone Pattern:** Simulates the dot-printing texture of 1980s record covers; typically applied to sky areas or large single-color regions
- **Lithograph Print:** Vintage poster printing effect with a subtly grainy color quality
- **Film Grain:** Light retro grain (opacity 5–15%) adds a sense of era authenticity
- **Chromatic Aberration at Edges:** *This is the defining soul of the City Pop aesthetic.* At high-contrast edges — especially where dark meets bright — add a subtle red-cyan or red-blue color separation to simulate vintage lens optics and misregistered print overlays, enhancing the retro optical quality
- **High Contrast:** Extreme contrast between light and dark; shadows use pure black (#000000); highlights retain full vibrancy
- **Clean Vector Edges:** All shapes have sharp, precise edges — clean as vector artwork

### Prohibited Effects
- No realistic light-and-shadow blending
- No soft blur or feathering
- No complex gradients (sky areas may use a simple two-color gradient only)
- No 3D depth or impasto brushwork
- No watercolor bleed effects

---

## Composition Strategy

- **Geometric Minimalism:** Simplify all forms to basic geometric shapes; prioritize design sensibility over realism
- **Horizontal Dominance:** The horizon line, building edges, and floor lines serve as the compositional framework
- **Strong Perspective:** Single-point perspective creates depth; the vanishing point is typically centered or at the golden ratio
- **Frame within Frame:** Architectural elements — windows, doorways, porticos — create internal frames that guide the eye toward the background
- **Symmetry and Balance:** Left-right symmetry or balanced visual weight creates stability and visual harmony
- **Clear Layering:** Foreground (silhouettes / objects) → Midground (primary scene) → Background (sky / mountains / sea) — distinct and well-defined
- **Use of Negative Space:** Large expanses of sky or water surface open up breathing room, bringing the primary subject into focus

---

## Typography Guidelines

- **Color usage:** High contrast; text should create a strong visual impact against the background
  - On dark backgrounds (black / deep blue): Neon Pink (#FF10F0), Electric Blue (#7DF9FF), Pure White (#FFFFFF), Lemon Yellow (#FFF44F)
  - On light backgrounds (sky / pool): Deep Indigo (#4B0082), Pure Black (#000000), Dark Magenta (#8B008B)

- **Type style:**
  - 1980s retro geometric typefaces (bold strokes, angular letterforms)
  - Heavy sans-serif display fonts
  - Futuristic condensed typefaces (for subheadlines and secondary information)

- **Weight:** Bold to Black — ensuring strong visual impact

- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No gradient fills
  - No drop shadows or inner shadows
  - No outer glow or inner glow
  - No stroke / outline
  - No blur effects
  - No text special effects of any kind

- **Placement:**
  - Top banner arrangement for primary headlines
  - Bottom information bar for dates and venue details
  - Integrated into the geometric composition, aligned with structural lines

- **Overall principle:** Typography functions as a visual element in its own right — carrying a strong 1980s retro-futurist sensibility — balancing legibility with decorative impact

## Mood & Atmosphere

**Core emotions:** Nostalgic romance, urban glamour dream, timeless summer, refined solitude

This aesthetic evokes the aspirations of Japan's bubble economy era — a longing for the Western affluent lifestyle: the leisurely afternoon at a beachfront villa, the hazy allure of neon-lit city nights, the freedom of a convertible racing along the coastline, the bittersweet romance of sitting alone by a pool. The imagery carries both the bright clarity of California sunlight and the refined detachment of Tokyo after dark. It communicates a world that is "beautiful but just out of reach" — nostalgic without being heavy, romantic with a trace of melancholy, sophisticated yet restrained.

**Applicable scenarios:** Music festival posters, technology brand events, Web3 community gatherings, retro-themed parties, cultural and art exhibitions, fashion launches — any occasion requiring a tone of "retro-futurism," "urban romance," or "refined taste."

## Example Prompt 1 — Indoor Pool Scene (Music Festival / Art Exhibition)

**Scene:** 80s Japanese City Pop illustration style, Nagai Hiroshi aesthetics, cel-shading flat art, vintage record cover texture.

**Composition:** Indoor poolside viewing scene with a strong single-point perspective. The foreground interior occupies the lower 40% of the frame; the mid-to-background view through large floor-to-ceiling windows or glass curtain walls reveals an outdoor seascape in the upper 60%, creating a frame-within-frame composition.

**Background and sky:** The outdoor scene visible through the glass — the upper portion features a bright turquoise sky (#40E0D0) dominating the top half of the view; a simple two-color gradient (cyan-to-light-blue) may be used. The distant horizon sits in the upper one-third of the frame; the sea renders in a deep cobalt blue (#0047AB) to deep sea blue (#003366) gradient. The coastline features simplified mountain silhouettes in pure black or deep violet-grey (#4B3F5C), flat-filled with clean paper-cut contours.

**Interior elements:** The foreground floor is rendered in golden bamboo (#D2691E) or striped wood, textured with evenly spaced parallel lines (1–2px each) for a geometric effect. The midground holds one to two wooden lounge chairs cel-shaded in layered golden brown (#CD853F) to dark brown (#4B3621), casting razor-sharp pure black shadows. A small side table or vase may be placed nearby, simplified to basic geometric forms. Palm fronds enter the frame from either side, rendered as pure black or deep green silhouettes with geometrically simplified fan-shaped leaves.

**Ceiling and architectural structure:** The upper portion of the frame may reveal interior ceiling details rendered as a series of parallel diagonal lines (simulating louvered ceilings or structural beams) in electric blue (#7DF9FF) or white against a dark background, forming a radiating geometric pattern that reinforces perspective depth. Structural columns or window frames are flat-filled in pure black or dark tones with sharp edges.

**Theme-specific elements (music / art theme):** A simplified vinyl record or vintage radio is placed on the small table beside the lounge chair, flat-filled in classic City Pop colors — black, gold, and red. Alternatively, minimal musical symbol silhouettes (treble clef, notes) appear on a distant wall or glass surface, outlined in neon pink (#FF10F0) or lemon yellow (#FFF44F) at 2–3px line weight. Optional: simplified sailboat silhouettes or geometric seagulls visible in the distance outside the window.

**Technical specifications:** Strict cel-shading throughout — no gradients except the sky. All shadows use pure black (#000000) with razor-sharp edges. Clean vector linework throughout. Apply halftone dot texture over sky and sea areas (opacity 20–30%) to simulate vintage printing. Add subtle film grain globally (opacity 8–12%). **Key effect:** Apply chromatic aberration at all high-contrast edges — particularly where black silhouettes meet bright backgrounds, along window frame edges, and at lounge chair contours — producing a subtle red-cyan color separation that simulates vintage optical lenses and misregistered print overlays, enhancing the 1980s retro quality. High saturation, high contrast aesthetic.

**Typography:** Primary headline in a 1980s bold geometric typeface at the top of the frame, flat-filled in neon pink (#FF10F0). Subtitle information (date / venue) at the bottom in pure white or electric blue, bold sans-serif.

**Overall atmosphere:** A leisurely afternoon, the refined solitude of a seaside retreat, retro romance, timeless summer — perfectly suited to the nostalgic elegance of music and arts events.

## Example Prompt 2 — Modernist Architecture Night Scene (Technology Brand / Web3 Event)

**Scene:** 80s Japanese City Pop illustration style, Suzuki Eiji aesthetic inspiration, cel-shading flat art, lithograph print texture, retro-futurism.

**Composition:** Urban night scene featuring a modernist building exterior in single-point perspective. The viewpoint is either looking up or at eye level; the building occupies slightly left or right of center, emphasizing the geometric beauty of vertical lines and glass curtain walls.

**Background sky:** A nighttime gradient sky — deep indigo (#4B0082) at the top transitioning to magenta (#FF1493) in the middle, then to deep violet (#8B00FF) at the horizon — evoking the glow of an urban neon night. Optional scattered star points (small white or cyan dots) or halftone dot texture (opacity 25%) enhance the vintage print quality. The distant urban skyline is rendered in pure black or deep blue silhouette, simplified to stepped geometric forms.

**Primary building:** The foreground subject is a high-rise modernist building or glass curtain wall office tower, built from minimal geometric forms — cubes and rectangular solids combined. The facade uses a cyan-to-electric-blue (#00CED1 to #7DF9FF) glass curtain wall effect, divided into windows by a regular grid of lines (1–2px, dark blue or black). Select windows glow with warm orange (#FF7F50) or lemon yellow (#FFF44F) light in regular or random patterns, suggesting interior activity and life. Shadow faces of the building are pure black flat fills — extreme contrast against the lit faces. The building top may optionally feature a simplified antenna or geometric decorative structure.

**Foreground elements:** The lower portion of the frame may include a simplified street, sidewalk, or plaza in dark tones (dark grey #2F4F4F or black). Optional one to two palm tree silhouettes in pure black along the frame edges add a signature City Pop element. Alternatively, a minimal vintage sports car silhouette parked at the curb — body in a single vivid color (hot pink #FF69B4 or cyan) flat fill, with white or pale blue color blocks representing window reflections.

**Theme-specific elements (tech / Web3 theme):** Convey technological identity through architecture and environment — avoid literal symbols. The building's futurist design itself communicates the tech identity. The curtain wall grid pattern can evoke "network / connectivity" — the lit window pattern may form a pixel-like or data-stream visual effect. The building side or top may carry a minimal neon sign displaying a brand name or DAO name, outlined in neon pink (#FF10F0) or electric blue lines (3–4px), with a subtle outer glow (10–15px radius). Optional: simplified geometric neon advertisements on distant buildings (circles, triangles, hexagons) suggesting decentralized / blockchain geometric aesthetics. Building glass reflects the night sky or neighboring building light using simple color blocks.

**Water reflection (optional):** If the composition includes a foreground water surface (fountain or reflecting pool), render it in deep blue (#003366) flat fill; the reflection simplifies to a mirrored building silhouette, slightly darker in tone with sharp edges; optional small white dots suggest surface shimmer.

**Technical specifications:** Pure cel-shading — no soft gradients except the sky. Neon lighting uses outer glow effects while maintaining relatively sharp edges (not soft blur). All shadows pure black with sharp edges. Clean vector linework throughout. Halftone dot texture applied to sky (opacity 20%). Global film grain (opacity 10%). **Key effect:** Apply chromatic aberration at all high-contrast edges — building outlines against the night sky, neon tube edges, vehicle silhouette edges, curtain wall grid lines — producing a subtle red-blue color separation that simulates misregistered vintage printing and optical lens chromatic aberration; this is the signature detail that defines the City Pop aesthetic. High saturation, high contrast — the neon urban night realized.

**Typography:** Primary headline in a 1980s bold futuristic typeface at the top or upper-center of the frame, flat-filled in electric blue (#7DF9FF) or neon pink (#FF10F0). Event information in pure white or pale cyan, modern sans-serif, placed at the bottom.

**Overall atmosphere:** Urban technological sophistication, the refined solitude of neon nightscapes, retro-futurism, the collision of modern and nostalgic — perfectly suited to technology brands and Web3 community events, communicating the balance between innovation and human sensibility.

## Example Prompt 3 — Coastal Highway at Sunset (DAO Community / Social Event)

**Scene:** 80s Japanese City Pop illustration style, hybrid Nagai Hiroshi and Suzuki Eiji aesthetics, cel-shading flat art, vintage record cover texture, lithograph printing effect.

**Composition:** Coastal highway sunset scene in strong single-point perspective, viewed from road level. The highway extends from the foreground to a distant vanishing point, creating deep spatial recession. The horizon (sea level) sits just above the midline of the frame, dividing the image into two major color zones: sky above and sea / land below.

**Background sky and ocean:** The sky occupies 50–55% of the frame, rendered in a sunset gradient — deep violet (#8B00FF) at the top transitioning to magenta (#FF1493), then coral orange (#FF7F50), with peach (#FFDAB9) to lemon yellow (#FFF3B0) near the horizon. The horizon is precisely horizontal. The sea below uses a deep cobalt blue (#0047AB) to turquoise (#40E0D0) gradient to convey water depth. The sea surface may optionally feature minimal shimmer effects — white or pale blue dots or short strokes in regular distribution. One to two simplified sailboat silhouettes on the horizon (triangular sails and slender hulls) in deep purple or black flat fill.

**Highway and land:** The lower 40–45% of the frame is land and road. The highway extends from the bottom-center toward the vanishing point in dark grey (#4B4B4B) or black flat fill. Yellow or white dashed centerlines (evenly spaced short segments) mark the lane and reinforce the perspective. The road shoulders or sandy verges flanking the highway use ivory (#FFF8DC) or beige flat fills. Clean, straight road edges reinforce the geometric quality.

**Signature elements — palm trees and vehicle:** Three to five palm trees line both sides of the highway in pure black or deep green silhouettes. Trunks are slender and straight; crowns are geometrically simplified fan shapes; trees decrease in height toward the vanishing point to reinforce perspective depth. Palm trees cast long, sharp shadows across the road or sand surface (in deeper grey or black). Optionally, a vintage convertible sports car in the lower-left or lower-right foreground — body in a vivid single color (hot pink #FF69B4 or electric blue #7DF9FF) cel-shading flat fill, dark window areas, simple highlight blocks (white or pale blue) for body reflection, pure black shadow. The vehicle faces the horizon, echoing the perspective lines.

**Theme-specific elements (DAO community / social theme):** Suggest community through multiple environmental elements rather than literal symbols. Multiple palm trees symbolize diverse members growing together; the highway symbolizes a shared direction and collective journey. Optional: alongside sailboats on the horizon, add two to three simplified building silhouettes or a lighthouse at varying heights (rectangular and cylindrical geometric forms), symbolizing community diversity and decentralized nodes. These structures may be rendered as dark silhouettes or outlined in neon color edges (cyan or pink, 2px lines), suggesting connectivity and vitality. Roadside signage or billboards may display a DAO or community name / logo simplified to geometric forms, outlined in neon colors.

**Light and shadow:** Sunset directional light illuminates the scene — all surfaces facing the light use warm tones (orange, yellow); shadowed faces and cast shadows use pure black or deep purple. The contrast is extreme. Shadows from palm trees, the vehicle, and signage all extend in the same direction (away from the sun), with razor-sharp shadow edges and no feathering.

**Technical specifications:** Strict cel-shading throughout — sky and ocean may use simple two- or three-color gradients; all other areas use flat solid fills with no soft transitions. Clean vector linework throughout. Halftone dot texture applied to sky (opacity 25–30%) and ocean (opacity 15%). Global film grain overlay (opacity 8–10%). **Key effect:** Apply chromatic aberration at all high-contrast edges — palm tree silhouettes against the sunset sky, vehicle contour edges, road edge lines, distant building silhouette edges — producing a subtle red-cyan color separation most visible at strong dark-to-bright contrasts. This perfectly recreates the retro quality of 1980s printing and optical lenses, and is the subtle physical detail that gives a poster its true City Pop soul. High-saturation color, extreme light-dark contrast.

**Typography:** Primary headline in a 1980s retro bold typeface in the upper sky area of the frame, flat-filled in lemon yellow (#FFF44F) or neon pink (#FF10F0). Subtitles and event information in pure white or electric blue, along the bottom or arranged following the perspective lines of the highway — modern bold sans-serif.

**Overall atmosphere:** The freedom of the open road, moving forward together, diversity and inclusion, the warm romance of a sunset and its promise of limitless possibility — perfectly suited to DAO community gatherings, social events, and team-building occasions. Communicates the uplifting spirit of "driving toward a better future together," balancing nostalgia with optimism.
