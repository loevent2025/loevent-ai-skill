# NEON TROPICAL NIGHTLIFE — Visual Style Guide

## Applicable Scenarios

Nightclub and bar event promotions, cocktail party invitations, music festival campaigns, summer beach parties, DJ nights, electronic music events, tropical resort marketing, fashion brand nightlife activations, bachelorette/bachelor parties, New Year's Eve countdown events.

---

## Color Palette

- **Primary tones:** High-saturation neon series — Electric Purple (`#9B30FF`), Neon Pink (`#FF1493`), Cyber Blue (`#00D4FF`), Tropical Teal (`#00FFC8`)
- **Background:** Deep purple gradient transitioning vertically from Deep Void Purple (`#1A0033`) → Neon Purple (`#6B238E`) → Rose Magenta (`#99004C`)
- **Glow accents:** Pinkish White (`#FFE0F0`) and Cyan Blue (`#00FFFF`) at medium opacity with soft luminous edges
- **Silhouette tones:** Pure Black (`#000000`) or Deep Purple (`#1A0033`) for palm tree and tropical foliage outlines
- **Saturation:** High (70–100%) — pursue bold, vivid neon-party visual impact
- **Color behavior:** High contrast between light and dark; neon glow effects create dramatic tension against dark backgrounds

### Recommended Color Combinations

| Name | Palette |
|---|---|
| Miami Nights | Deep Purple `#1A0033` → Electric Purple `#9B30FF` → Neon Pink `#FF1493` |

| Tropical Sunset | Deep Magenta `#330022` → Rose Red `#FF0066` → Coral Pink `#FF6B9D` |

## Visual Elements

- Palm tree silhouettes as signature tropical motifs, typically flanking both sides of the composition
- Central focal object: martini glass / champagne flute / cocktail glass with a neon gradient fill
- Circular orbs / bubble elements simulating a disco ball or dreamlike floating spheres
- Vertical light-streak textures evoking rainfall or stage lighting projections
- Star flares / sparkle accents in varying sizes to enhance the dazzling party atmosphere
- Gradient-filled liquid in the glass, flowing from pink → purple → blue
- Layered tropical foliage silhouettes along the bottom edge to add spatial depth

---

## Effects & Rendering

- Apply vertical stripe texture overlay on the background at 10–20% opacity
- Add soft neon glow to key elements (e.g., the cocktail glass) — 10–20px feather radius
- Render circular orbs with a glassy texture, including specular highlight points
- Star flare effects use 4–6 ray spikes in white or pale pink
- Palm tree silhouettes remain pure black or deep-toned with no glow applied
- Use **Overlay** or **Screen** blend modes to intensify the neon luminosity
- Overall tone leans warm purple-pink or cool cyan-blue — maintain a unified neon atmosphere
- Optional subtle bloom blur (5–15px) to amplify the hypnotic nightclub mood

---

## Composition Strategy

- Center-symmetric layout with the main element (cocktail glass) anchored at the center
- Palm tree silhouettes frame both sides, forming a natural border
- Circular orb positioned above the glass to guide the viewer's eye upward
- Typography arranged in layers: primary headline centered in the upper area, secondary info at the bottom
- Clearly defined foreground, midground, and background depth
- Reserve the bottom section for event details and supporting information
- Prioritize vertical (portrait) format — optimized for social media Stories and poster layouts
- Every element serves the unified "hot tropical party" atmosphere

---

## Typography

**Color selection:**
- Use bright text against the dark gradient background for strong contrast
- Recommended: Neon Pink (`#FF1493`), Pure White (`#FFFFFF`), Bright Violet (`#CC66FF`)
- Secondary info: Light Pink (`#FFB6C1`) or Lavender (`#DDA0DD`) for bottom-section detail text

**Fill method:** Solid flat fill — single color only, no gradients

**Prohibited effects:**
- No gradient fills
- No drop shadows or cast shadows
- No outer glow / inner glow
- No strokes or outlines
- No blur effects
- No text special effects of any kind

**Typeface style:** Mixed pairing — bold sans-serif for high-impact keywords (e.g., "PARTY"), elegant script/handwriting for atmospheric words (e.g., "Cocktail"); font weights from Bold to Black to convey party energy

**Overall principle:** Keep all text clean, sharp, and highly legible. Establish visual hierarchy through size contrast and vivid solid colors against the dark background — primary versus secondary information should be instantly distinguishable.

## Mood & Atmosphere

Passionate. Euphoric. Sensual. Dreamy. Electric. The overall tone channels a **tropical nightclub** party vibe — neon colors deliver excitement, while tropical elements add an exotic flair. Ideal for nightlife brands targeting fashion-forward, young, and celebration-driven audiences. Evokes the desire for summer night parties, a pleasant buzz, and uninhibited freedom.
