# AI Hackathon — General Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply this module when the event is a comprehensive AI hackathon covering multiple tracks/themes:
- Comprehensive AI hackathons, Multi-track AI development competitions
- AI innovation marathons, Annual AI developer challenges
- Multi-organizer joint AI hackathons

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Multi-track parallel competition + Expert judging + Investor connections + Demo Day |
| Duration | 48–72 hours, or 2–4 week online competition period |
| Participants | Various AI developers, Model providers, Investment firms, Corporate sponsors |
| Core Objective | Ecosystem building, Talent discovery, Project incubation, Brand exposure |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| P0 | AI Application Developers | Technical challenges, Prize pool, Project incubation |
| P1 | AI Startup Teams | MVP development, Funding opportunities, Market validation |
| P2 | Students / Junior Developers | Hands-on learning, Résumé builder, Industry entry point |
| P3 | Enterprise Technical Teams | Innovation exploration, Talent recruitment, Ecosystem partnerships |

---

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| Large-Scale Hackathon | Formal & Professional (A) | Scale and impact, Multi-dimensional value |
| Annual Developer Challenge | Energetic & Engaging (B) | Broad participation, Community atmosphere |
| High-Prize Competition | Value-Driven (D) | Rich resources, Participation incentives |
| Innovation Exploration | Suspense-Driven (C) | Direction exploration, Creative inspiration |

---

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Scale and Impact (at least 2 instances)
- "Substantial prize pool across [number of tracks] major tracks"
- "[Number]+ teams, [number]+ developers participating"

### 2. Multi-Dimensional Resources (at least 2 instances)
- "[Number]+ model providers offering technical support"
- "[Number]+ investment firms judging / observing"

### 3. Incubation Pipeline (at least 1 instance)
- "Winning projects gain entry to [incubator/accelerator]"

## ❓ FAQ Templates

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Can I enter multiple tracks? | Each team can only select one track |
| Do I have to use a designated model? | Recommended to use partner models, but not required |
| Is Demo Day attendance mandatory? | Strongly recommended for finalists; remote participation can be requested |
| How do I get my API credits? | Credits are issued to your designated account after successful registration |

---

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Exaggerating AI product capabilities or results
- ❌ Promising confirmed investment or incubation outcomes
- ❌ Plagiarizing or reusing existing project code
- ❌ Misrepresenting sponsors or investment firms
