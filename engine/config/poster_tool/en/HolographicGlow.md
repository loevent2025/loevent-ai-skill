# HOLOGRAPHIC IRIDESCENT STYLE

## Use Cases
NFT drops, DAO governance proposals, product launches, artist collaborations, streetwear / hype community events, futurist brand campaigns, limited-edition releases, metaverse activations

---

## Color Palette

- **Primary tones:** Iridescent spectrum, pearlescent shifting hues
- **Background:** Deep black, deep purple, or gradient holographic surfaces
- **Saturation:** Variable — shifts from soft pastel to neon depending on viewing angle
- **Color behavior:** Chromatic aberration, rainbow gradients, multi-directional color shifting

### Recommended Color Combinations

- **Classic Holographic:** Cyan (#00FFFF) → Magenta (#FF00FF) → Yellow (#FFFF00) gradient with Green (#00FF00) accents
- **Pearlescent:** Soft Pink (#FFB3E6) → Lavender (#E0BBE4) → Mint Green (#B4F8C8) → Peach (#FFA07A)
- **Neon Iridescent:** Electric Blue (#0D7EFF) → Hot Pink (#FF006E) → Lime Green (#CCFF00) → Purple (#9D4EDD)
- **Chrome Dream:** Silver White (#F0F0F0) → Ice Blue (#C4E7FF) → Rose Gold (#F4C2C2) → Lilac (#D4BBFF)

## Compositional Strategy

- Central focal point with radiating holographic effects
- Layered translucent elements to create depth
- Background gradient from deep dark to glowing
- Typography or logo with holographic treatment
- Floating iridescent geometric surfaces
- Dreamy atmospheric bokeh background
- Light source effects generating rainbow refraction
- Symmetrical or centered compositions for visual balance
- Contrast between matte black and glossy holographic elements

---

## Typography

- **Color:** Choose solid colors that create high contrast against dark backgrounds
  - Deep black / deep purple backgrounds → Recommended: Pure White (#FFFFFF), Cyan (#00FFFF), Hot Pink (#FF00FF)
  - Alternative solid colors: Lime Green (#CCFF00), Electric Blue (#0D7EFF)
- **Fill method:** Solid color only, single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No metallic chrome finish
  - No liquid chrome effects
  - No rainbow gradients
  - No prismatic effects
  - No chromatic aberration / RGB separation
  - No edge glow
  - No iridescent color shifting
  - No text special effects of any kind
- **Core principle:** Typography must remain clean, sharp, and highly legible. Visual impact is achieved through font choice and scale — creating a clear contrast against the holographic background effects
