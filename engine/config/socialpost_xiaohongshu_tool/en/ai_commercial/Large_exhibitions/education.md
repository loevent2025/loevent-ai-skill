# AI Large-Scale Conference Knowledge Base — Education Scene

## Scene Overview
Education-focused AI events center on systematic knowledge transfer and skill development, targeting working professionals, career transitioners, and students who want a structured approach to learning AI. These events emphasize knowledge comprehensiveness, progressive learning, and transferable skills. They serve as critical pathways for entering, advancing in, and transitioning into the AI field.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Tens to hundreds of participants
- **Duration**: Half-day to multi-day programs
- **Format**: Structured curriculum + hands-on training + case study analysis + assignments and assessment + certificate issuance
- **Participants**: Working professionals, career transitioners, students, and corporate L&D stakeholders
- **Defining Traits**: Systematic content, progressive pacing, strong emphasis on practice

## Participant Guidance

### Before the Program
- Define your personal learning goals
- Review the curriculum and difficulty level
- Block time in your schedule for study
- Approach the program with an open, growth mindset
- Optionally review foundational concepts in advance

### During the Program
- Complete every assignment thoughtfully
- Participate actively in class discussions
- Ask questions immediately — don't let gaps accumulate
- Engage and collaborate with fellow cohort members
- Take good notes and review regularly

### After the Program
- Apply what you've learned in your work right away
- Keep practicing to reinforce your new skills
- Stay in touch with your cohort
- Keep up with industry news and developments
- Plan your next learning milestone
