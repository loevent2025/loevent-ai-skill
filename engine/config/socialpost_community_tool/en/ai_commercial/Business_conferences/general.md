# AI B2B Conference — General Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply this module when the event is a comprehensive AI B2B conference covering multiple topics:
- AI industry executive sessions, quarterly strategy meetings
- AI ecosystem partner conferences, multi-party networking events
- AI annual review conferences, AI industry trend discussions

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Themed discussions + Panel discussions + Breakout sessions + Business dinner |
| Content Scope | Industry trends, Strategic planning, Partnership development, Resource alignment |
| Participants | AI company founders/executives, Investors, Industry experts |
| Core Objective | Information exchange, Strategic alignment, Partnership development, Resource integration |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| P0 | AI Company Founders/CEOs | Strategic direction, Industry trends, Core resources |

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| AI Industry Executive Session | Formal & Professional (A) | In-depth discussion, Strategic insights |
| Partner Conference | Formal & Professional (A) | Partnership development, Resource alignment |
| Trend Discussion | Suspense-Driven (C) | Industry outlook, Strategic direction |

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Professional Positioning (at least 1 instance)
- "An invitation-only executive session for AI company founders and C-suite leaders"
- "In-depth dialogue among AI industry core decision-makers"

### 2. Agenda Value (at least 2 instances)
- "Exploring [year/phase] AI industry trends and strategic direction"
- "Sharing hands-on experience and decision-making insights from leading AI companies"

### 3. Networking Value (at least 1 instance)
- "Facilitating key business partnerships"
- "High-quality AI industry peer network"

### 4. Practical Disclaimer (required)
- "💡 All viewpoints shared are for informational purposes only and do not constitute investment advice. Market assessments should be conducted independently."

## ❓ FAQ Templates

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| What are the participation requirements? | AI company founders, CEOs, co-founders, or equivalent decision-makers |
| Is the session confidential? | We follow the Chatham House Rule — no external sharing of discussions |
| Can I bring my co-founder? | In principle, one seat per company. Special requests may be considered |
| Do I need to register separately for dinner? | All attendees automatically receive a dinner seat |

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Publicly sharing the attendee list
- ❌ Disclosing strategic information from executive sessions
- ❌ Turning the session into a fundraising pitch
- ❌ Excessive self-promotion of your own business
