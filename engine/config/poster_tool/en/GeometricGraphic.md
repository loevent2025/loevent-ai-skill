# BUILDER NIGHTS / COMMUNITY STYLE

## Use Cases
User community events, educational tutorial posters, crypto wallet promotional campaigns

---

## Signature Color Palette

- **Primary:** Deep Brick Red (#5C2817), Vivid Orange (#FF6B35), Warm Coral (#FFB195)
- **Secondary:** Soft Cream (#F5F1E8), Light Blue-Gray (#D4E4F7)
- **Accents:** Pure White (#FFFFFF), Medium Brown (#8B4513)
- **Color behavior:** Smooth orange-to-coral gradients; high-contrast warm tones against cool backgrounds
- **Distinguishing qualities:** Cartoonish geometry, faceted low-poly texture, community warmth

---

## Visual Elements

### Typography System

- **Headline typography:** Extra Bold / Black geometric sans-serif (Montserrat Black, Lexend Exa Bold, Space Grotesk Bold)
- **Font size hierarchy:** Oversized headlines at 120–200pt, occupying 60–70% of the canvas
- **Body text:** 24–40pt Regular / Medium weight, used sparingly
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned or centered; wide letter-spacing (3–8%)
- **Color:** Deep Brick Red (#5C2817) solid fill

### Geometric Shape Elements

- **Abstract shapes:** Cartoonish, faceted low-poly style geometric forms
- **Polygon count:** 30–80 triangular faces for balanced detail
- **Shape colors:** Orange-to-coral gradient fill (#FF6B35 to #FFB195)
- **Edge treatment:** Sharp angular edges, no rounded corners
- **Shadow / depth:** Subtle darker tones on downward-facing polygon surfaces (10–15% darker)
- **Size:** Decorative elements occupy 25–40% of canvas height

### Photo Treatment

- **Frame style:** Rounded rectangle (15–20px radius)
- **Arrangement:** 2×2 or 3-column grid on the right side
- **Photo treatment:** Natural color, no filters; clean professional headshots
- **Text overlay:** Name and title displayed below each photo in Brick Red text
- **Border:** Optional 2px Brick Red border

---

## Visual Treatment

### Color Gradients

- **Direction:** Diagonal or vertical, top to bottom
- **Range:** Light Coral (#FFB195) to Deep Orange (#FF6B35)
- **Application:** Geometric shape fills; occasionally used as background accents
- **Smoothness:** 3–5 color stops for smooth transitions

### Texture & Finish

- **Texture:** Completely flat; no grain or noise
- **Finish:** Matte appearance, no gloss effects
- **Shadows:** Minimal — only within low-poly facets (5–10% opacity)
- **Highlights:** None; maintain pure flat design aesthetic

### Spatial Treatment

- **Background:** Single solid color (light blue-gray or cream), no gradients
- **Negative space:** 15–25% open space; generous margins (8–10% on all sides)
- **Depth cues:** Expressed only through low-poly faceting — no drop shadows or 3D effects
- **Layering:** Flat Z-axis; minimal element overlap

---

## Compositional Strategy

### Layout Structure

- **Grid system:** 12-column grid for precise alignment
- **Golden ratio:** Primary headline placed vertically at the 62% mark
- **Visual weight:** 60–70% headline, 30–40% graphic / photo elements
- **Balance:** Asymmetric — heavier text weight on the left, lighter graphic elements on the right

### Element Placement

- **Top section (0–15%):** Event details (venue, date) in small type
- **Hero section (15–75%):** Oversized bold headline dominates
- **Mid section (30–60%):** Optional photo grid or geometric decoration
- **Bottom section (75–100%):** Additional or secondary information
- **Margins:** Consistent 8–10% padding on all sides

### Visual Flow

- **Reading order:** Upper-left to lower-right, Z-pattern
- **Focal point:** Headline first, then supporting graphics
- **Eye movement:** Large type commands attention; geometric shapes provide visual interest
- **Hierarchy:** Size-based (largest = most important)

---

## Typography

- **Color:** Choose solid colors that create high contrast against light backgrounds
  - Light Blue-Gray background (#D4E4F7) → Recommended: Deep Brick Red (#5C2817)
  - Soft Cream background (#F5F1E8) → Recommended: Deep Brick Red (#5C2817)
  - Dark areas → Recommended: Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- **Fill method:** Solid color only, single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No horizontal cut lines
  - No letter segmentation effects
  - No outer glow / inner glow
  - No stroke / outline
  - No drop shadows
  - No gradient fills
  - No orange highlights
  - No text special effects of any kind
- **Core principle:** Typography must remain clean, friendly, and highly legible. Visual impact is achieved through extra-bold weight and oversized scale — consistent with the flat design aesthetic

## Technical Specifications

- **Resolution:** Minimum 4K (3840×2160); 300 DPI for print
- **Color space:** Digital RGB; convert to CMYK for print
- **File format:** Vector typography (AI, SVG); raster for photos
- **Typography:** Web-safe fonts or embedded fonts for consistency
- **Export layers:** Separate text, graphics, and background layers for editability
