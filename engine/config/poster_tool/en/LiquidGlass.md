# TON ECOSYSTEM / WEB3 TECH VISUAL STYLE

## Applicable Scenarios
Web3 product presentations, cross-chain collaboration projects, developer ecosystem visual content

## Signature Color System
- **Primary:** Deep Black (#000000) to Bright Blue (#0088CC) gradient
- **3D Elements:** Cool White (#F0F4F8), Light Blue (#A8C5DD), frosted glass semi-transparency
- **Typography:** Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent Colors:** Electric Blue (#0098FF), Soft Cyan (#B8E0F6)
- **Characteristics:** Clean flat layout, calm digital aesthetic, 3D glass material quality

## Visual Elements

### Strictly Prohibited Visual Elements:
- ❌ **Natural scenes:** Grass, fields, sky, clouds, trees, plants, green vegetation
- ❌ **Outdoor environments:** Horizon lines, landscapes, mountains, ocean, beaches, ground surfaces
- ❌ **Physical materials:** Paper texture, fabric, wood, stone, concrete
- ❌ **Real-world objects:** Physical product mockups, product photography, desktop scenes
- ❌ **Decorative borders:** Picture frames, art frames, borders, ornamental edges

### Typography System
- **Typeface:** Extra-bold, rounded geometric sans-serif (SF Pro Display, Inter, Manrope Bold)
- **Size:** Mid-scale headlines at 48–72pt, occupying 30–40% of the canvas
- **Color:** Pure White on dark gradient background
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned or centered, generous line height (1.1–1.2)
- **Spacing:** Clear letter spacing, minimal body text

### 3D Geometric Elements
- **Style:** Frosted glass geometric shapes (rounded triangles, cylinders, TON logo coin elements)
- **Material:** Semi-transparent with inner blue glow, smooth reflective surfaces
- **Lighting:** Soft ambient light with subtle highlights and shadows
- **Texture:** Glass-like quality with refraction effects
- **Scale:** Occupying 40–50% of canvas, positioned in the lower half or right side

## Effect Processing

### Gradient Treatment
- **Background gradient:** Vertical gradient from Black (#000000) at the top to Bright Blue (#0088CC) at the bottom
- **Transition:** Smooth 3–5 color stops, transition zone centered — no visible banding, no hard edges, no color stepping; add subtle dithering to eliminate banding artifacts
- **Alternative:** Diagonal gradient from upper-left to lower-right

### 3D Material Effects
- **Glass shader:** 70–85% transparency with frosted effect
- **Inner glow:** Soft blue light emanating from inside the object
- **Reflections:** Subtle environmental reflections on surfaces
- **Shadow:** Soft contact shadow at 20–30% opacity

### Spatial Treatment
- **Depth:** 3D elements create spatial layering
- **Composition:** Asymmetric balance — more text at the top, more visuals at the bottom
- **Full bleed:** Content extends to all edges

## Composition Strategy

### Layout Structure
- **Grid:** Flexible asymmetric layout
- **Text zone:** Upper 50–60%, left-aligned or centered
- **Visual zone:** Lower 40–50% or right-side 40%
- **Full coverage:** Elements extend to canvas edges

### Element Hierarchy
- **Layer 1:** Gradient background (base)
- **Layer 2:** 3D glass elements (mid-layer)
- **Layer 3:** White typography (top layer)

## Mood & Atmosphere
Professional, technically precise, forward-thinking innovation, calm and rational tone, premium modern aesthetic, crisp digital purity, institutional trustworthiness, developer-oriented credibility

## Art References
Frosted glass UI trend, Apple product visuals, modern 3D web design, Blender Eevee rendering, minimalist tech branding, Material Design depth

## Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840×2160), 300 DPI
- **Color Space:** RGB, sRGB profile
- **3D Software:** Blender, Cinema 4D, Spline
- **Rendering:** Ray-traced or Eevee-rendered glass materials
- **Format:** PNG with transparency, final delivery in JPG
