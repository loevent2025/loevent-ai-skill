# COCKTAIL & SOCIAL EVENTS × INVESTMENT

## Applicable Scenarios
Investor social mixers, Founder × Investor mixers, Deal Flow nights, VC happy hours, fundraising after-parties

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format**: Relaxed socializing between investors and founders, informal networking, relationship building
- **Core Value**: Building trust in a low-pressure setting, informal exchange, relationships before deals

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Investors — meeting founders in a relaxed setting
- **Secondary Audience**: Founders — connecting with investors in an informal context
- **Extended Audience**: Industry professionals — an opportunity to meet people in the investment community
- **Audience Behavior**: Casual socializing, building relationships, getting to know people (not pitching projects)

## Content Elements
- Venue atmosphere (upscale yet relaxed)
- Attendee description (understated but clear)
- Emphasis on informal exchange
- Clear messaging: "This is not a pitch session"
- Compliance disclaimer (mandatory but seamlessly integrated)

## Language Approach
- ✅ Relaxed yet refined
- ✅ Emphasize "not a pitch session," "not a networking event"
- ✅ Relationships before deals
- ❌ Do not create fundraising anxiety
- ❌ Avoid terms like "exclusive," "top-tier," or "elite"
- ⚠️ Must include NFA disclaimer (but it can feel natural)

## Structure Strategy
- Scene-setting opening block: 25%
- Atmosphere description block: 25% — convey the relaxed "not a pitch session" vibe
- Crowd description block: 20% — subtly clarify who will be there
- Participation details block: 15%
- Compliance + action block: 15%
- Emoji density control: 6–8 total, restrained and tasteful

## Emotional Tone
Relaxed yet refined, informal yet professional. A "let's be friends first — business can come later" atmosphere.

## Style Variants

### Variant A: VC × Founder Mixer
- **Use Case**: Mixed social event for investors and founders
- **Opening Format**: 🥂 Not a pitch session — just a drink
- **Tone**: Relaxed, informal, yet refined

### Variant B: Investor-Only Social
- **Use Case**: Socializing among investors
- **Opening Format**: 🍷 GPs, care for a drink?
- **Tone**: Insider, understated, laid-back

---

## Atmosphere Keyword Library
Relaxed, refined, informal, get to know each other first, relationships, grab a drink, not a pitch session, not a networking event

## Prohibited Terms
❌ Exclusive, top-tier, elite, scarce, rare opportunity, fundraising opportunity, investment opportunity, premium resources

## Mandatory Inclusion
⚠️ NFA disclaimer (can be written naturally, e.g., "The usual: this is a social gathering, not a funding commitment")

---

## Key Considerations

### Investment Social Event Essentials
1. **"Not a pitch session" must be reinforced repeatedly**: Distinguish from formal events
2. **NFA disclaimer is required**: But it can be worded naturally
3. **Balance the crowd**: Maintain a good ratio of investors to founders
4. **Maintain a sense of taste**: Venue, drinks, and atmosphere must all be on point
5. **Relationships come first**: Emphasize meeting people, not pitching projects
