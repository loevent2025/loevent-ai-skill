# Web3 Large-Scale Conference Knowledge Base — Education Scene

## Scene Overview
Education-focused Web3 large-scale conferences center on systematic knowledge transfer and on-chain skill development in the blockchain and Web3 space. They are designed for working professionals, Web2 career transitioners, and students who want a structured approach to learning Web3. These events emphasize comprehensive curriculum design, progressive on-chain practice, and transferable skills — serving as critical pathways for entering, advancing in, and transitioning into the Web3 field.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Tens to hundreds of participants
- **Duration**: Half-day to multi-day programs
- **Format**: Structured curriculum + on-chain lab sessions + case study analysis + assignments and assessment + on-chain certificate issuance
- **Participants**: Working professionals, Web2 career transitioners, students, and corporate L&D stakeholders
- **Defining Traits**: Systematic content, progressive pacing, strong emphasis on on-chain practice

## Participant Guidance

### Before the Program
- Define your learning goal (entry-level / development / investment research)
- Review the curriculum and difficulty levels
- Set aside dedicated study time and prepare your on-chain environment
- Install your wallet (MetaMask, OKX Wallet, etc.)
- Stay open to making mistakes — it's part of the process

### During the Program
- Complete every on-chain lab assignment
- Participate actively in class discussions and Q&A
- Ask questions when you're stuck — the Web3 community is welcoming
- Collaborate and team up with fellow cohort members on projects
- Take good notes and revisit and practice regularly

### After the Program
- Continue practicing on-chain right away — don't let the momentum stop
- Stay active and engaged in the builder community
- Participate in hackathons to sharpen your skills in a real setting
- Apply for grants or DAO contributions that interest you
- Keep up with industry developments and plan your next learning milestone
