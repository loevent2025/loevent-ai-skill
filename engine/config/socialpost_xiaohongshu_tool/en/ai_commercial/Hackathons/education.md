# AI Hackathon Knowledge Base – Education Track

## Overview
Education-focused AI hackathons are designed for AI learners and early-stage developers. They emphasize the learning journey and skill development while lowering the barrier to entry. These events are one of the most effective pathways into AI development — using a "learn by building" approach to help participants rapidly acquire hands-on AI skills, backed by comprehensive guidance and mentor support throughout.

---

## Event Profile
- **Scale**: Tens to hundreds of participants; mid-sized for optimal mentorship
- **Duration**: Typically 24–48 hours, with pre-event training sessions
- **Format**: Pre-event curriculum + team building + mentor-led development + showcase and judging
- **Participants**: Students, career changers, AI newcomers, beginner developers
- **Defining characteristics**: Low barrier to entry, high mentor density, strong learning culture, inclusive environment

## Participation Tips

### Mindset
- Don't worry about being "not good enough" — everyone here is a beginner
- Ask questions boldly; there are no dumb questions
- Enjoy the learning process; focus less on the outcome
- Support your teammates and grow together
- Done beats perfect

### Learning Strategies
- Make full use of the pre-event training and learning materials
- Familiarize yourself with the code templates ahead of time
- Ask mentors for help the moment you get stuck
- Take notes you can refer back to later
- Divide tasks with teammates based on strengths and learn from each other

### Project Approach
- Choose a simple, completable project scope
- Lean on the provided templates and examples
- Get it working first, then optimize
- Aim for complete, not complex
- Prepare to clearly demo your project's core feature

### After the Event
- Save your learning materials and code
- Stay in touch with your learning partners
- Continue deepening your knowledge along the suggested learning path
- Try building a similar project independently
- Join the learning community and keep the conversation going
