# Developer Meetup × Community Type (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
Open-source AI project user meetups, LLM framework community gatherings, AI contributor get-togethers, local AI community exchanges, ecosystem developer meetups, community newcomer welcome events

## Scene Characteristics
- **Format**: Community exchange, user sharing, contributor gatherings, casual socializing
- **Core Value**: Community belonging, user connections, contributor recognition, ecosystem awareness

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Community members—developers who use community projects
- **Secondary Audience**: Contributors—people who have contributed to community projects
- **Tertiary Audience**: Community newcomers—new members who recently joined the community
- **Extended Audience**: Community-curious—people interested in learning about the community
- **Audience Behavior**: Community exchange, meeting members, sharing experiences, strengthening belonging

### Common Audience Combinations
- **User Exchange**: Primarily community users; suited for user meetups
- **Contributor Gathering**: Primarily contributors; suited for contributor get-togethers
- **Newcomer Welcome**: Newcomers + established members; suited for welcome events

## Content Elements
- Community identity rallying
- Community topic curation
- User experience sharing
- Contributor recognition
- Community updates
- Community entry points

## Language Approach
- Community-oriented conversational tone
- Sense of belonging through "we" and "us"
- Warm and friendly
- Recognition of contributions
- Welcoming to newcomers

## Structure Strategy
- Opening rallying block: 20%
- Event content block: 35%
- Community atmosphere block: 25%
- Participation details block: 10%
- Call-to-action block: 10%
- Emoji density: 12–16 total, warm community feel

## Emotional Tone
Community belonging, warm connections. The feeling of home when "gathering with people from the same community."

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Community Meetup
- **Use for**: Regular gatherings for community members
- **Opening Format**: 💙 {Community Name} {City} Meetup | Let's Meet IRL
- **Tone**: Community-driven, belonging, relaxed

### Variant B: Contributor Exchange
- **Use for**: Exchange among contributors
- **Opening Format**: 🛠️ {Community Name} Contributor Gathering | Core Members Get-Together
- **Tone**: Contribution-focused, recognition, exchange

### Variant C: Newcomer Welcome
- **Use for**: Welcoming community newcomers
- **Opening Format**: 🌱 {Community Name} Newcomer Meetup | Welcome to the Community
- **Tone**: Welcoming, friendly, guided

## Key Considerations

### Community Meetup Essentials
1. **Belonging is the core**: "The community's," "ours"
2. **Recognize contributions**: Make contributors feel valued
3. **Welcome newcomers**: Make newcomers feel accepted
4. **Online to offline**: Turn online acquaintances into real-life friends
5. **Sustained connection**: Community entry points after the meetup
