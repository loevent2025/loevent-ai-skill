# Web3 Technical Workshop — General Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Conditions

Apply this module when the event is a comprehensive technical workshop covering multiple themes or audiences:
- Comprehensive technical training, Multi-module workshops
- Workshop series, Technical training weeks
- Multi-host joint technical training

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Multi-module instruction + Group hands-on practice + Mentor guidance + Project showcase |
| Duration | Half-day to full-day, multiple topic modules |
| Participants | Learners at various levels, Multiple instructors, Teaching assistant team |
| Core Objectives | Comprehensive skill building, Multi-dimensional learning, Broad coverage |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Focus |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| P0 | General learners | Structured learning, Comprehensive upskilling, One-stop training |
| P1 | Focused learners | Selective learning, Modular participation |
| P2 | Skill upgraders | Filling knowledge gaps, Expanding capabilities |
| P3 | Companies / Teams | Team training, Batch upskilling |

---

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| Comprehensive technical training day | Formal Professional (A) | Systematic coverage, Professional depth |
| Technical training week | Energetic & Engaging (B) | Rich content, Continuous learning |
| Custom corporate training | Formal Professional (A) | Tailored curriculum, Guaranteed outcomes |
| Open technical day | Benefits-Driven (D) | Broad participation, Resource-rich |

---

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Coverage Scope (at least 2 instances)
- "Spanning [number] major modules including [module list]"
- "Full coverage from [fundamentals] to [advanced topics]"

### 2. Teaching Resources (at least 1 instance)
- "[Number]+ instructors teaching jointly"
- "Supported by a full teaching assistant team"

### 3. Flexible Participation (at least 1 instance)
- "Choose the modules that interest you"
- "Select your difficulty level based on your experience"

### 4. Compliance Disclaimer (required)
- "⚠️ This is a technical training event and does not involve investment advice (NFA)"

## ❓ FAQ Templates

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Do I have to attend the full day? | No — you can choose the modules that interest you |
| Can I attend with limited experience? | Yes — we recommend starting with beginner-level modules |
| Can I switch modules midway? | You can, but we recommend completing a full module for best results |
| Will there be recordings? | Some modules will be recorded, but we recommend attending hands-on sessions in person |

---

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Module content that doesn't match what was advertised
- ❌ Overstating the learning outcomes of a single-day training
- ❌ Neglecting the needs of participants at different skill levels
- ❌ Linking technical training to investment advice
