# BUSINESS CONFERENCES × COMMUNITY

## Applicable Scenarios
Community annual conferences, user conferences, ecosystem gatherings, fan festivals, DAO governance summits, member appreciation events, brand community celebrations, project anniversary events

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Scale**: 200–1,000 attendees
- **Duration**: 1–2 days
- **Format**: Keynote sessions, community recognition, product launches, social events, parties
- **Core Value**: Community belonging, brand identity, social connection, perks & rewards

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Core Members—active participants, early supporters
- **Secondary Audience**: Project Users—product users
- **Tertiary Audience**: Potential Members—people interested in the community
- **Extended Audience**: Partners—ecosystem partners
- **Audience Behavior**: Attending milestone events, witnessing growth, claiming perks, building connections

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Core Gathering**: Primarily core members; suitable for anniversaries / invitation-only sessions
- **Open Celebration**: All audiences; suitable for user conferences / public events

## Content Elements
- Community identity call-out
- Team / founder sharing
- Product roadmap reveal
- Community recognition segment
- Perks / airdrops (only when they genuinely exist)
- Social / party segment

## Language Treatment
- Conversational, warm
- Emphasize belonging
- Build anticipation and surprise
- Moderate use of community inside language
- Avoid overly commercial tone

## Structural Strategy
- Opening call-out block: 15%
- Event highlights block: 35%
- Perks information block: 20%
- Participation details block: 15%
- Call-to-action block: 15%
- Emoji density control: 12–16 total, lively and celebratory

## Emotional Atmosphere
Warm, festive, connected. "You belong here." "This is our moment." Support fun, support belonging.

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Anniversary Celebration
- **Use Case**: Anniversaries, milestone celebrations
- **Opening Format**: 🎉 {Community Name} Anniversary! Time for a Reunion!
- **Tone**: Enthusiastic, grateful, forward-looking

### Variant B: Product Launch
- **Use Case**: New feature releases, roadmap announcements
- **Opening Format**: 🚀 {Project Name} Product Day | First Look at What's New
- **Tone**: Anticipatory, surprising, co-creative

### Variant C: User Conference
- **Use Case**: Annual user conferences, ecosystem conferences
- **Opening Format**: 🌟 {Project Name} Annual Conference | Our Yearly Gathering
- **Tone**: Formal yet warm

## Key Considerations

### Community Conference Essentials
1. **Belonging is the core**: Emphasize "we," "together," "yours"
2. **Perks must be real**: Never fabricate what doesn't exist
3. **Language should feel community-native**: Inside jokes and community lingo are fine, but ensure the target audience can understand
4. **Atmosphere matters more than information**: Community events sell belonging
