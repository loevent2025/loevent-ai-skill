# AI Large-Scale Conference Knowledge Base — General Scene

## Scene Overview
General AI large-scale conferences are comprehensive, cross-industry events that bring together foundation model providers, AI startups, enterprise users, investment institutions, and technical developers. These events offer the broadest coverage and are ideal for participants who want a full panorama of AI industry trends. They are premier destinations for gauging the industry's direction, discovering business opportunities, and building wide-ranging professional networks.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Thousands to tens of thousands of participants
- **Duration**: Typically 2–3 days
- **Format**: Exhibition booths + keynote presentations + product launches + business networking sessions + technical breakout forums
- **Participants**: AI vendors, enterprise users, developers, investors, media, and government representatives
- **Defining Traits**: High information density, broad coverage, rich business opportunities

## Attendee Tips

### Before the Event
- Research the exhibitor list and flag your priority visits
- Bring plenty of business cards
- Download the official event app and map your route
- Pre-schedule 1-on-1 meetings with target exhibitors
- Define your key objectives and questions for each meeting

### On the Floor
- Wear comfortable shoes — the venue is large
- Bring a power bank — photos and networking drain batteries fast
- Do a quick walkthrough first, then go deep on priority stops
- Engage actively with booth staff rather than just collecting materials
- Take notes on key takeaways from each conversation

### Business Networking
- Have a clear, concise introduction of your company and needs
- Ask targeted questions rather than broad, unfocused ones
- Exchange contact information and define next steps before you part
- Organize business cards the same day — follow up while the conversation is fresh
- Attend official networking sessions and matchmaking events
