# AI Hackathon — Investment Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply this module when the event theme involves the following keywords:
- AI startup hackathon, AI Startup Challenge, Founder Marathon
- Investor-judged competition, AI project pitch hackathon, fundraising challenge
- AI incubator selection competition, AI accelerator admission challenge

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Product development + Pitch deck refinement + Investor judging + Demo presentation |
| Duration | 48–72 hours intensive, or 2–4 week competition cycle |
| Participants | AI startup teams, investment firms, incubators, financial advisors (FAs) |
| Core Objective | Project screening, fundraising matchmaking, incubator admission, early-stage deal discovery |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Concerns |
|----------|---------------|--------------|
| P0 | Early-Stage AI Founders | Fundraising opportunities, incubation resources, investor visibility |
| P1 | Technical Founders | MVP validation, team building, path to commercialization |
| P2 | AI Investment Firms | Early-stage deal sourcing, talent discovery, sector coverage |
| P3 | Serial Entrepreneurs | New direction validation, resource integration, rapid launch |

---

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| Incubator Selection Competition | Formal & Professional (A) | Incubation resources, professional judging |
| AI Founder Marathon | Energetic & Engaging (B) | Startup energy, team building |
| Fundraising Challenge | Value-Driven (D) | Investor matchmaking, fundraising opportunities |
| Business Model Competition | Curiosity-Driven (C) | Business validation, model innovation |

## 📝 Required Content Elements

### 1. Fundraising Value (minimum 2 instances)
- "[Number]+ investment firms judging / observing"
- "Winning teams receive seed-round investment opportunities"

### 2. Incubation Resources (minimum 1 instance)
- "Fast-track entry to [Incubator] AI Accelerator program"
- "Receive [duration] of complimentary co-working space"

### 3. Mentorship Support (minimum 1 instance)
- "[Number]+ AI startup mentors providing 1-on-1 coaching"
- "Investor Office Hours for Q&A"

- "💡 All fundraising opportunities require independent evaluation by both parties and do not constitute investment commitments."

## ❓ FAQ Templates

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Do I need a complete team? | No — you can form teams on-site. We'll facilitate a team-matching session. |
| Can I enter with an existing AI project? | Yes, but you'll need to demonstrate new progress during the competition. |
| Is the investment guaranteed? | Investment interest is subject to independent post-event discussions between both parties. |
| What should I prepare? | We recommend having a project idea and a brief pitch deck ready in advance. |

---

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Promising specific investment amounts or terms
- ❌ Overstating incubator admission rates
- ❌ Exaggerating AI product capabilities or market outlook
- ❌ Misrepresenting investment firm participation
