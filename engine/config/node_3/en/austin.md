# Austin Venue Knowledge Base

## Receptions/Social Venues

### 1. The W Austin (Living Room Bar)
- **Capacity**: 80-200 people
- **Suitable for**: Industry receptions, investor networking, high-end Networking Nights

### 2. Geraldine's (Hotel Van Zandt)
- **Capacity**: 60-150 people
- **Suitable for**: Founder dinners, VC networking, private industry receptions

### 3. Higher Ground (East Austin)
- **Capacity**: 100-300 people
- **Suitable for**: Tech After Parties, SXSW events, industry celebrations, brand parties

### 4. The Roosevelt Room
- **Capacity**: 40-100 people

### 5. Rainey Street District (Multiple Venues)
- **Capacity**: 50-500 people
