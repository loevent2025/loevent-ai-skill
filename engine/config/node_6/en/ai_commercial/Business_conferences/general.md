# AI Industry — B2B Conference Marketing Strategy

## Event Definition

A B2B conference is a high-end event format designed for enterprise decision-makers, investors, and senior industry executives. It centers on AI industry trends, enterprise implementation practices, and business partnerships. Typical formats include keynote presentations, panel discussions, case study sessions, and networking meetings — with an emphasis on content depth and attendee quality.

## Core Objectives

- Establish industry thought leadership
- Facilitate high-quality business partnerships
- Showcase real-world AI implementation outcomes
- Deliver in-depth industry insights
- Build a high-caliber professional network

## Core Channel Mix

| Channel | Priority | Role |

| WeChat Ecosystem | Primary | In-depth content, targeted reach |
| Media Network | Primary | Industry coverage, professional credibility |

| Private Communities | Important | Precision invitations |
| Podcasts | Supporting | Long-form content, long-tail distribution |

## Communications Strategy

### Hashtag Strategy
- Primary: #[EventName] #AIBusinessSummit
- Content: #AIImplementation #EnterpriseDigitalTransformation #AIStrategy
- Avoid overly technical or niche tags

### Content Strategy

| Phase | Content Types | Publishing Frequency |
|-------|---------------|----------------------|
| Warm-Up | Speaker interviews, topic previews, industry insights | Once daily |
| Build-Up | Case study previews, speaker perspective articles | Every 2 days |
| Push | Agenda highlights, speaker quote previews | 1–2 times daily |
| Live | Real-time key takeaways, case study highlights | Live curation |
| Post-Event | In-depth recap, insights report | Series release |

### Content Tone
- Professional and substantive
- Business insights-driven
- Case study-led
- Written from a decision-maker's perspective

## Thought Leader Partnership Strategy

### Thought Leader Profiles

| Type | Profile | Engagement Format |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Enterprise Executives | Heads of AI or digital transformation at notable companies | Keynote presentations, case study sessions |
| Investors | VC partners focused on the AI sector | Trend commentary, panel discussions |
| Industry Experts | Consulting firm partners, research analysts | Insights sharing, report releases |
| Academic Leaders | University professors, research institute experts | Emerging technology presentations |

### Engagement Formats
- Speaker Invitations: keynotes, panel discussions, moderation
- Content Co-creation: interviews, joint reports
- Social Amplification: personal account endorsements
- Podcast Collaboration: in-depth conversations

## Distribution Timeline

### T-4 Weeks: Launch Phase
- Publish event announcement
- Begin speaker confirmation outreach
- Initiate media partnership discussions
- Launch targeted enterprise invitations

### T-3 Weeks: Warm-Up Phase
- Announce headline speakers progressively
- Release topic previews
- Prepare speaker interview content
- Publish in-depth preview on official channels

### T-2 Weeks: Build-Up Phase
- Release full agenda
- Publish case study previews
- Secure in-depth media coverage
- Release podcast warm-up content

### T-1 Week: Push Phase
- Publish full speaker lineup overview
- Highlight standout sessions
- Release attendee guide
- Send final registration reminders

### T-Day: Live Phase
- Publish curated live updates
- Distribute key takeaways in real time
- Amplify speaker quotes and case study highlights
- Guide attendees toward networking opportunities

### T+1 Week: Post-Event Phase
- Compile and publish in-depth content
- Release curated case study collection
- Publish industry insights report
- Coordinate media recap coverage

## Enterprise Client Outreach Strategy

### Targeted Invitation Channels

| Channel Type | Outreach Method | Content Focus |
|--------------|-----------------|---------------|
| Target Enterprise Executives | One-on-one personalized invitations | Industry insights, benchmark case studies |
| Industry Associations | Official endorsement and referral | Professional credibility, industry value |
| Partners | Joint invitations | Partnership opportunities, resource matching |
| Past Attendees | Returning attendee outreach | New sessions, new speakers |

### Invitation Approach
- Emphasize the caliber and seniority of the attendee base
- Highlight implementation case studies and practical takeaways
- Communicate the value of premium networking opportunities
- Prepare personalized invitation letters for key targets
