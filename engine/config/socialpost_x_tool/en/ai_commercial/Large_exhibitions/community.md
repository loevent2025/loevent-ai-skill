# Large-Scale Exhibition × Community Type (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
AI developer community annual conferences, open-source AI project user conferences, AI Builder community gatherings, LLM framework ecosystem conferences, AI tool community events, AI contributor summits

## Scene Characteristics
- **Format**: Community sharing, contributor recognition, product/roadmap releases, social events, parties
- **Core Value**: Community belonging, contribution recognition, Builder connections, ecosystem awareness

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Core community members—developers who actively use and contribute
- **Secondary Audience**: Project users—developers who use community projects/tools
- **Tertiary Audience**: Potential contributors—people who want to participate in community contributions
- **Extended Audience**: Ecosystem partners—projects and companies collaborating with the community
- **Audience Behavior**: Participate in the community, gain belonging, witness growth, build connections

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Core Gathering**: Primarily core members; suited for contributor events
- **Open Conference**: Full community participation; suited for annual conferences
- **Ecosystem Showcase**: Community + partners; suited for ecosystem conferences

## Content Elements
- Community identity rallying
- Project/product updates
- Contributor recognition
- Roadmap releases
- Community sharing (customer case studies, contribution stories)
- Social events / parties
- Swag / perks (only when they actually exist)

## Language Approach
- Community-oriented conversational tone
- Sense of belonging through "we" and "us"
- Recognition and gratitude for contributions
- Builder culture atmosphere
- Technically enthusiastic yet relaxed and friendly

## Structure Strategy
- Opening rallying block: 15%
- Event highlights block: 35%
- Community value block: 25%
- Participation details block: 15%
- Call-to-action block: 10%
- Emoji density: 12–16 total, lively and energetic

## Emotional Tone
Community belonging, Builder enthusiasm. A sense of "being among your people." Technically passionate but not overly serious.

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Annual Conference
- **Use for**: Community annual events, anniversary celebrations
- **Opening Format**: 🎉 {Community Name} Annual Conference | The Yearly Builder Gathering
- **Tone**: Celebratory, reflective, forward-looking

### Variant B: Contributor Recognition
- **Use for**: Contributor events, Contributor Day
- **Opening Format**: 🛠️💙 {Community Name} Contributor Day | Thank You to Every Builder
- **Tone**: Recognition, gratitude, inspiration

### Variant C: Ecosystem Showcase
- **Use for**: Ecosystem conferences, partner showcases
- **Opening Format**: ⚡ {Ecosystem Name} Ecosystem Day | See What Everyone Is Building
- **Tone**: Ecosystem-focused, showcase, connection

## Key Considerations

### Community Exhibition Essentials
1. **Belonging is the core**: "We," "together," "the community's"
2. **Recognize contributors**: Make contributors feel seen
3. **Technically enthusiastic yet relaxed**: Not a rigid technical conference
4. **Perks must be real**: Only mention what actually exists—don't fabricate
5. **Community connections**: Emphasize ongoing, sustained participation
