## 🎭 Cross-Disciplinary Investment Atmosphere Guide

### 1. Three-Dimensional Review — The Collision Moment
```
Tech VC: "The technology checks out, but the path to commercialization isn't clear."
Industry VC: "You've nailed the use case — companies in our portfolio are actively looking for this solution."
Industry Partner: "If you can deliver that outcome, we'll be your first customer."
Three perspectives, three assessments — founders get the most authentic market feedback in real time.
```

### 2. Cross-Background Team Building — The Bonding Moment
```
Day 1, past midnight: The tech person and the industry person are still debating product direction.
Day 2, morning: They finally align — efficient division of labor kicks in.
One hour before demo: The tech person handles the live demo, the industry person tells the story.
48 hours ago they were strangers. Now they're discussing equity splits.
```

### 3. Cross-Disciplinary Investment Vocabulary
- Increased certainty: "A tech + industry combination reduces execution risk."
- Complementary value: "Each person's gaps are covered by the other."
- Investor confidence: "This team composition gives me more conviction."

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Promising specific investment amounts or terms
- ❌ Overstating fundraising success rates for cross-background teams
- ❌ Forcing unsuitable cross-background pairings
- ❌ Disclosing specifics from closed-door judging sessions
- ❌ Creating an adversarial dynamic between technical and industry backgrounds
