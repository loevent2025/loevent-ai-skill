# Technical Workshop × Education (AI/Model Commercialization Edition)

## Applicable Scenarios
AI development beginner workshops, hands-on LLM application introductions, first AI course for career changers, AI development for students, AI tools for non-technical professionals, Prompt Engineering fundamentals

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format**: Progressive instruction + ample hands-on practice, sufficient teaching assistants, patient Q&A support
- **Core Value**: Beginner-friendly onboarding, confidence building, completing a first project, clear learning direction

## Audience Positioning
- **Primary Audience**: Complete beginners — those who want to learn AI development but don't know where to start
- **Secondary Audience**: Career changers — traditional developers looking to transition into AI
- **Tertiary Audience**: Students — those interested in AI development while still in school
- **Extended Audience**: Non-technical backgrounds — product managers and operations professionals who want to understand AI development
- **Audience Behavior**: Learning the basics, building confidence, taking the first step, finding direction

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Complete Beginners**: Primarily learners, suited for introductory-level workshops
- **Transitioning Developers**: Those with programming fundamentals, suited for accelerated onboarding
- **Student Sessions**: University students, suited for campus workshops

## Content Elements
- Beginner-friendly declaration
- Learning objectives (set realistic expectations)
- Step-by-step instruction
- Ample hands-on practice time
- Teaching assistant staffing details
- Pre-configured environments
- Follow-up learning paths

## Language Approach
- Lower psychological barriers
- Encouraging tone
- Normalize saying "I don't understand"
- Break steps down clearly
- Emphasize "You can do this too"

## Structure Strategy
- Opening information block: 15%
- Confidence-building block: 15%
- Learning content block: 40%
- Support resources block: 20%
- Call-to-action block: 10%
- Emoji density: 12–16 total, warm and encouraging

## Emotional Atmosphere
Friendly and approachable, fully supportive. An encouraging vibe of "You can learn this too."

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Complete Beginner
- **Use for**: Absolute beginners with no prior experience

## Key Considerations

### Essential Principles for Education Workshops
1. **Lowering barriers is the top priority**: Make everyone feel "I can learn this too"
2. **Adequate teaching assistants**: Ensure every participant can get help
3. **Pre-configured environments**: Don't waste time on setup
4. **Small, incremental steps**: One small step at a time
5. **Encouragement first**: If someone struggles, that's on the instructor — not the learner
