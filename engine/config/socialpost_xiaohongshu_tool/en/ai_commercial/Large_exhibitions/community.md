# AI Large-Scale Conference Knowledge Base — Community Scene

## Scene Overview
Community-focused AI events center on interaction and exchange among AI enthusiasts and practitioners, emphasizing a relaxed atmosphere, peer dialogue, and a sense of belonging. These events are flexible in scale and diverse in format, serving as key venues for AI community building, experience sharing, and professional networking—as well as an important expression of AI culture and community spirit.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Tens to hundreds of participants
- **Duration**: Typically 2–4 hours
- **Format**: Meetup presentations + panel discussions + open networking + themed salons + hybrid online/offline sessions
- **Participants**: AI enthusiasts, developers, entrepreneurs, students, and practitioners
- **Defining Traits**: Relaxed atmosphere, high interactivity, strong social focus

## Community Operations Recommendations

### Event Preparation
- Begin promotion 2 weeks in advance
- Choose a venue with convenient transportation access
- Prepare check-in materials and name badges
- Plan icebreaker and facilitation activities
- Provide refreshments and light snacks

### Setting the Atmosphere
- Organizers should warmly welcome new attendees
- Design simple icebreaker activities
- Prepare conversation starters
- Encourage attendees to introduce themselves to each other
- Maintain a relaxed and friendly tone throughout

### Community Maintenance
- Share post-event summaries and photos
- Create an ongoing community group for continued engagement
- Organize regular online and offline events
- Encourage community members to lead sessions
- Build and preserve the community's shared content and memories
