# DEMO DAYS × EDUCATION

## Applicable Scenarios
Student demo days, bootcamp graduation showcases, learning project presentations, beginner project exhibitions, educational hackathon demos, end-of-course final presentations

## Scene Characteristics
- **Format:** Learning outcome showcase, mentor feedback, encouraging reviews, graduation ceremony
- **Core Value:** Demonstrating learning outcomes, recognizing growth, building experience, developing confidence

## Audience Profiles
- **Primary Audience:** Learners / Students — showcasing what they've built
- **Secondary Audience:** Mentors / Instructors — providing guidance and feedback
- **Tertiary Audience:** Potential Employers — discovering emerging talent
- **Extended Audience:** Future Learners — getting a sense of what the program delivers
- **Audience Behavior:** Demonstrating growth, earning recognition, building confidence, exploring opportunities

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Learning Focus:** Learners + Mentors — ideal for course graduation
- **Career Focus:** Learners + Employers — ideal for employment-oriented programs

## Content Elements
- Program / course introduction
- Learner project presentations
- Mentor feedback sessions
- Graduation / completion ceremony
- Next steps and continued learning pathways
- Employment opportunities (if applicable)

## Language Guidelines
- Encouraging and affirming throughout
- Emphasize growth over competition
- Reflect on the learning journey
- Point toward future pathways
- Reduce presentation anxiety

## Structure Strategy
- Opening info block: 15%
- Learning journey recap block: 20%
- Project showcase block: 35%
- Mentor feedback block: 15%
- Future pathways block: 15%
- Emoji density: 12–14 total, warm and encouraging

## Emotional Tone
Celebratory, encouraging, growth-oriented. The feeling of "look how far you've come."

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Course Graduation / Course Graduation
- **Best for:** Bootcamp or training program graduation
- **Opening format:** 🎓 {Course Name} Demo Day | Learning Outcomes Showcase

### Variant B: Student Showcase / Student Showcase
- **Best for:** University projects, student-led events

## Notes

### Key Principles for Educational Demo Days
1. **Reduce presentation anxiety** — don't judge perfection; recognize growth
2. **Keep feedback constructive and warm** — mentor reviews should build confidence, not intimidate
3. **Acknowledge every participant** — certificates, POAPs, and community access for all
4. **Point toward next steps** — show learners how to continue growing after the event
