# Hong Kong Venue Knowledge Base

- **Capacity**: 100-250 people
- **Suitable for**: High-end industry cocktail parties, summit after-parties, investor networking, brand launch celebrations

### 2. Potato Head (Sai Ying Pun)
- **Capacity**: 80-200 people
- **Suitable for**: Community mixers, builder gatherings, casual networking, industry after-parties

### 3. Terrible Baby (Central)
- **Capacity**: 40-80 people
- **Suitable for**: Small VIP cocktail parties, core team social events, private roundtable follow-up discussions

### 4. Zuma (Landmark Central)
- **Capacity**: 60-150 people
- **Suitable for**: Investor dinners, VC/founder networking, high-end business cocktail parties

### 5. CÉ LA VI (California Tower, Central)
- **Capacity**: 80-200 people
- **Suitable for**: Industry cocktail parties, networking nights, brand events, social parties

### 6. Foxglove (Central)
- **Capacity**: 50-120 people
