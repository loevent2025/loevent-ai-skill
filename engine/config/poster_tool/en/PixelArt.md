# PIXEL ART Style Guide

## Applicable Scenarios
NFT collectibles, DAO community branding, blockchain game assets, metaverse avatars, crypto-punk communities, retro gaming events

---

## Color Scheme

- **Primary palette:** Limited color palette (8–16 colors), vibrant retro gaming tones
- **Background:** Single solid color (black, white, or bold flat hues)
- **Saturation:** High contrast, bold color blocks
- **Color behavior:** Hard edges, no color bleed, precise pixel placement

### Common Color Combinations

| Style | Colors | Background |
|---|---|---|
| **Classic 8-bit** | Bright Cyan (#00FFFF) + Hot Pink (#FF1493) + Yellow (#FFD700) | Pure Black (#000000) |
| **Retro Gaming** | Royal Blue (#4169E1) + Orange (#FF8C00) + Lime Green (#32CD32) | White (#FFFFFF) |
| **Crypto-Punk** | Purple (#9D4EDD) + Gold (#FFB627) + Electric Blue (#0BFFFF) | Deep Violet (#4B0082) |
| **Soft Pixel** | Soft Pink (#FFB3D9) + Mint Green (#B4E7CE) + Lavender (#C5B9E5) | Cream (#FFF8DC) |

---

## Visual Elements

- Low-resolution pixel elements (8×8, 16×16, 32×32, 64×64)
- Blocky geometric shapes
- Retro game character sprites
- Pixel fonts and typography
- Center-focused composition on solid backgrounds
- Dithering patterns for shading
- Pixelated icons and symbols
- Thick borders (1–2 pixel outline)

---

## Effects & Treatment

- Hard pixel edges (no anti-aliasing)
- Manual dithering for shading
- Limited color indexing
- Pixel-perfect alignment
- Clean scaling that preserves pixel integrity
- Deliberate color layering
- Constrained resolution aesthetics
- CRT scanline effect (optional)

---

## Composition Strategy

- Centered portrait composition (NFT avatars)
- Isometric pixel environments
- Simple layouts on solid color backgrounds
- Sprite organization with clear spacing
- Front-facing or ¾ view character orientation
- Strong outlines for instant recognition
- Solid backgrounds to maximize focal clarity
- Modular trait-based composition (for NFT collections)

---

## Typography Settings

- **Color selection:** Choose colors with high contrast against the solid background
  - On black backgrounds: Bright Cyan (#00FFFF), Electric Green (#00FF41), Pure White (#FFFFFF)
  - On white backgrounds: Pure Black (#000000), Electric Blue (#0BFFFF)
  - On deep violet backgrounds: Electric Green (#00FF41), Yellow (#FFD700), Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- **Fill method:** Solid color fill, single color, no gradients — consistent with pixel art's hard-edge aesthetic
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No drop shadow or offset shadow
  - No glow effects
  - No stroke/outline (except the pixel art standard 1px black outline)
  - No gradient fill
  - No text special effects of any kind
- **Font style:** Pixel fonts with hard edges, no anti-aliasing
- **Overall principle:** Keep text pixel-perfect, clean, and highly legible — consistent with pixel art's flat color block aesthetic

---

## Mood & Atmosphere

Nostalgic gaming charm, playful retro energy, collectible appeal, 80s–90s arcade nostalgia. The overall vibe is fun, approachable, and instantly recognizable — celebrating early video game culture with a modern crypto community personality.
