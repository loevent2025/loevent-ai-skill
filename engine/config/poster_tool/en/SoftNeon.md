# CYBERPUNK METROPOLIS — Visual Style Guide

## Applicable Scenarios

Electronic music festivals and DJ event promotions, tech product launches, gaming campaign visuals, futuristic theme parties, anniversary celebrations, nightclub opening campaigns, esports tournaments, sci-fi film promotions, metaverse / Web3 activations, brand tech-identity building.

---

## Color Palette

- **Primary tones:** Deep neon series — Cyber Blue (`#00D4FF`), Electric Purple (`#9B30FF`), Neon Magenta (`#FF00FF`), Tech Cyan (`#00FFCC`)
- **Background:** Deep blue-purple gradient with subtle transitions from Deep Navy (`#0A0A1A`) → Midnight Purple (`#1A1A3A`) → Deep Space Blue (`#0D1B2A`)
- **Glow accents:** Cyan Blue luminescence (`#00FFFF`), Pink-Violet highlight (`#FF66FF`), White spotlight (`#FFFFFF`) — medium-to-high opacity with soft diffused edges
- **Line art tones:** Cyan Blue linework (`#4A90D9`), Pale Violet outlines (`#7B68EE`) — used for architectural and mechanical structure rendering
- **Saturation:** Background low-saturation (10–20%), glow effects high-saturation (80–100%) — creating strong light/dark contrast
- **Color behavior:** Dark backgrounds create dramatic tension against neon glow effects; light sources are concentrated at the composition's focal point

## Visual Elements

- Futuristic city skyline — dense, towering cyberpunk-style architectural clusters
- Vertical light beams / data streams — neon light columns cascading from buildings or the sky
- Holographic projections / glowing logos — geometric forms or brand marks suspended above structures
- Human silhouettes — typically positioned in the foreground at the bottom, facing the city or light source, evoking a sense of solitary epic scale
- Neon signage / LED screens — scattered across building facades to add urban detail
- Line art illustration elements — intricate mechanical or architectural linework in a blueprint aesthetic
- Starfields / particle accents — adding deep spatial dimension
- Halos / lens flares — focusing the visual center

---

## Effects & Rendering

- Apply fine horizontal or vertical scan-line texture overlay on the background at 5–15% opacity
- Primary light sources use wide-radius glow diffusion (30–80px) in cyan-blue or pink-violet
- Architectural structures use gradient fills transitioning from shadow to highlight
- Neon elements feature multi-layered glow: bright white inner core, colored outer diffusion
- Line art illustration maintains uniform stroke weight (1–3px) in a cyan-blue palette
- Use **Overlay** or **Screen** blend modes to enhance light-effect layering
- Maintain a unified overall tone — either cool cyan-blue or warm pink-violet; avoid mixing
- Optional: light noise texture overlay (3–8%) for added tactile quality

---

## Composition Strategy

- Strong forced-perspective depth, directing the viewer's eye toward the center or vanishing point
- Central focal point principle — the primary light source or landmark structure anchors the center
- Foreground silhouettes (figures or building edges) frame the composition and add depth layers
- Flanking architectural clusters form a "canyon" enclosure effect
- Clear text zoning: brand name / headline at the top, date / event details at the bottom
- Prioritize vertical (portrait) format — emphasizes architectural height and urban intensity
- Preserve breathing room — keep sky or light-source areas visually clean and uncluttered
- Every element serves the unified "futuristic, epic, technological" atmosphere

---

## Typography

**Color selection:**
- Use bright text against the dark background for strong contrast
- Recommended: Cyber Blue (`#00D4FF`), Pure White (`#FFFFFF`), Tech Cyan (`#00FFCC`)
- Secondary info: Light Blue-Gray (`#A0B4C8`) or Pale Violet (`#B8A0D0`) for bottom-section detail text

**Fill method:** Solid flat fill — single color only, no gradients

**Prohibited effects:**
- No gradient fills
- No drop shadows or cast shadows
- No outer glow / inner glow
- No strokes or outlines
- No blur effects
- No text special effects of any kind

**Typeface style:** Tech-forward sans-serif or geometric typefaces; font weights from Regular to Bold; sharp, angular letterforms that emphasize a futuristic mechanical character

**Overall principle:** Keep all text clean, sharp, and highly legible. Establish visual hierarchy through typeface selection and vivid solid colors against the dark background — balancing technological character with readability.

---

## Mood & Atmosphere

Futuristic. Technological. Epic. Solitary. Immersive. The overall tone channels a **cyberpunk metropolis** grandeur — deep backgrounds evoke mystery, while neon glow effects inject vitality and a sense of possibility. Ideal for brand activations targeting audiences drawn to tech-forward aesthetics, futuristic experiences, and immersive environments. Evokes imagination of worlds yet to come, awe at the scale of technological power, and the hypnotic allure of the city at night.
