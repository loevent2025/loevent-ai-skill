# 1. GRADIENT BLUR Style Guide

## Applicable Scenarios
Music festivals, art exhibitions, fashion brands, beauty products, meditation / wellness apps, album covers, lifestyle brands, editorial design, social media campaigns

## Color Palette
- **Primary Colors:** Smooth transitions across vivid mid-to-high saturation hues — Vivid Purple (#8B2FC9), Magenta (#E0459A), Coral Orange (#FF7F50), Rose Pink (#FF6B9D)
- **Background Colors:** Gradient base flowing from Soft Pink (#FFCCE0) → Magenta (#D946A0) → Deep Purple (#7B2D8E)
- **Accent Colors:** Tomato Red (#FF6347), Bright Purple (#9932CC), Warm Apricot (#FFAB76)
- **Saturation:** Mid-to-high (60–85%) — vivid yet easy on the eye, elegant and energetic
- **Color Behavior:** Seamless multi-color gradients with fluid transitions, resembling a lava lamp or organic fluid-blending effect

### Common Color Combinations
- **Lava Rose:** Magenta (#E0459A) → Vivid Purple (#8B2FC9) → Deep Purple (#6B2D9B)
- **Twilight Flow:** Coral Orange (#FF7F50) → Rose Pink (#FF6B9D) → Magenta (#D946A0)
- **Purple Haze Wash:** Blush Pink (#FFD0E8) → Magenta (#E056A0) → Blue-Violet (#7B4DB8)

## Visual Elements
- Soft-edge spheres and circles with gradient fills
- Floating organic forms with blurred edges
- Fluid geometric shapes with blur applied (circles, ellipses, irregular curves)
- Minimal line elements, or none at all
- Abstract cloud-like / lava-like forms
- Layered semi-transparent shapes
- **Grain texture overlay (recommended — adds tactile warmth)**

## Effect Treatment
- Broad Gaussian blur (50–150px radius)
- Soft gradient transitions across 3–5 mid-to-high saturation colors
- Gentle contrast between elements — colors are vivid but boundaries remain diffused
- All shape edges are feathered
- Subtle ambient halo effects
- **Grain / noise overlay (8–15% opacity) to enhance texture and vintage warmth**
- Feathered boundaries — no hard edges anywhere
- Overlapping areas use Soft Light or Screen blend modes

## Composition Strategy
- Centered or slightly off-center focal point
- 30–40% negative space to maintain visual breathing room
- Depth created through layering — larger, more blurred forms in the background
- Smaller, slightly sharper elements in the foreground
- Vertical or diagonal flow to guide the eye
- Asymmetric organic flow layout supported
- Elements float freely — no grounding line
- Text placed in high-contrast, legible zones (light or dark areas)

## Typography Settings
- **Color Selection:**
  - For light / bright background areas: Deep Purple (#3D1A54), Deep Magenta (#6B1A4A), Pure White (#FFFFFF)
  - For dark background areas: Pure White (#FFFFFF), Warm White (#FFF8F0)
- **Fill Method:** Solid color fill, single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited Effects:**
  - No gradient fill
  - No drop shadow / shadow
  - No outer glow / inner glow
  - No stroke / outline
  - No blur effects
  - No text effects of any kind
- **Typeface Style:** Modern geometric sans-serif; weight may range flexibly from Light to Bold
- **Overall Principle:** Text remains clean, sharp, and highly legible — font choice and high-contrast solid color establish a clear visual hierarchy against the vivid background

## Emotional Tone
Elegant energy, fluid dreaminess, warm boldness, artistic avant-garde, sensory romance. The overall atmosphere is full of vitality without being restless — vivid without being overwhelming. Evokes passion, creative inspiration, and visual pleasure. Ideal for brands seeking an artistic, contemporary aesthetic that balances elegance with dynamic energy.

The overall mood is elegant energy, fluid dreaminess, and warm boldness — ideal for art exhibitions, music events, fashion brands, or creative agency promotions.
