# Low-Poly 3D Style Guide

## Applicable Scenarios
Project explainer visuals, whitepaper illustrations, event flow diagrams, data narrative posters, virtual environment design, metaverse promotional materials, spatial concepts, blockchain infrastructure visualization

## Color Palette

- **Primary Colors:** Pastel gradient tones, soft tech hues, clean minimalist palette
- **Background Colors:** Sky gradients, atmospheric fade-outs, clean light tones
- **Saturation:** Medium — between vivid and muted
- **Color Behavior:** Flat shading per polygon face, with subtle gradient transitions between surfaces

### Common Color Combinations

- **Pastel Tech:** Soft Pink (#FFB3D9) + Mint Green (#B4F8C8) + Lavender (#E0BBE4) + Peach (#FFDAB9)
- **Cyber Minimal:** Sky Blue (#A0D8F1) + Coral (#FF9B85) + Pale Yellow (#FFF4A3) + Soft Lilac (#D4BBFF)
- **Future Neutral:** Cool Gray (#B8C5D6) + Cerulean (#87CEEB) + Warm Sand (#F5DEB3) + Soft White (#F8F8FF)
- **Metaverse Dream:** Purple (#C5B9E5) + Teal (#7DD4D0) + Rose (#FFB6C1) + Cream (#FFF8DC)

## Visual Elements

- Simplified geometric primitives (cubes, pyramids, low-poly spheres)
- Multifaceted terrain with triangular mesh grids
- Angular architectural structures
- Low-resolution silhouettes of figures or objects
- Crystal / gem-like forms
- Isometric or perspective 3D environments
- Minimalist trees, clouds, and mountains rendered in polygon form
- Floating geometric islands or platforms
- Simple vehicle or building shapes
- Abstract geometric sculptures

## Effect Treatment

- Flat shading (no smooth interpolation)
- Visible polygon edges creating a multifaceted appearance
- Soft ambient lighting with minimal shadows
- Atmospheric fog / distance fade-out effect
- Simple gradient sky
- Rim lighting along edges
- Minimal highlights
- No complex textures — each face is a solid color
- Subtle ambient occlusion
- Clean vector-like rendering

## Composition Strategy

- Isometric perspective for a clean, technical feel
- Perspective view for an immersive environment
- Foreground, midground, background depth layers
- Simplified horizon line
- Geometric elements arranged with clear spatial hierarchy
- Rule-of-thirds composition for focal emphasis
- Atmospheric perspective (distant elements brighter / more blue)
- Negative space reserved for data or text overlays
- Balanced distribution of geometric forms
- Leading lines through terrain or architectural elements

## Typography Settings

- **Color Selection:** Choose solid colors that provide high contrast against the gradient background
  - Pastel Tech background: Deep Gray (#333333), Deep Purple (#4A3A5C)
  - Cyber Minimal background: Dark Blue-Gray (#2C3E50), Pure Black (#000000)
  - Future Neutral background: Deep Gray (#333333), Deep Blue (#1A365D)
  - Metaverse Dream background: Deep Purple (#4A3A5C), Deep Teal (#1D4E5C)
  - Dark areas: Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- **Fill Method:** Solid color fill, single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited Effects:**
  - No outer glow / inner glow
  - No halo or aura effects
  - No stroke / outline
  - No drop shadow / shadow
  - No gradient fill
  - No contour lighting
  - No text effects of any kind
- **Overall Principle:** Text remains clean, sharp, and highly legible — consistent with the minimalist aesthetic of the low-poly style; professional tone established through typography and layout

## Emotional Tone
Clean futurism, approachable technology, optimistic digital world, light-touch elegance. The overall atmosphere is friendly yet refined — celebrating concepts through accessible geometric aesthetics that are playful without sacrificing professionalism.
