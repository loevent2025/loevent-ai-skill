# AI Tech Workshop Knowledge Base – Community

## Scene Overview
Community AI tech workshops are organized by tech communities or enthusiast groups, emphasizing peer learning, experience sharing, and a sense of belonging. These events are typically free or low-cost, with a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. They serve as an important channel for community members to exchange knowledge, learn from one another, and build connections — a vital reflection of a healthy, active tech community.

---

## Scene Characteristics
- **Scale**: Typically 10–50 participants, facilitating interaction
- **Duration**: 2–4 hours, flexibly arranged
- **Format**: Community sharing + hands-on practice + open networking + collaborative learning
- **Participants**: Community members, tech enthusiasts, independent developers, learners
- **Key Traits**: Welcoming atmosphere, low barrier to entry, strong peer-support culture, social engagement

## Community Operations Guide

### Event Preparation
- Identify volunteer instructors and facilitators
- Prepare content that supports collaborative learning
- Choose a venue that encourages open interaction
- Arrange refreshments and snacks
- Create a group chat ahead of the event for pre-event engagement

### Atmosphere Building
- Frame the event as "learning together," not "being taught"
- Encourage questions and peer support
- Volunteers should warmly welcome newcomers
- Include icebreaker and introduction segments
- Maintain a light, friendly, and inclusive tone throughout

### Peer Support Mechanisms
- Encourage experienced members to assist beginners
- Facilitate guided discussions around common questions
- Set up peer learning pairs or small groups
- Recognize and appreciate members who help others
- Foster a culture of mutual encouragement and support

### Ongoing Operations
- Maintain a consistent event cadence
- Keep the community group active and engaged
- Curate and share learning resources regularly
- Celebrate members' learning milestones
- Encourage members to step up and host their own sessions
