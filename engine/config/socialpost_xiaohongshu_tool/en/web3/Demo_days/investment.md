# WEB3 DEMO DAY × INVESTMENT

## Applicable Scenarios
Web3 fundraising pitch days, crypto VC-only demo days, accelerator graduation pitch events, angel investor matching sessions, vertical track pitch days, corporate venture special showcases

## Scenario Characteristics
- **Format:** Fundraising pitches + live on-chain product demos + investor Q&A + 1-on-1 matching
- **Core Value:** Investor connections, feedback from capital allocators, deal flow generation, project screening

## Audience Profile
- **Primary Audience:** Crypto investors and VCs — discovering early-stage projects, evaluating investment opportunities, screening deals efficiently
- **Secondary Audience:** Web3 founders — showcasing projects, gathering feedback, seeking investment
- **Tertiary Audience:** Corporate venture and exchange investment teams — identifying investment, listing, or partnership targets
- **Extended Audience:** Financial advisors and OTC desks — sourcing project information and connecting resources
- **Audience Behavior:** Screening projects, evaluating live demos and on-chain data, meeting founders, making investment decisions

### Common Audience Combinations
- **Institutional Focus:** Crypto VCs + founders — ideal for formal fundraising pitch events
- **Angel Focus:** Angel investors + early-stage founders — ideal for seed and pre-seed matching

## Content Elements
- Investment positioning for the demo day (funding stage + track focus)
- Project information (track, stage, on-chain data, team background)
- Investor and judge lineup (fund name + AUM + portfolio highlights)
- Pitch + live demo format details
- 1-on-1 matching and office hour mechanism
- Compliance disclaimer (required — NFA / DYOR)

## Language Guidelines
- Investor-first perspective; lead with project quality and on-chain data
- Investor lineup is the primary draw
- Demo completeness, on-chain traction, and tokenomics design are the key signals
- Matching mechanism must be clearly explained
- NFA / DYOR disclaimer is mandatory
- No yield or return promises of any kind

## Content Structure
- Opening info block: 15%
- Project overview block: 25% — project quality + on-chain data highlights
- Investor lineup block: 25% — investor backgrounds + portfolio
- Matching mechanism block: 20% — how connections are made + office hour process
- Compliance and action block: 15%
- Emoji density: 8–12 total, professional and restrained

## Tone & Atmosphere
Professional, efficient, and deal-oriented. The anticipation of: *"Finding the next Web3 unicorn."*

## Style Variants

### Variant A: Fundraising Pitch Day
- **Best for:** Explicitly fundraising-oriented demo days
- **Opening format:** 🚀 {Demo Day Name} | Web3 Founders, Meet Your Investors
- **Tone:** Fundraising-driven, efficient matchmaking

### Variant B: Investor-Only Session
- **Best for:** Closed sessions exclusively for investors
- **Opening format:** 💼 {Demo Day Name} | Investor-Only Web3 Project Showcase
- **Tone:** Curation-focused, quality-assured

### Variant C: Track Focus
- **Best for:** Vertical track pitch days (DeFi, Infra, DePIN, AI × Crypto, etc.)
- **Opening format:** 🔥 {Track} Demo Day | The Latest Projects in the Space, All at Once
- **Tone:** Vertical-specific, professional, in-depth

## Attendee Tips

### For Founders
- Prepare a pitch version tailored specifically for crypto VCs — emphasize on-chain data and tokenomics
- Test your demo thoroughly in advance, including on-chain interaction components
- Have your key metrics ready: TVL, user count, on-chain indicators, growth rate
- Know your ask: round, amount, valuation, token structure
- Prepare deck + one-pager + on-chain data dashboard

### For Investors
- Review project materials and on-chain data ahead of time; come with DD questions
- Focus on the authenticity and completeness of the live demo and on-chain traction
- Observe how the team handles unexpected questions in real time — it reveals execution quality
- Schedule office hours promptly with teams you're interested in
- Compare projects within the same track to sharpen your thesis

### After the Event
- Send follow-up messages within 24 hours via Telegram or email
- Deliver on any materials promised (deck, data, audit reports) promptly
- Track on-chain data continuously
- Stay engaged with appropriate follow-up — avoid over-pushing

## Notes

### Key Principles for Investment Demo Days
1. **Compliance Disclaimer Is Mandatory:** NFA / DYOR / does not constitute investment advice / matchmaking ≠ commitment
2. **Project Quality Must Be Vouched For:** On-chain data, funding stage, demo completeness, audit status
3. **Investor Lineup Is Critical:** Must have actual decision-making authority, be actively deploying capital, and represent a strong portfolio
4. **Matching Mechanism Must Be Clear:** How to schedule 1-on-1s, office hour rules, follow-up process
5. **Demo Is the Deciding Factor:** Web3 projects must demonstrate live on-chain interaction on-site
6. **Regulatory Red Lines:** No token recommendations, price predictions, or yield commitments of any kind
