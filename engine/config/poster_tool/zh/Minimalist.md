# MINIMAL FLAT / 扁平极简风格

## 适用场景
Corporate presentations, white paper visuals, product launches, B2B marketing materials, tech startups, SaaS platforms

## 配色方案
- **Primary Colors:** Single bold brand color (blue #0066FF, orange #FF6B35, or teal #00B8A9)
- **Background:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) or light gray (#F5F5F5)
- **Accent Colors:** Neutral grays (#E0E0E0, #9E9E9E) for secondary elements
- **Saturation:** Moderate to high for brand color, low for supporting elements
- **Color Behavior:** Flat application with no gradients or shadows

### 常用配色组合
- Tech/Corporate: Primary blue (#0066FF) + light gray (#F5F5F5) + dark gray text (#333333)
- Creative/Startup: Orange (#FF6B35) + white + charcoal (#2C3E50)
- Finance/Healthcare: Teal (#00B8A9) + white + navy (#1A365D)

## 视觉元素
- Simple geometric shapes (circles, squares, rectangles, triangles)
- Thin line illustrations (2-3px stroke weight)
- Minimalist icons with consistent stroke weight
- Sans-serif typography (geometric typefaces)
- Grid-based alignment
- Abstract geometric patterns
- Negative space as a design element

## 效果处理
- Zero shadows or depth effects
- No gradients or color transitions
- Crisp edges with no blur
- Flat color fills only
- Uniform stroke weights
- Clean vector appearance
- High contrast between elements and background

## 构图策略
- Asymmetric balance with generous white space
- 60-70% negative space to content ratio
- Grid-based alignment (often thirds or golden ratio)
- Clear visual hierarchy with size differentiation
- Elements grouped in clusters with breathing room
- Text blocks occupy 20-30% of composition
- Focal point positioned off-center for dynamic tension

## 文字设置
- **颜色选择:** 选择与背景形成高对比度的纯色
  - 白色/浅灰背景推荐: 深灰色 (#333333)、纯黑色 (#000000)、或品牌主色
  - 品牌色块上推荐: 纯白色 (#FFFFFF)
- **填充方式:** 纯色填充，单一颜色，无渐变，与扁平设计美学保持一致
- **禁止效果:** 
  - 无阴影/投影
  - 无外发光/内发光
  - 无描边
  - 无渐变填充
  - 无任何文字特效
- **字体风格:** 几何无衬线字体，保持干净利落
- **整体原则:** 文字保持极简、干净、高可读性，与扁平设计的零特效美学完全一致

## 情绪氛围
Professional, clean, modern, trustworthy, efficient. The overall energy is calm, sophisticated, and confident—perfect for conveying clarity and competence in business contexts.
