# AI Cross-Disciplinary Bootcamp – Education Event Knowledge Base

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Cross-disciplinary icebreakers + dual-mentor teaching + mixed-team hands-on projects + showcase & critique |
| Duration | 1–3 day intensive training or 2–4 week course series |
| Participants | Students from diverse backgrounds, cross-domain mentors, industry partners |
| Core Objective | Break cognitive boundaries, complement each other's skills, produce cross-disciplinary work, develop versatile AI talent |

---

## 🔥 Cross-Disciplinary Core Elements (Unique to This Module)

### Contrast & Creative Tension
**Narrative formula**:
> "When [a student from World X] meets [a student from World Y], what will they learn?"

**Essential elements**:
- ✅ Clearly highlight the background differences between both sides
- ✅ Use contrast phrasing: "X excels at…, Y is skilled in…"
- ✅ Emphasize the learning value and novelty of this combination

### High-Energy Action Verbs
Use dynamic verbs to describe the learning process:
- ❌ "collaborative learning," "joint training," "co-participation"
- ✅ "collide," "fuse," "cross boundaries," "catalyze," "co-create"

## 🎭 Cross-Disciplinary Atmosphere Guide

### 1. Vivid Scene-Setting
```
❌ "The event will be held at XX"
✅ "The whiteboard is split in two — the left side covered in clinical workflow diagrams, the right side filled with technical architecture, and a giant question mark in the middle"
```

### 2. Collision Moments
```
"2:00 PM — Icebreaker: two worlds meet face-to-face for the first time"
"4:00 PM — A doctor successfully runs their first AI code"
"10:00 PM — A developer finally understands why 'this feature is simple' is anything but simple in a medical setting"
```

### 3. Emotional Energy Language
- Cognitive impact: "So that's how it works," "I never thought of it that way"
- Complementary achievement: "I finally get it," "Couldn't have done it without you"
- Co-creation excitement: "We actually built this"

## ❓ FAQ Templates

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Can I join if I know nothing about the other field? | That's exactly why cross-disciplinary learning exists! Foundational content will be covered to get you started |
| Will I fall behind? | The dual-mentor format is designed to keep participants from both sides on track |
| Are teams assigned randomly? | Teams are formed to ensure a mix of backgrounds in every group |
| Are the final project standards high? | The focus is on the cross-disciplinary collaboration process, not a polished product |

---

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Creating a sense of superiority or bias based on background
- ❌ Letting one side dominate while ignoring the other
- ❌ Over-emphasizing technical difficulty and intimidating non-technical participants
- ❌ Overstating career promises from cross-disciplinary learning
- ❌ Neglecting the learning experience of participants from either background
