# CYBERPUNK Style Guide

## Applicable Scenarios
Conferences, hackathons, NFT launches, crypto art festivals, blockchain summits, metaverse events, tech subculture gatherings

## Color Palette
- **Primary Colors:** Neon Blue (#00F0FF), Electric Purple (#B026FF), Hot Pink (#FF006E)
- **Background:** Deep Black (#0A0A0A) or Deep Navy Blue (#0D1B2A)
- **Accent Colors:** Neon Cyan (#00FFF5), Acid Yellow (#FFFF00), Toxic Green (#39FF14)
- **Saturation:** Neon elements at maximum saturation to simulate a glowing effect
- **Color Behavior:** Luminous halos, chromatic aberration, light bleed effects

### Recommended Color Combinations
- Classic Cyberpunk: Neon Blue (#00F0FF) + Hot Pink (#FF006E) on Deep Black (#0A0A0A) background
- Blade Runner: Purple (#B026FF) + Orange (#FF6B35) with cyan accents
- The Matrix: Toxic Green (#39FF14) + Black, with digital rain effects

## Visual Elements
- Holographic UI panels and borders
- Glitch scanlines and digital artifacts
- Tech symbols and geometric decorative elements (no text characters)
- Circuit board patterns
- Wireframe grids and perspective lines
- Detailed futuristic skylines with multi-layered architecture
- Rain streaks and light reflections
- Hexagonal and angular tech shapes
- QR codes and data visualizations
- Neon signage and holographic advertisements
- Aircraft and drones

## Effect Processing
- Neon glow with outer bloom (5–10px radius)
- Edge chromatic aberration (RGB color separation)
- Motion blur along light trails
- Grain/noise overlay (15–25% opacity)
- Light bleed and lens flares
- Metallic gradient reflections
- Holographic iridescent shimmer
- Screen glitch distortion
- Atmospheric fog/haze with volumetric lighting

## Composition Strategy
- Vertical emphasis: towering cityscape occupying 70–80% of the frame
- Asymmetric composition with strong diagonal lines
- Layered depth: distant skyscrapers, mid-ground buildings, foreground structures
- Neon elements concentrated in the upper two-thirds of the frame
- Minimal street-level / ground presence (maximum 10–15% at the bottom)
- Perspective grid converging to a distant vanishing point
- Floating UI elements scattered throughout
- Rain or light trails creating vertical rhythm
- High-contrast zones: bright neon lights against deep shadows

## Typography Settings
- **Color Selection:** Use solid colors with high contrast against dark backgrounds
  - Recommended for deep black / navy backgrounds: Pure White (#FFFFFF), Neon Blue (#00F0FF), Hot Pink (#FF006E)
  - Alternative solid colors: Neon Cyan (#00FFF5), Electric Purple (#B026FF), Toxic Green (#39FF14)
- **Fill Method:** Solid color fill — single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited Effects:**
  - No neon glow / outer bloom
  - No chromatic aberration (RGB separation)
  - No glitch scanlines
  - No holographic iridescent shimmer
  - No light bleed effects
  - No outlines / strokes
  - No shadows / drop shadows
  - No text effects of any kind
- **General Principle:** Typography must remain clean, sharp, and highly legible — serving as a clear information anchor within the neon cityscape. Visual impact is achieved through typeface selection and high-contrast solid color alone, creating a deliberate contrast between "informational clarity" and "visual chaos."

## Mood & Atmosphere
Dystopian yet vibrant, tech-rebellious, underground innovation culture. The overall energy is intense, mysterious, and forward-looking — capturing the intersection of high-tech and low-life, with a noir-like atmospheric tension and digital optimism.
