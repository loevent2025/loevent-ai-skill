# AI Business Meeting – Education Event Knowledge Base

## 📌 Module Activation Criteria

Apply the rules in this module when the event theme involves the following keywords:
- Enterprise AI training workshops, internal training program meetings, AI talent strategy sessions
- University AI collaboration discussions, industry-academia partnership meetings, AI curriculum development sessions
- AI certification system workshops, training institution partnerships, AI education resource networking

---

## 🎯 Scenario Overview

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Format | Invitation-only workshop + proposal review + partnership discussion + resource networking |
| Content Depth | AI training system design, curriculum development, certification standards, talent strategy |
| Participants | Enterprise HR/training leaders, university representatives, AI education institutions |
| Core Objective | Establish training partnerships, deploy curriculum frameworks, build talent pipelines |

---

## 👥 Audience Profiles (by Priority)

| Priority | Audience Type | Key Concerns |
|----------|---------------|--------------|
| P0 | Enterprise Training Leaders | Team AI upskilling, internal training programs, ROI measurement |
| P1 | Universities/Research Institutions | AI curriculum partnerships, internship programs, industry-academia projects |
| P2 | AI Education & Training Institutions | Course resources, certification partnerships, enrollment channels |
| P3 | Enterprise HR | AI talent recruitment, employer branding, campus recruitment partnerships |

## 🔀 Style Selection Guide

| Event Type | Recommended Style | Core Focus |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| Enterprise AI Training Workshop | Formal & Professional (A) | Program evaluation, results validation |
| University AI Partnership Discussion | Formal & Professional (A) | Academic collaboration, resource exchange |
| AI Talent Strategy Session | Suspense-driven (C) | Recruitment pain points, solutions |
| AI Training Resource Networking | Benefits-oriented (D) | Partnership opportunities, resource access |

## ❓ FAQ Templates

|----------|--------|

## ⚠️ Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Turning the session into a sales pitch for training institutions
- ❌ Overstating AI training results or upskilling claims
- ❌ Disclosing internal training budgets of participating companies
- ❌ Publicly sharing the attendee company list
