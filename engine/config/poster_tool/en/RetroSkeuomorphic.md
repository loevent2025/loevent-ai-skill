# RETRO TECH Style Guide

## Applicable Scenarios
Nostalgic tech branding, retro design projects, technology history exhibitions, creative studio visuals, digital art events, minimalist branding, designer communities, tech museum posters

---

## Color Scheme

- **Primary tones:** Off-white, warm gray, vintage cream, soft ivory, pale yellow, light tan
- **Background:** Pure black, deep charcoal black
- **Saturation:** Low saturation, emphasizing retro texture
- **Color behavior:** Flat color blocks, no gradients, high contrast

### Common Color Combinations

| Style | Colors |
|---|---|
| **Classic Retro** | Pure Black (#000000) + Off-White (#F5F5DC) + Warm Gray (#D3D3D3) + Pale Yellow (#FFF8DC) |
| **Minimalist Nostalgia** | Deep Charcoal (#1A1A1A) + Cream (#FFFDD0) + Light Gray (#E8E8E8) + Light Tan (#D2B48C) |
| **Tech Memory** | Black (#000000) + Ivory (#FFFFF0) + Mid Gray (#BEBEBE) + Beige (#F5F5DC) |
| **Designer Edition** | Pure Black (#000000) + Paper White (#FAF9F6) + Silver Gray (#C0C0C0) + Almond (#FFEBCD) |

### Element Selection Principles
- **Single hero element:** 1 element occupies 60–80% of the frame as the absolute visual focal point
- **Hero + supporting element:** Hero at 50–65%, supporting element at 15–25% — clear visual hierarchy
- **Free combination:** Any two elements may be paired — not limited to conventional pairings (e.g., floppy disk + keyboard, mouse + monitor, etc.)

---

## Effects & Treatment

- **Faithful device reproduction:** Preserve accurate proportions, details, and structural characteristics of each device
- **Flat photography aesthetic:** Resembles a top-down product photography overhead shot — not an illustration or icon
- **Precise geometric rendering:** Retain authentic corners, seams, keys, slots, and other structural details
- **Solid color fill:** No gradients — flat single-color blocks — while preserving the device's internal structural zones
- **High contrast:** Strong contrast between black background and light-colored devices
- **No drop shadows:** Remove all shadow effects entirely
- **No lighting treatment:** No light sources or reflections depicted
- **Device-level detail:** Retain real-world details such as screw holes, vents, ports, and labels
- **Unified surface treatment:** All surfaces rendered as flat matte — no plastic sheen or gloss

---

## Composition Strategy

- **Single hero composition:** Centered and symmetrical, dominating the primary visual space
- **Hero + support composition:** Hero centered or slightly offset; supporting element to the side or corner, noticeably smaller than the hero
- **Top-down first-person view:** Strict vertical overhead perspective
- **Black background contrast:** Pure black background accentuates the featured device
- **Negative space for information:** Surrounding areas accommodate text content
- **Visual weight balance:** Mindful of compositional balance across element positions

---

## Typography Settings

- **Fill method:** Solid color fill, single color, no gradients
- **Prohibited effects:**
  - No outer/inner glow
  - No bloom effect
  - No stroke/outline
  - No drop shadow or projection shadow
  - No gradient fill
  - No edge lighting
  - No text special effects of any kind
- **Overall principle:** Keep text clean, sharp, and highly legible — use classic serif or sans-serif typefaces consistent with the retro flat design's minimalist aesthetic; establish a sense of nostalgic professionalism through typography and layout

---

## Mood & Atmosphere

Nostalgic warmth, timeless classicism, design purity, minimalist elegance. The overall tone is a tribute to the history of technology — humanistic, design-driven, and evocative — using iconic retro tech objects to summon collective memories of the golden age of personal computing.
